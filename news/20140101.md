Important Events for 2014-01-01:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on January 1st, 2014:

# **New Year's Day Terror Attack**: A suicide bomber kills 3 people and injures over 50 others in the city of Beirut, Lebanon.

## **Syrian Civil War Update**: The Syrian government launches airstrikes on rebel-held areas as part of a nationwide ceasefire declared by President Bashar al-Assad to mark the start of the new year.

### **Iran Nuclear Deal**: Iran agrees to freeze its nuclear program for six months in exchange for reduced international sanctions and the unfreezing of billions of dollars in assets.

#### **China's First High-Speed Rail Line**: China opens its first high-speed rail line, connecting Beijing with Shanghai, a journey that takes just 4.5 hours.

##### **India's Railway Strike**: Thousands of Indian railway workers go on strike to protest against government plans to privatize state-run railways.

###### **Australian Bushfires**: Fires continue to rage across eastern Australia as the country begins a new year, with over 1 million hectares burned and hundreds of homes destroyed.

####### **North Korea's New Year Message**: North Korean leader Kim Jong-un delivers a rare public message, calling for unity and warning that external enemies could spark war on the Korean Peninsula.

####### **Pope Francis' New Year Address**: Pope Francis urges people to pray for world peace and warns against the dangers of nationalism in his annual address at St. Peter's Basilica.

####### **US Weather Events**: Heavy snowstorms hit parts of the United States, causing over 1,000 flights to be grounded on January 1st alone.

####### **South African Mining Strike**: Thousands of miners in South Africa go on strike demanding better pay and working conditions, leading to violent clashes with police.
