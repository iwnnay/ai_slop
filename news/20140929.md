Important Events for 2014-09-29:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on September 29, 2014:

# Russia-Ukraine Conflict
The Ukrainian army launched an operation to retake the city of Donetsk from pro-Russian separatists.

# Ferguson Unrest
Protests and violence erupted in Ferguson, Missouri, as people demonstrated against the police shooting of Michael Brown, an unarmed black teenager.

# Ebola Outbreak
The World Health Organization (WHO) reported that a total of 4,024 people had been infected with Ebola in West Africa, with 2,097 deaths.

# North Korea Nuclear Test
North Korea conducted its fifth nuclear test, claiming it was a hydrogen bomb capable of being miniaturized for use on ballistic missiles.

# Ukraine Crisis
The Ukrainian government accused Russia of sending troops into eastern Ukraine to support the separatist rebels.

# Apple Event
Apple announced its new Apple Pay mobile payment system, which would allow users to make purchases with their iPhones and iPads.

# New York City Football Club
The team was officially named the New York City Football Club (NYCFC), a Major League Soccer franchise owned by Manchester City FC.

# Nobel Prize in Literature
French author Patrick Modiano won the Nobel Prize in Literature for his novels, which explored themes of identity, memory, and the search for meaning.

# Russia Sanctions
The European Union imposed additional sanctions on Russia over its actions in Ukraine, including travel bans and asset freezes.

# Hong Kong Protests
Hong Kong protesters clashed with police as they demanded greater autonomy from China and the withdrawal of a controversial extradition bill.
