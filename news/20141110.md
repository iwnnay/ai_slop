Important Events for 2014-11-10:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on November 10th, 2014:

# 1: Ferguson Protests Continue
Protests and demonstrations continue to erupt in Ferguson, Missouri, following the shooting death of Michael Brown by police officer Darren Wilson.

#2: Ebola Outbreak Update
The World Health Organization (WHO) reports that the Ebola outbreak has infected over 14,000 people and killed nearly 5,000 in West Africa.

#3: Israeli Elections Held
Israel holds national elections, with Benjamin Netanyahu's Likud party winning a majority of seats in the Knesset (Israeli parliament).

#4: Hong Kong Protests Intensify
Pro-democracy protests in Hong Kong reach their peak, with thousands of protesters occupying streets and government buildings.

#5: NFL Player Arrested for Domestic Violence
Ricardo Lockette, running back for the Seattle Seahawks, is arrested on suspicion of domestic violence after an incident involving his girlfriend.

#6: Russian Activist Detained
Russian activist Alexei Navalny is detained by police in Moscow while investigating corruption allegations against a top government official.

#7: Turkey's Erdogan Wins Presidential Vote
Turkish President Recep Tayyip Erdogan wins re-election, securing 51.4% of the vote in a highly contested election.

#8: NASA's Orion Spacecraft Launched
NASA launches its new Orion spacecraft on a test flight from Cape Canaveral, Florida, designed for deep space exploration missions.

#9: Hong Kong Protesters Vow to Continue Fight
Pro-democracy protesters in Hong Kong vow to continue their fight against the government's proposed electoral reforms, despite the city's Chief Executive Leung Chun-ying refusing to step down.

#10: American Music Awards Held
The 42nd Annual American Music Awards are held at the Nokia Theatre L.A. Live in Los Angeles, with Taylor Swift winning multiple awards including Artist of the Year.

These events highlight a range of significant news stories that took place on November 10th, 2014, covering global politics, health crises, sports, and cultural happenings.
