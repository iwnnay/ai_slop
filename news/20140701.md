# July 1st, 2014: Major Global Events
=====================================================

### News and Politics

* **Scotland Votes No to Independence**: The Scottish independence referendum saw Scots vote 55.3% to remain part of the United Kingdom, rejecting a proposal for Scotland to become an independent country.
* **Ukraine Crisis Escalates**: Pro-Russian separatists in eastern Ukraine seized government buildings and police stations, escalating tensions between Ukraine's government and Russian-backed rebels.

### Sports

* **Tour de France Stage 1**: The 101st edition of the Tour de France began with a flat stage from Leeds to Harrogate in England.
* **FIFA World Cup Qualifying**: Several countries including Brazil, Argentina, and Germany secured spots for the 2018 FIFA World Cup qualifiers.

### Culture and Arts

* **Comic-Con International: San Diego**: The annual comic book and pop culture convention drew thousands of fans to San Diego, featuring panels, screenings, and celebrity appearances.
* **New Music Releases**: Artists like Coldplay, Maroon 5, and Ed Sheeran released new music on July 1st.

### History

* **Nelson Mandela's Birthday Commemorated**: The former President of South Africa would have turned 96 years old if he were alive. Mandela passed away in December 2013.
* **World Population Day**: The United Nations marked the day with a focus on population growth, sustainability, and environmental concerns.

### Other Events

* **Google Chrome OS Update**: Google released an update for its Chrome operating system, adding new features and improvements to the browser-based OS.
* **Apple iOS 8 Beta Release**: Apple made available the first beta version of iOS 8, the next major update to its mobile operating system.

These are just a few of the significant events that took place on July 1st, 2014.
