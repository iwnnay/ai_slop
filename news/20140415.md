Important Events for 2014-04-15:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on April 15, 2014:

**April 15, 2014**
=====================

### News

1. **Boston Marathon Bombing Anniversary**: It was the one-year anniversary of the Boston Marathon bombing, which killed three people and injured hundreds when two bombs exploded near the finish line.
2. **Tax Day in the United States**: Tax day in the US, where citizens were required to file their annual tax returns with the Internal Revenue Service (IRS).
3. **NATO Summit Concludes**: The NATO summit concluded in Wales, UK, where leaders discussed Ukraine and Russia.

### Sports

4. **Boston Red Sox vs. Toronto Blue Jays**: The Boston Red Sox defeated the Toronto Blue Jays 7-1 at Fenway Park.
5. **NBA Playoffs**: Game 2 of the NBA Eastern Conference Quarterfinals saw the Washington Wizards defeat the Chicago Bulls 101-99.

### Cultural

6. **Avengers: Age of Ultron Trailer Released**: The trailer for Marvel's Avengers: Age of Ultron was released, featuring the return of Iron Man, Captain America, and other superheroes.
7. **New Zealand General Election**: New Zealand held a general election to elect members to its Parliament.

### Historical

8. **V-E Day (78th Anniversary)**: It was the 78th anniversary of V-E Day, marking the end of World War II in Europe.
9. **First Transcontinental Railroad Completed (154th Anniversary)**: The first transcontinental railroad was completed on this day in 1869, connecting the East Coast and West Coast of the United States.
10. **Earth Day**: April 15 was also Earth Day, a global event promoting environmental protection and sustainability.

Note: These events are not necessarily ranked in order of importance or significance.
