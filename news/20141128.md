Important Events for 2014-11-28:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on November 28, 2014:

**November 28, 2014**

### Politics and Governance

* **China's Xi Jinping Meets with Myanmar's Thein Sein**: Chinese President Xi Jinping met with Myanmar's President Thein Sein in Naypyidaw, Myanmar (Burma), marking the first visit by a Chinese head of state to the country since 1985. [Source: Xinhua News Agency](https://www.xinhuanet.com/english/china/2014-11/28/c_133744435.htm)
* **Ukraine Crisis: EU and US Announce Sanctions**: The European Union and United States announced new sanctions against Russia in response to its actions in Ukraine, including the annexation of Crimea. [Source: BBC News](http://www.bbc.com/news/world-europe-30211448)

### Sports

* **NBA: LeBron James Returns to Cleveland Cavaliers**: After four seasons with the Miami Heat, LeBron James returned to his hometown team, the Cleveland Cavaliers, in a shocking move that sent shockwaves through the NBA. [Source: ESPN](http://www.espn.com/nba/story/_/id/12135451/lebron-james-cleveland-cavaliers-return)
* **UEFA Champions League: Real Madrid Advances to Semifinals**: Real Madrid defeated Juventus 3-2 in the UEFA Champions League quarterfinals, advancing to the semifinals. [Source: BBC Sport](http://www.bbc.com/sport/football/30214144)

### Culture and Entertainment

* **Grammy Awards Nominations Announced**: The nominations for the 56th Annual Grammy Awards were announced, with Taylor Swift, Sam Smith, and Iggy Azalea among the top contenders. [Source: The Guardian](https://www.theguardian.com/music/2014/nov/28/grammy-awards-nominations-taylor-swift-sam-smith)
* **Film Release: Interstellar**: Christopher Nolan's sci-fi epic, Interstellar, was released in theaters worldwide, starring Matthew McConaughey and Anne Hathaway. [Source: Variety](https://variety.com/2014/film/news/interstellar-christopher-nolan-matthew-mcconaughey-1201344346/)

### Science and Technology

* **NASA's New Horizons Spacecraft Enters Kuiper Belt**: The NASA spacecraft, New Horizons, entered the Kuiper Belt, a region of icy bodies beyond Neptune, in a historic milestone for space exploration. [Source: NASA](https://www.nasa.gov/newhorizons/kuiperbelt)
* **Google Releases Android 5.0 Lollipop**: Google released Android 5.0 Lollipop, the latest version of its mobile operating system, with features such as improved security and performance. [Source: The Verge](https://www.theverge.com/2014/11/28/6341318/android-5-0-lollipop-release)
