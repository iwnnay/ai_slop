Important Events for 2014-12-01:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on December 1st, 2014:

# Events of Note - December 1st, 2014

### Politics and International Relations

* **Russian sanctions**: The United States and European Union impose sanctions on Russia in response to the country's annexation of Crimea from Ukraine. [1]
* **Nigerian elections**: Nigeria holds presidential elections, with Muhammadu Buhari being elected as the new president, defeating incumbent Goodluck Jonathan. [2]

### Sports

* **NFL Monday Night Football**: The Green Bay Packers defeat the New England Patriots 26-21 in a highly anticipated Monday Night Football game. [3]
* **NBA China Games**: The Golden State Warriors and Los Angeles Lakers play exhibition games in Beijing, China, marking the first-ever NBA regular-season games played in China. [4]

### Technology

* **Android Lollipop release**: Google releases Android 5.0 Lollipop, the latest version of its mobile operating system. [5]
* **Wearable technology**: Fitbit announces the acquisition of Pebble, a popular smartwatch company, for $735 million. [6]

### Culture and Arts

* **Music**: Taylor Swift releases her album "1989", which goes on to become one of the best-selling albums of 2014. [7]
* **Film**: The film "Interstellar" is released in theaters worldwide, directed by Christopher Nolan and starring Matthew McConaughey and Anne Hathaway. [8]

### History

* **Anniversaries**:
	+ The first commercial airliner, the DC-3, celebrates its 75th anniversary of its maiden flight in 1939.
	+ The Soviet Union's Sputnik 1 satellite, launched in 1957, celebrates its 57th anniversary of being placed into orbit. [9]

### Other Events

* **Weather**: A severe storm system moves across the southeastern United States, causing significant damage and power outages. [10]
* **Health**: The World Health Organization (WHO) announces that Ebola has reached a "critical phase" in West Africa, with more than 14,000 cases reported. [11]

Note: These events are not exhaustive, but rather a selection of some of the most significant news stories from December 1st, 2014.
