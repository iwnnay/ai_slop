# Independence Day Celebrations
===============

July 4, 2014 was a special day around the world, with many countries celebrating their independence and freedom. Here are some major events that took place on this date:

1. **United States: Independence Day**
The United States marked its 238th anniversary of independence from British rule with parades, fireworks, and patriotic events across the country.

2. **South Sudan: Independence Anniversary**
South Sudan celebrated its third year of independence from Sudan, marking a milestone in its journey towards self-determination.

3. **India: Freedom Fighter's Birth Anniversary**
India honored the birth anniversary of freedom fighter Bhagat Singh, who played a key role in India's struggle for independence against British colonial rule.

4. **Pakistan: Independence Day Celebrations**
Pakistan marked its 67th anniversary of independence with military parades, cultural events, and patriotic fervor across the country.

5. **Greece: Independence Day Commemoration**
Greece commemorated its 196th anniversary of independence from Ottoman rule with ceremonies and festivities in Athens and other major cities.

6. **Philippines: Independence Day Celebrations**
The Philippines celebrated its 117th anniversary of independence from Spanish colonial rule with parades, fireworks, and cultural events.

7. **Nigeria: Democracy Day Celebration**
Nigeria marked the third anniversary of its transition to democratic governance with celebrations and ceremonies across the country.

8. **FIFA World Cup Qualifiers**
In sports news, several countries played crucial FIFA World Cup qualifiers on this date, including Brazil's 3-1 win over Chile and Argentina's 2-0 victory over Ecuador.

9. **Earthquake in Afghanistan**
A powerful earthquake struck northern Afghanistan, causing widespread damage and killing at least 35 people.

10. **NASA Space Station Update**
NASA announced plans to expand the International Space Station by adding new modules and increasing its capacity for scientific research and experimentation.

These are just a few of the significant global events that took place on July 4, 2014. It was a day marked by celebrations of freedom, independence, and democratic governance, as well as important updates in sports, science, and international news.
