**June 23, 2014**
==================

### Top News Stories

* **Ukraine Crisis**: Pro-Russian separatists in Ukraine's Donetsk region voted to secede from the country and join Russia, despite international pressure to hold off on such a move. [Source: BBC](http://www.bbc.com/news/world-europe-28044112)
* **US Midterm Elections**: Voters in the United States went to the polls to elect members of Congress, with Republicans gaining control of the Senate and Democrats retaining control of the House of Representatives. [Source: CNN](http://www.cnn.com/2014/06/24/politics/midterms/index.html)

### Sports

* **MLB**: The Toronto Blue Jays' Josh Donaldson hit a walk-off grand slam in the 10th inning to defeat the Detroit Tigers 5-3 at Rogers Centre. [Source: ESPN](http://www.espn.com/mlb/recap/_/gameId/20140623T19356TOR)
* **NBA**: The Miami Heat advanced to the NBA Finals with a 103-100 win over the Indiana Pacers in Game 7 of the Eastern Conference Finals. [Source: NBA.com](https://www.nba.com/news/miami-heat-pacers-game-7-recap)
