Important Events for 2014-05-17:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on May 17th, 2014:

# 1: Ukraine's Ousted President Yanukovych Flees Country
Ukrainian opposition leader Oleksandr Turchynov took control of the government, while former President Viktor Yanukovych fled to Russia amidst the ongoing crisis in Eastern Ukraine.

The European Parliament passed a resolution condemning Hungary's anti-Roma laws, citing concerns over discrimination and human rights violations against the Romani minority.

Chinese Premier Li Keqiang visited Vietnam to strengthen bilateral ties amid tensions over territorial disputes in the South China Sea.

Pro-democracy activists in Hong Kong held massive protests against Beijing's plans to limit electoral reforms, sparking concerns over the city's autonomy and democratic future.

The US military launched airstrikes against Islamic State (ISIS) militants in Iraq, marking the beginning of a new phase in the international response to the group's expansion.

Emanuel Ungaro, a renowned French fashion designer known for his elegant and sophisticated designs, passed away at the age of 86.

# 9: US Comedian Joan Rivers Dies After Complications from Surgery

Russian President Vladimir Putin submitted a bill to the State Duma (legislative body) aimed at strengthening laws prohibiting "gay propaganda" among minors, sparking concerns over the country's growing anti-LGBTQ+ atmosphere.
