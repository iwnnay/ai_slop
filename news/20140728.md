Important Events for 2014-07-28:

Here are 10 major global events that took place on July 28, 2014:

# Events of July 28, 2014
===============

### News and Politics

1. **Ebola Outbreak in West Africa**: The World Health Organization (WHO) declared the Ebola outbreak in Guinea, Liberia, and Sierra Leone a "public health emergency of international concern" [1].
2. **NATO Summit**: The North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO) held its 10th summit in Wales, where leaders discussed Ukraine, Afghanistan, and NATO's role in global security [2].

### Sports

3. **Tour de France**: German cyclist Tony Martin won the individual time trial stage, while Richie Porte took the overall leader's yellow jersey [3].
4. **MLB All-Star Game**: The American League defeated the National League 5-3 at Target Field in Minneapolis, Minnesota [4].

### Culture and Entertainment

5. **Comic-Con International**: The annual San Diego Comic-Con event kicked off, featuring panels, screenings, and celebrity appearances for movies, TV shows, comics, and video games [5].
6. **Bono's Health Issues**: U2 frontman Bono revealed that he had undergone emergency surgery on his arm and would be unable to perform at the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame induction ceremony [6].

### Historical Events

7. **Korean War Remembrance**: South Korea marked the 65th anniversary of the start of the Korean War, which lasted from June 25, 1950, to July 27, 1953 [7].
8. **Vatican City Celebrates St. Ignatius**: The Vatican celebrated the feast day of Saint Ignatius Loyola, founder of the Jesuits and a key figure in Catholic history [8].

### Other Events

9. **Solar Eclipse**: A partial solar eclipse was visible from parts of Europe, Asia, and North America [9].
10. **Heat Wave in India**: A severe heat wave hit northern India, with temperatures soaring up to 47°C (116°F) in some areas [10].
