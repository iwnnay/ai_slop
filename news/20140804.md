A protest over the killing of 18-year-old Michael Brown by a Ferguson, Missouri police officer turns violent, with looting and property damage reported. The incident sparks widespread outrage and calls for justice.

The European Union imposes new sanctions against Russia over its actions in Ukraine, including restrictions on Russian state-owned banks and energy companies. The move aims to pressure Russia into withdrawing its support for separatist groups.

The Shanghai Stock Exchange Composite Index plummets by over 5% as concerns about China's slowing economy and potential government intervention spark a market rout.

NASA's Lunar Atmosphere and Dust Environment Explorer (LADEE) spacecraft crashes into the moon's surface after completing its mission to study the lunar atmosphere. The crash marks the end of the six-month mission.
