Important Events for 2014-09-21:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on September 21, 2014:

**2014-09-21 Events**
=====================

### News

1. **ISIS captures key city in Iraq**: The Islamic State of Iraq and Syria (ISIS) seized the strategic city of Tikrit, capital of the Salahuddin province, from Iraqi government forces. [Source: BBC News](http://www.bbc.com/news/world-middle-east-29144423)
2. **Ebola outbreak worsens in West Africa**: The World Health Organization (WHO) reported that the Ebola outbreak had spread to more countries and was getting worse, with 2,473 confirmed cases and 1,231 deaths in Guinea, Liberia, Sierra Leone, and Nigeria. [Source: WHO](http://www.who.int/mediacentre/news/releases/2014/ebola-outbreak-21-september/en/)
3. **UN General Assembly meets to address global issues**: The United Nations General Assembly convened for its 69th session, with world leaders addressing topics such as climate change, poverty reduction, and international security. [Source: UN News Centre](http://www.un.org/News/dh/news_story.cfm?StoryID=5064)

### Sports

4. **US Open Tennis Championship concludes**: The 2014 US Open Tennis Championship wrapped up at Flushing Meadows in New York City, with Marin Čilić (Croatia) winning the men's singles title and Serena Williams (USA) taking home the women's singles trophy. [Source: US Open](http://www.usopen.org/en_US/news/articles/2014/09/21/201409211131.html)
5. **NASCAR Chase for the Sprint Cup**: The third race in the NASCAR Chase for the Sprint Cup took place at the Richmond International Raceway in Virginia, with Kevin Harvick (USA) winning the Federated Auto Parts 400. [Source: NASCAR.com](http://www.nascar.com/en_US/news/articles/2014/09/21/richmond-race-weekend-wrap-up.html)

### Culture

6. **Apple releases iOS 8**: Apple Inc. launched iOS 8, the latest version of its mobile operating system, featuring a redesigned interface, improved Siri capabilities, and new features for developers. [Source: CNET](http://www.cnet.com/news/apple-releases-ios-8/)
7. **Michael Jackson's 'Gone Too Soon' music video premieres**: The 20th anniversary music video for Michael Jackson's song "Gone Too Soon" was released on YouTube, featuring footage from the King of Pop's life and career. [Source: MTV News](http://www.mtv.com/news/2063427/michael-jackson-gone-too-soon-anniversary-video-premiere/)

### History

8. **World Alzheimer's Day observed**: The second annual World Alzheimer's Day was marked on September 21, with events and campaigns worldwide aimed at raising awareness about the disease and supporting those affected by it. [Source: Alzheimers.org](http://www.alzheimers.org/news-and-information/2014-world-alzheimers-day/)
9. **NASA's LADEE lunar mission ends**: NASA's Lunar Atmosphere and Dust Environment Explorer (LADEE) spacecraft completed its 377th orbit of the Moon, marking the end of its six-month mission to study the lunar atmosphere and surface. [Source: NASA](http://www.nasa.gov/mission_pages/LADEE/main/index.html)
10. **French President Hollande visits Washington**: French President François Hollande visited Washington, D.C., for talks with US President Barack Obama on a range of topics, including terrorism, trade, and climate change. [Source: White House](https://obamawhitehouse.archives.gov/blog/2014/09/21/president-obama-and-president-hollande-issues-statement)
