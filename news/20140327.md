Important Events for 2014-03-27:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on March 27, 2014:

**March 27, 2014**

# Ukraine Crisis
Ukraine's Prime Minister Arseniy Yatsenyuk accuses Russia of waging war against his country, as pro-Russian protesters occupy government buildings in eastern Ukraine.

# Malaysia Airlines Flight 370
The Malaysian government confirms that the search for missing flight MH370 will continue until at least August, after the wreckage is yet to be found.

# Hong Kong Protests
Thousands of protesters gather outside the Hong Kong government's headquarters, demanding greater democracy and accountability from the city's leaders.

# Ebola Outbreak
The World Health Organization (WHO) confirms 157 deaths and 258 cases of Ebola in Guinea, Liberia, and Sierra Leone, sparking international concern over the spread of the deadly virus.

# NASA Spacecraft
NASA's Kepler space telescope has run out of fuel, but its data continues to help scientists understand the properties of distant stars and planets.

# European Union Elections
The European Parliament holds elections for 751 seats, with voter turnout reaching a record low in many member states.

# World Cup Qualifying
Brazil defeats Chile 2-1 in their World Cup qualifying match, while Argentina wins 3-2 against Uruguay.

# Music Industry
Rock legend Bruce Springsteen announces his upcoming album "High Hopes", which will feature songs about love, loss, and social justice issues.

# Nobel Prize Winner Dies
Physicist and Nobel laureate Vitaly Ginzburg dies at the age of 92 in Moscow, Russia.
