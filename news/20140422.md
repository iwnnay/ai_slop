Important Events for 2014-04-22:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on April 22nd, 2014:

# Earth Day Celebrations
April 22nd marked the 44th anniversary of Earth Day, which was first observed in 1970. The day is celebrated to raise awareness about environmental protection and conservation.

# Malaysia Airlines Flight 370 Investigation Update
The search for missing Malaysia Airlines Flight 370 continued on April 22nd, with investigators analyzing data from a black box flight recorder recovered earlier that month. The flight disappeared on March 8th, 2014, with 239 people on board.

# Ukrainian Crisis Escalation
Tensions between pro-Russian separatists and Ukrainian forces continued to escalate in eastern Ukraine, with reports of gunfire and clashes between the two sides. This marked a turning point in the crisis, which began in November 2013.

# Boston Marathon Bombing Suspects Sentenced
Dzhokhar Tsarnaev, one half of the pair responsible for the Boston Marathon bombing on April 15th, 2013, was sentenced to death by a federal jury. His brother Tamerlan had been killed during a shootout with police in April 2013.

# FIFA World Cup Draw Held
The draw for the 2014 FIFA World Cup took place in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. The event saw teams grouped into 8 pools of 4 countries each, with the top two teams from each pool advancing to the knockout stage.

# Google Acquires Skybox Imaging
Google announced it was acquiring Skybox Imaging, a satellite imaging company, for $500 million. This acquisition expanded Google's capabilities in remote sensing and mapping technology.

# European Parliament Election Results Announced
The results of the 2014 European Parliament election were announced, with voters in the European Union electing representatives to the parliament. The election saw several parties gain or lose seats compared to previous elections.

# Nobel Prize Winners Honored
Nobel laureates from around the world gathered in Stockholm, Sweden, to celebrate the anniversary of Alfred Nobel's passing on December 10th, 1896. This year marked the 118th annual Nobel Week.

# Russian Military Intervention in Ukraine Begins
Russian forces began deploying troops into eastern Ukraine to quell anti-government protests and separatist movements. This marked a significant escalation in the conflict between Ukraine and Russia.

# MLB's Toronto Blue Jays Acquire Josh Thole from New York Mets

The Toronto Blue Jays acquired catcher Josh Thole from the New York Mets in exchange for minor league pitcher Sean Henn. This move added depth to the Blue Jays' lineup behind the plate.
