# Events of May 11, 2014
===============



### News

* **Ukrainian Crisis**: Pro-Russian separatists in Ukraine's Donetsk region declare independence from Kiev, following a referendum with a reported 90% turnout. (Source: BBC)
* **Ebola Outbreak**: The World Health Organization (WHO) declares the Ebola outbreak in West Africa to be a "public health emergency of international concern." (Source: WHO)

### Sports

* **NFL Draft**: The 2014 NFL Draft takes place, with quarterback Blake Bortles selected by the Jacksonville Jaguars as the third overall pick. (Source: ESPN)
* **NBA Playoffs**: The Indiana Pacers defeat the Washington Wizards 114-100 in Game 1 of their NBA playoff series. (Source: ESPN)

### Culture

* **Music Release**: American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift releases her fifth studio album, "1989," which goes on to become one of the best-selling albums of all time. (Source: Rolling Stone)
* **Film Release**: The animated film "The Grand Budapest Hotel" is released in theaters, directed by Wes Anderson and starring Ralph Fiennes. (Source: IMDb)

### Historical

* **Anniversaries**:
	+ The 67th anniversary of the end of World War II is marked worldwide.
	+ The 65th anniversary of the first nuclear test at Bikini Atoll is observed.

### Business

* **Economy**: The European Central Bank (ECB) announces a series of measures to boost the eurozone's economy, including cutting interest rates and introducing a new stimulus program. (Source: Reuters)
* **Technology**: Apple announces that it will open its first retail store in China, marking a significant expansion into the country's growing tech market. (Source: The Verge)

Note: These events are listed in no particular order of importance or significance.
