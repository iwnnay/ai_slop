Important Events for 2014-04-29:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on April 29, 2014:

**April 29, 2014**
=====================================================

### News and Politics

1. **Ukraine Crisis**: Pro-Russian separatists seize government buildings in eastern Ukraine, escalating the crisis in the country.
2. **NATO Meeting**: NATO foreign ministers meet in Brussels to discuss the crisis in Ukraine and the threat from Russia.

### Sports

3. **NFL Draft**: The 2014 NFL Draft takes place at Radio City Music Hall in New York City, with the Houston Texans selecting Jadeveon Clowney as the first overall pick.
4. **Boston Marathon Bombing Trial**: The trial of Dzhokhar Tsarnaev, one of the Boston Marathon bombers, begins.

### Culture and Entertainment

5. **Box Office Success**: The movie "Captain America: The Winter Soldier" opens at number one at the box office in the United States.
6. **Music Releases**: Singer-songwriter Ed Sheeran releases his second studio album, "x", also known as "Multiply".

### History and Science

7. **Voyager 1**: NASA's Voyager 1 spacecraft becomes the first human-made object to leave our solar system, entering interstellar space.
8. **SpaceX Reusable Rocket**: SpaceX successfully lands its Grasshopper reusable rocket back on Earth after launching it into space.

### Other Significant Events

9. **Earthquake in Chile**: A magnitude 6.7 earthquake strikes central Chile, causing widespread damage and injuries.
10. **Tornado Outbreak**: A series of tornadoes hits the southern United States, particularly in Arkansas and Mississippi, killing two people and injuring many others.

These are just a few of the significant events that occurred on April 29, 2014.
