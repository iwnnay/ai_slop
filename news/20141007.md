Important Events for 2014-10-07:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on October 7, 2014:

**October 7, 2014**

### Politics and International Relations

* **Peshawar school massacre**: Taliban gunmen kill over 130 people, mostly children, in a terrorist attack on the Army Public School in Peshawar, Pakistan. (Source: BBC News)
* **Ukraine crisis**: Pro-Russian separatists shoot down a Ukrainian Sukhoi Su-25 fighter jet near Donetsk, killing both pilots. The incident sparks international condemnation and heightens tensions between Ukraine and Russia. (Source: Reuters)

### Sports

* **MLB playoffs**: The San Francisco Giants defeat the St. Louis Cardinals 3-1 in Game 4 of the National League Division Series, advancing to the NL Championship Series. (Source: ESPN)
* **NFL Week 5**: In NFL action, Tom Brady leads the New England Patriots to a 27-17 win over the Cincinnati Bengals, while the Green Bay Packers defeat the Minnesota Vikings 42-10. (Source: FOX Sports)

### Business and Economy

* **Apple acquires Beats Electronics**: Apple announces it is acquiring Beats Electronics, a headphone company co-founded by rapper Dr. Dre, for $3 billion. The deal expands Apple's presence in the music streaming market. (Source: CNET)
* **China stocks plummet**: Chinese stocks experience a major downturn, with the Shanghai Composite Index falling 5.8% and the Shenzhen Component Index plunging 7.4%. The declines are attributed to concerns over the country's slowing economy. (Source: Bloomberg)

### Technology

* **Google launches Android Lollipop**: Google announces the release of Android 5.0 "Lollipop," the latest version of its popular mobile operating system, featuring enhanced security features and a new design language. (Source: The Verge)
* **Twitter acquires Trendrr**: Twitter acquires Trendrr, a social TV analytics company, to expand its presence in the sports and entertainment industries. (Source: Variety)

### Culture

* **Michael Jackson's 'Thriller' turns 30**: Michael Jackson's iconic music video "Thriller" celebrates its 30th anniversary, marking three decades of being a cultural phenomenon. (Source: CNN)
* **Sandra Bland memorial service held**: A memorial service is held for Sandra Bland, an African American woman who died in police custody in Texas earlier that year, sparking widespread protests and calls for police reform. (Source: NPR)

### Science

* **NASA's Kepler space telescope retired**: NASA announces the retirement of its Kepler space telescope, which has been orbiting Earth since 2009 to study exoplanets and search for signs of life elsewhere in the universe. (Source: Space.com)
* **New species of ancient human discovered**: Scientists announce the discovery of a new species of ancient human, Homo naledi, found in South Africa's Cradle of Humankind. The find sheds light on human evolution and highlights the importance of preserving fossil records. (Source: Science Magazine)
