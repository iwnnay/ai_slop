On January 17, 2014, several notable events took place globally. Here are 3-5 major events that occurred on this date:

1. **Malaysia Airlines Flight MH370 disappearance**: On January 17, 2014, Malaysia Airlines Flight MH370 disappeared en route from Kuala Lumpur to Beijing, carrying 239 people. The flight was reported missing after it lost contact with air traffic control, and despite extensive search efforts, no wreckage or bodies were found until March 8, 2015, when a piece of debris was discovered in the Indian Ocean. The cause of the disappearance remains unknown.
2. **South Korea ferry sinking**: On January 17, 2014, the Sewol ferry capsized off the coast of South Korea, resulting in one of the country's deadliest maritime disasters. The ferry, carrying 476 people, mostly high school students on a field trip, was traveling from Incheon to Jeju Island when it lost balance and eventually sank. The incident led to widespread outrage and calls for greater safety measures.
