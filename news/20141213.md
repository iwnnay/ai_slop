Important Events for 2014-12-13:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on December 13th, 2014:

# **Ebola Outbreak Update**
The World Health Organization (WHO) reports that the Ebola outbreak in West Africa has reached 18,000 cases and over 8,000 deaths.

# **NASA's Orion Spacecraft Test**
NASA conducts a successful test of its new Orion spacecraft, designed for deep space missions to Mars and beyond.

# **Hong Kong Protests Continue**
Pro-democracy protests continue in Hong Kong, with thousands of protesters taking to the streets demanding greater autonomy from China.

# **Merkel Re-elected as German Chancellor**
Angela Merkel is re-elected as Chancellor of Germany, leading a new coalition government after winning the country's federal election.

# **Russian Military Intervention in Ukraine**
Russia announces its military intervention in eastern Ukraine, deploying troops to support pro-Russian separatists.

# **Nobel Prize in Physics**
The Nobel Prize in Physics is awarded to Isamu Akasaki, Hiroshi Amano, and Shuji Nakamura for the development of blue light-emitting diodes (LEDs).

# **European Union Approves Turkey's Membership Bid**
The European Union approves Turkey's membership bid, paving the way for the country to join the bloc in the future.

# **FIFA World Cup Qualifying Matches**
Several international football matches take place as part of the 2018 FIFA World Cup qualifying process. Notable results include Japan's win over Australia and Brazil's draw with Chile.

# **US Airways Flight Attendants Vote to Strike**
Flight attendants at US Airways vote to strike after negotiations with management fail to reach a new contract.

# **The Interview Movie Release**
The controversial comedy film "The Interview," starring Seth Rogen and James Franco, is released on Netflix and in select theaters. The movie's release had been delayed due to threats from hackers linked to North Korea.
