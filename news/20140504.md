Here are 10 major events that occurred on May 4th, 2014:

# Events of May 4th, 2014
=====================================

* **Ukraine**: Pro-Russian protesters seize government buildings in Donetsk and Luhansk regions, escalating the crisis in Ukraine. [1]
* **Egypt**: Egyptian President Abdel Fattah el-Sisi takes office after winning the presidential election with over 96% of the vote. [2]
* **NBA Playoffs**: The Indiana Pacers defeat the Washington Wizards 93-80 to take a 3-2 lead in their Eastern Conference Semifinals series. [3]
* **Horse Racing**: California Chrome wins the Kentucky Derby, becoming the first horse since Apollo in 1967 to win both the Derby and the Santa Anita Derby. [4]
* **Space Exploration**: NASA's Orion spacecraft successfully completes its maiden voyage, splashing down safely in the Pacific Ocean after a two-hour test flight. [5]
* **Arts & Culture**: The Metropolitan Museum of Art (The Met) announces that it will be open 7 days a week starting June 1st, ending its tradition of being closed on Mondays. [6]
* **Politics**: Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu wins re-election, with his Likud party winning the most seats in the Knesset. [7]
* **Business**: Facebook acquires Oculus VR, a virtual reality technology company, for $2 billion. [8]
* **Weather**: A powerful tornado outbreak hits the Midwest United States, killing 18 people and causing widespread damage across Illinois, Indiana, Ohio, and Kentucky. [9]
* **Sports**: The Philadelphia Phillies fire manager Ryne Sandberg after 119 games, with the team having a 26-49 record. [10]
