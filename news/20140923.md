Important Events for 2014-09-23:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on September 23, 2014:

**Global Events**
==================

### 1. **Ebola Outbreak Update**: The World Health Organization (WHO) reported that the Ebola outbreak in West Africa had reached 6,574 cases and 3,091 deaths.

### 2. **NATO Summit**: The NATO summit was held in Wales, where leaders discussed Ukraine's crisis and Russia's involvement.

### 3. **Ukraine Crisis**: Pro-Russian separatists in eastern Ukraine announced a ceasefire, while Ukrainian forces continued to battle against the rebels.

### 4. **ISIS Attacks**: ISIS militants attacked a police station in the Iraqi city of Kirkuk, killing at least 25 people.

### 5. **Ebola Vaccine Trials**: The WHO launched trials for an experimental Ebola vaccine in Guinea, aiming to test its effectiveness and safety.

### 6. **Hurricane Odile**: The storm made landfall in Mexico's Baja California Peninsula, causing widespread damage and power outages.

### 7. **FIFA World Cup Qualifying**: In the FIFA World Cup qualifying tournament, several teams won crucial matches, including Brazil, Argentina, and Spain.

### 8. **India Floods**: Heavy rainfall caused devastating floods in parts of India, affecting over a million people and killing at least 12.

### 9. **Nobel Prize Nominees**: The Nobel Committee announced the nominees for the 2014 Nobel Prizes, including those in Physics, Chemistry, Medicine, Literature, Peace, and Economics.

### 10. **SpaceX Launch**: SpaceX launched its Falcon 9 rocket from Vandenberg Air Force Base in California, successfully placing a satellite into orbit.

These events give you an idea of some of the significant global happenings on September 23, 2014.
