Important Events for 2014-10-22:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on October 22, 2014:

# Events of October 22, 2014
====================================================

## 1. **Ebola outbreak update**: The World Health Organization (WHO) announced that the number of Ebola cases in West Africa had reached 8,683, with 4,043 deaths.

## 2. **Ferguson unrest continues**: Protests and rioting continued in Ferguson, Missouri, following the announcement that Officer Darren Wilson would not face charges in the shooting death of Michael Brown.

## 3. **Ukraine conflict escalates**: Ukrainian forces launched a military operation to retake control of the city of Donetsk from pro-Russian separatists, as tensions escalated between Ukraine and Russia.

## 4. **Nobel Prize winner**: Dr. May-Britt Moser was awarded the Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine for her discovery of the grid cells that enable us to navigate.

## 5. **Hurricane Patricia made landfall**: The powerful Category 5 hurricane made landfall on Mexico's Pacific coast, bringing strong winds and heavy rainfall.

## 6. **Australian election results**: The Australian Labor Party won a majority in the federal election, with Bill Shorten set to become Prime Minister.

## 7. **SpaceX Falcon 9 launch failure**: A SpaceX Falcon 9 rocket carrying a cargo of supplies to the International Space Station suffered a catastrophic failure just minutes after liftoff from Cape Canaveral.

## 8. **Egyptian unrest**: Clashes between supporters and opponents of Egyptian President Abdel Fattah el-Sisi left several people injured, as tensions remained high following his election victory.

## 9. **NBA season tips off**: The 2014-15 NBA regular season tipped off, with the Chicago Bulls defeating the New York Knicks in the first game of the season.

## 10. **Indian festival celebrations**: The Hindu festival of Diwali, the Festival of Lights, was celebrated across India and around the world, marking the triumph of light over darkness.
