Important Events for 2014-12-25:

Here are 10 significant events that occurred on December 25, 2014:

**December 25, 2014**

# Christmas Celebrations Around the World
The world came together to celebrate the holiday season with family and friends. People from diverse cultures and backgrounds shared in the joy of giving, sharing, and spending quality time with loved ones.

# Ferguson Grand Jury Decision Announced
A grand jury in Ferguson, Missouri declined to indict police officer Darren Wilson in the fatal shooting of Michael Brown, sparking renewed protests and calls for reform in the US criminal justice system.

# NASA's Orion Spacecraft Completes First Flight Test
NASA successfully tested its new Orion spacecraft, marking a major milestone in the development of a new heavy-lift rocket capable of taking humans to deep space.

# Apple Pay Launches in the UK
Apple launched its mobile payments service, Apple Pay, in the United Kingdom, allowing customers to make purchases with their iPhones and Apple Watches.

# Sony's PlayStation TV Hits Stores
Sony released the PlayStation TV, a compact gaming console that allows users to play PlayStation Vita games on their TVs.

# 'The Interview' Releases Online Amid Controversy
Comedy film "The Interview," starring Seth Rogen and James Franco, was made available online after facing backlash over its portrayal of North Korean dictator Kim Jong-un.

# 2014 NFL Season Comes to a Close
The National Football League (NFL) wrapped up the 2014 regular season, with teams preparing for the playoffs and eventual Super Bowl XLIX in February.

# 'Unbroken' Opens on Big Screen
War drama film "Unbroken," directed by Angelina Jolie and based on the life of Olympic athlete and prisoner-of-war Louis Zamperini, premiered worldwide.

# 'The Hobbit: The Battle of the Five Armies' Hits Cinemas
Fantasy adventure film "The Hobbit: The Battle of the Five Armies" was released in theaters, concluding the trilogy based on J.R.R. Tolkien's classic novel.
