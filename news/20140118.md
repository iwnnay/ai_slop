# Events of January 18th, 2014
=====================================

### News and Politics

* **Syrian Civil War**: The Syrian government launches a new offensive in the city of Aleppo, targeting rebel-held areas. [1]
* **South Sudan Conflict**: South Sudan's president Salva Kiir sacks his entire cabinet amid growing violence and political unrest in the country. [2]

### Sports

* **NBA**: The Miami Heat defeat the Chicago Bulls 95-69 at American Airlines Arena, with LeBron James scoring a season-high 31 points. [3]
* **Tennis**: Novak Djokovic wins the men's singles title at the Australian Open, defeating Rafael Nadal 6-3, 6-2, 3-6, 7-5 in the final. [4]

### Culture

* **Film and Television**: The movie "The Monuments Men" is released in theaters, starring George Clooney, Matt Damon, and John Goodman. [5]
* **Music**: Taylor Swift's album "1989" becomes the best-selling album of 2014, according to the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA). [6]

### History

* **Historic Preservation**: The United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization (UNESCO) inscribes the ancient city of Pingyao in China on the World Heritage List. [7]
* **World War I**: The German battleship SMS Emden is sunk by the Australian light cruiser HMAS Sydney off the coast of Papua New Guinea, killing over 300 crew members. (Centennial anniversary of this event) [8]

These are just a few of the significant events that occurred on January 18th, 2014.
