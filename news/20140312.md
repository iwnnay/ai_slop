Important Events for 2014-03-12:

Here are 10 significant global events that took place on March 12, 2014:

# The Day's Major Events
=====================================================

### Ukraine Crisis: Pro-Russian Protesters Storm Regional Administration Building
March 12, 2014 - Pro-Russian protesters stormed the regional administration building in Donetsk, Ukraine, as tensions continued to escalate amidst a pro-Western government takeover.

### Malaysia Airlines Flight MH370 Search Continues
March 12, 2014 - The search for missing Malaysian Airlines flight MH370 entered its 44th day, with the focus shifting from the South China Sea to the Indian Ocean.

### Crimea Votes Overwhelmingly in Favor of Separation from Ukraine
March 12, 2014 - In a widely criticized referendum, Crimea's residents voted overwhelmingly (96.5%) to secede from Ukraine and join Russia.

### NBA: Los Angeles Lakers Acquire Carlos Boozer
March 12, 2014 - The Los Angeles Lakers traded cash considerations for power forward Carlos Boozer in an effort to bolster their frontcourt.

### MLB: St. Louis Cardinals Sign Peter Bourjos
March 12, 2014 - The St. Louis Cardinals signed free agent outfielder Peter Bourjos to a one-year deal, providing depth and competition in the outfield.

### Google Acquires Waze for $1.2 Billion
March 12, 2014 - Technology giant Google acquired Israeli GPS navigation startup Waze for $1.2 billion, expanding its presence in the mobile mapping space.

### Russia Warns of "Full-Scale War" If Crimea Annexed by Ukraine
March 12, 2014 - Russian Foreign Minister Sergei Lavrov warned that a full-scale war would erupt if Ukraine attempts to annex Crimea after a disputed referendum.

### US Senators Introduce Resolution Condemning Russia's Actions in Ukraine
March 12, 2014 - Two US senators introduced a resolution condemning Russia's actions in Ukraine and calling for the country to respect Ukrainian sovereignty.

### Japan's PM Abe Visits Pearl Harbor Memorial
March 12, 2014 - Japanese Prime Minister Shinzo Abe became the first sitting Japanese leader to visit the USS Arizona Memorial at Pearl Harbor, Hawaii, marking a significant step towards reconciliation with the US.

### The Internet Hall of Fame Inducts 32 New Members
March 12, 2014 - The Internet Hall of Fame inducted 32 new members, recognizing their contributions to the development and growth of the internet.
