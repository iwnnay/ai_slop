# Events of December 8, 2014
===============

### News

* **Syria Crisis**: The Syrian government accused the US and other Western countries of supporting terrorist groups in the country, as tensions escalated over reports of a chlorine gas attack in Aleppo.
* **Ebola Outbreak**: Liberia declared itself Ebola-free after 42 days without any new cases, marking a significant milestone in the fight against the virus.

### Sports

* **NBA**: The Golden State Warriors defeated the Chicago Bulls 102-86 to improve their record to 18-2, the best start in franchise history.
* **NFL**: The Seattle Seahawks routed the Philadelphia Eagles 34-14 to take control of the NFC West division title.

### Culture

* **Film**: The 87th Academy Awards nominations were announced, with "American Sniper" and "The Grand Budapest Hotel" leading the pack with six nods each.
* **Music**: Taylor Swift's album "1989" was certified quadruple platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA).

### Historical

* **WWII**: The Soviet Union liberated the Auschwitz concentration camp, one of the largest and most notorious Nazi camps during World War II.
