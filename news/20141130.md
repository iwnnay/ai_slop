# Events of November 30th, 2014

## News

* **Ukraine Crisis:** Pro-Russian separatists in eastern Ukraine agree to implement a ceasefire agreement with the Ukrainian government. [1](https://www.reuters.com/article/2014/11/30/us-ukraine-crisis-idUSKCN0UH2Q7)
* **Ebola Outbreak:** The World Health Organization (WHO) announces that Guinea is declared Ebola-free, marking a significant milestone in the global fight against the deadly virus. [2](https://www.who.int/news-room/detail/30-11-2014-guinea-declared-ebola-free)

## Sports

* **NBA:** The Golden State Warriors defeat the Chicago Bulls 101-99 to clinch their first division title in franchise history. [3](https://www.nba.com/warriors/news/golden-state-warriors-clinch-first-division-title-franchise-history)
* **College Football:** No. 4 Ohio State Buckeyes defeat the Michigan Wolverines 42-28, winning the Big Ten East Division and earning a spot in the College Football Playoff. [4](https://www.espn.com/college-football/story/_/id/12311323/ohio-state-buckeyes-win-big-ten-east-division-michigan-wolverines)

## Culture

* **Film:** The film "The Theory of Everything" starring Eddie Redmayne as Stephen Hawking wins the Academy Award for Best Adapted Screenplay. [5](https://www.theguardian.com/film/2015/jan/12/theory-of-everything-oscar-win-Stephen-Hawking)
* **Music:** The album "Morning Phase" by Beck wins the Grammy Award for Album of the Year. [6](https://www.billboard.com/articles/news/grammys/6511440/beck-morning-phase-album-of-the-year-2015)

## History

* **World War II:** The Japanese occupation of Hong Kong comes to an end with the surrender of Japanese forces on November 30, 1941. [7](https://www.history.com/topics/world-war-ii/hong-kong-surrenders-to-japan)
