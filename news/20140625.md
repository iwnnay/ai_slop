Important Events for 2014-06-25:

Here are the top 10 global events that occurred on June 25, 2014:

# 1: **Ukrainian Crisis**: Pro-Russian separatists seize government buildings in Donetsk and Luhansk, Ukraine, sparking concerns of civil war.

## 2: **World Cup**: Colombia defeats Uruguay 2-0 in a World Cup Round of 16 match at the Maracanã Stadium in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.

# 3: **NASA's LADEE Mission**: NASA's Lunar Atmosphere and Dust Environment Explorer (LADEE) space probe crashes into the Moon after completing its mission to study the lunar atmosphere.

## 4: **Egyptian Politics**: Egypt's military-backed government announces a new cabinet, with several key posts filled by military officers and politicians close to President Abdel Fattah el-Sisi.

# 5: **European Economy**: The European Union agrees to provide €10 billion (approximately $13.6 billion) in loans to Ukraine to help stabilize its economy.

## 6: **Hollywood Premiere**: The movie "Earth to Echo" premieres at the Dolby Theatre in Los Angeles, California.

# 7: **Indian Aviation**: India's Civil Aviation Ministry announces plans to privatize Air India, the country's national carrier, amid concerns about its financial health and competition from low-cost airlines.

## 8: **French Politics**: French Prime Minister Manuel Valls reshuffles his cabinet, replacing several ministers in response to criticism over his government's handling of economic and social issues.

# 9: **Japanese Economy**: Japan's economy grows at an annualized rate of 4.5% in the first quarter, driven by strong consumer spending and a recovery in exports.

## 10: **Environmental Disaster**: The Chinese government announces plans to pump water into the Yangtze River to rescue thousands of fish stranded in a polluted lake due to a toxic algae bloom.
