Important Events for 2014-08-16:

Here are 10 major global events that took place on August 16, 2014:

# Ukraine-Russia Crisis
On August 16, 2014, Ukrainian forces recaptured the city of Donetsk from pro-Russian separatists in eastern Ukraine.

# Gaza-Israel Conflict
A ceasefire was reached between Israel and Hamas militants in Gaza after a four-day conflict that left over 150 Palestinians and 7 Israelis dead.

# Ebola Outbreak
The World Health Organization (WHO) reported that there were 1,291 confirmed cases of the Ebola virus in West Africa, with 729 deaths.

# Malaysian Airlines Flight MH17 Downing
An investigation by the Dutch Safety Board concluded that the Boeing 777 plane was shot down by a Buk surface-to-air missile system fired from a location controlled by pro-Russian separatists.

# Sports: US Open Tennis Tournament
Victoria Azarenka won her third US Open title, defeating Serena Williams in the women's singles final.

# Music: VMA Awards
The MTV Video Music Awards took place at The Forum in Inglewood, California, with Iggy Azalea and Eminem winning several awards.

# Technology: Google Acquisition
Google acquired Skybox Imaging, a satellite imaging company, for $500 million to expand its Earth observation capabilities.

# Environmental: California Wildfires
A series of wildfires broke out across Northern California, forcing thousands of people to evacuate their homes and prompting air quality warnings.

# Business: Alibaba IPO
Alibaba Group Holding Ltd's initial public offering (IPO) was priced at $68 per share, raising $25 billion in the largest-ever US IPO.

# Entertainment: TV Emmys
The 66th Primetime Emmy Awards ceremony took place at the Nokia Theatre L.A. Live in Los Angeles, with "Breaking Bad" winning several awards, including best drama series.
