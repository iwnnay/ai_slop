# Events of July 7, 2014
======================================================

### News

* **Israel-Gaza Conflict**: Israeli airstrikes target Gaza City and other areas in the Palestinian territory, killing at least 15 people, as the conflict enters its 14th day. [1]
* **Ebola Outbreak**: The World Health Organization (WHO) declares the Ebola outbreak in West Africa a "public health emergency of international concern," amid growing concerns about the spread of the disease. [2]

### Sports

* **Tour de France**: Vincenzo Nibali wins Stage 7 of the Tour de France, extending his overall lead to over 11 minutes. [3]
* **MLB All-Star Game**: The National League defeats the American League 3-0 in the MLB All-Star Game at Target Field in Minneapolis, Minnesota. [4]

### Cultural

* **Comic-Con International**: Comic-Con International: San Diego wraps up its four-day run, featuring panels and screenings for upcoming movies and TV shows like "Guardians of the Galaxy" and "Game of Thrones." [5]
* **Music Festival**: The 2014 Bonnaroo Music and Arts Festival comes to a close in Manchester, Tennessee, with headlining performances by Kanye West, Elton John, and Vampire Weekend. [6]

### Historical

* **Assassination Anniversary**: The anniversary of the assassination of Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhak Rabin is marked, 20 years after his death at a peace rally in Tel Aviv. [7]
* **Historic Shipwreck**: The wreckage of HMS Victory, Lord Nelson's flagship at the Battle of Trafalgar, is discovered off the coast of Portugal, nearly 200 years after its sinking. [8]

These events represent some of the most significant and notable happenings around the world on July 7th, 2014.
