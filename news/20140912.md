Important Events for 2014-09-12:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on September 12, 2014:

# 2014 Israeli-Gaza Conflict
The conflict between Israel and Gaza continued to escalate with both sides exchanging heavy fire.

# Ukraine Crisis: Russian Invasion
Russian troops crossed the border into eastern Ukraine, sparking fears of a full-scale invasion.

# Syria Civil War: Islamic State Gains Control of Mosul
The Islamic State (ISIS) took control of the Iraqi city of Mosul, marking a significant gain in its quest for a caliphate.

# Ebola Outbreak Spreads to Nigeria
Nigeria confirmed its first case of Ebola, raising concerns about the spread of the disease beyond West Africa.

# Scotland Referendum: No Vote for Independence
Scotland voted "no" on independence from the United Kingdom, rejecting the prospect of going it alone.

# 2014 NFL Season Kicks Off
The 2014 NFL season began with a bang as the Pittsburgh Steelers took on the New England Patriots in the opener.

# NASA's LADEE Spacecraft Crashes into Moon
NASA's LADEE spacecraft, which was designed to study the lunar atmosphere, intentionally crashed into the moon's surface.

# US Open Tennis Tournament Concludes
The 2014 US Open tennis tournament came to a close with Novak Djokovic winning his third title in five years.

# India Wins ICC World Twenty20 Cricket Championship
India defeated Sri Lanka in the final of the ICC World Twenty20 cricket championship, securing its second title in three years.

# European Parliament Votes Against TTIP Free Trade Deal
The European Parliament voted against a proposed free trade deal with the United States, citing concerns over labor and environmental standards.
