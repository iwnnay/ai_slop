# Events of January 4th, 2014

**Politics & Government**

1. **South Sudan Conflict**: The United Nations warns of a possible humanitarian crisis in South Sudan as fighting intensifies between government forces and rebels.
2. **Ukraine Protests**: Thousands gather in Ukraine's capital Kiev to protest President Viktor Yanukovych's decision to delay signing the European Union Association Agreement.

**Sports**

3. **NBA**: The Houston Rockets trade center Omer Asik to the New Orleans Pelicans in exchange for forward Greg Smith and a 2014 first-round draft pick.
4. **NFL**: Quarterback Peyton Manning announces his retirement from the Denver Broncos after winning Super Bowl XLVIII.

**Business & Economy**

5. **Apple Inc.**: Apple's quarterly profit falls by 16% due to weak iPhone sales, but revenue surpasses Wall Street expectations.
6. **European Union**: The EU introduces new rules to prevent another financial crisis like the 2008 global recession.

**Culture & Entertainment**

7. **Film**: The highly anticipated sci-fi film "The Hunger Games: Mockingjay - Part 1" is released worldwide, grossing over $250 million in its opening weekend.
8. **Music**: Taylor Swift's album "Red" tops the US Billboard 200 chart for a second time.

**Science & Technology**

9. **NASA**: NASA's Kepler space telescope discovers three new planets beyond our solar system using gravitational lensing technique.

**Other Events**

10. **Weather**: A severe cold snap hits Eastern Europe, with temperatures in Ukraine and Poland dropping to -30°C (-22°F) and lower, causing widespread disruptions.
