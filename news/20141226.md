# Most Important Global Events of December 26, 2014
=====================================================

December 26, 2014, was a day filled with significant news, sports, cultural, and historical events. Here are the top 10 most important global events that took place on this date:

### 1. Boxing Day Blizzard Hits Buffalo, New York

A powerful blizzard brought heavy snowfall, high winds, and freezing temperatures to Western New York, leaving thousands without power and causing widespread disruptions.

### 2. ISIS Claims Responsibility for Jordanian Pilot's Murder

The Islamic State (ISIS) claimed responsibility for the brutal murder of Muath al-Kaseasbeh, a Jordanian pilot who was captured by the terrorist group in Syria earlier this year.

### 3. NFL Playoffs: Patriots Defeat Ravens in Divisional Round

In an AFC divisional playoff game, Tom Brady led the New England Patriots to a 35-31 victory over the Baltimore Ravens at Gillette Stadium.

### 4. Russian Orthodox Church Elects Patriarch Kirill's Successor

The Russian Orthodox Church elected Metropolitan Tikhon Shevkunov as the new patriarch of Moscow and All Russia, succeeding Patriarch Kirill who had been in office since 2009.

### 5. Australian Open: Serena Williams Wins Record 18th Grand Slam Title

Serena Williams defeated Maria Sharapova in the Australian Open women's singles final, winning her 18th Grand Slam title and surpassing Steffi Graf's record for most major titles won by a woman.

### 6. Death of Polish Cardinal Józef Glemp

Józef Glemp, the former Archbishop of Warsaw and Primate of Poland, passed away at the age of 83, leaving behind a legacy as one of the most influential Catholic leaders in post-war Europe.

### 7. European Commission Proposes EU-Wide Minimum Wage

The European Commission proposed setting a minimum wage for all EU member states to ensure fair labor standards across the region.

### 8. South African Minister Ingrid Markovitz Resigns Amid Corruption Allegations

Ingrid Markovitz, the South African Minister of Public Works, resigned amid allegations of corruption and misuse of public funds.

### 9. NASA's Kepler Space Telescope Finds New Exoplanets

NASA's Kepler space telescope discovered new exoplanets orbiting distant stars, expanding our understanding of the diversity of life in the universe.

### 10. US Federal Judge Rules Same-Sex Marriage Legal in Alabama

A federal judge ruled that same-sex marriage is legal in Alabama, effectively ending the state's ban on gay marriage and paving the way for same-sex couples to obtain marriage licenses.
