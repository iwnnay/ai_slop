Important Events for 2014-11-25:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on November 25, 2014:

# November 25, 2014
## Global Events

### Ukraine crisis escalates: Russian-backed separatists in eastern Ukraine

* Russian-backed separatist rebels in eastern Ukraine reject the country's peace plan and vow to continue fighting government forces.
* The United States and European Union impose new sanctions on Russia in response to its actions.

### Ferguson protests turn violent again

* Protests erupt in Ferguson, Missouri, after a grand jury decides not to indict white police officer Darren Wilson in the fatal shooting of unarmed black teenager Michael Brown.
* Demonstrators clash with police, with several injuries reported and multiple arrests made.

### EU agrees on new sanctions against Russia over Ukraine crisis

* European Union leaders agree to impose new economic sanctions on Russia over its actions in Ukraine.
* The sanctions target key sectors such as energy, finance, and defense.

### NASA's New Horizons spacecraft flies by Pluto

* NASA's New Horizons spacecraft flies within 7,800 miles (12,500 kilometers) of Pluto's surface, providing the first close-up images of the dwarf planet.
* The flyby marks a major milestone in the exploration of the solar system.

### China's new leader Xi Jinping visits Kazakhstan and Turkmenistan

* Chinese President Xi Jinping begins a two-day visit to Kazakhstan and Turkmenistan, aimed at strengthening ties with Central Asian nations.
* The visit is seen as part of Beijing's efforts to expand its influence in the region.

### US Open tennis champion Marin Cilic tests positive for doping

* Croatian tennis player Marin Cilic, who won the 2014 US Open title, tests positive for cocaine use during the tournament.
* Cilic faces a suspension and potential loss of his Grand Slam title.

### Ebola outbreak spreads in West Africa

* The Ebola virus outbreak continues to spread in West Africa, with cases reported in Sierra Leone, Liberia, Guinea, and Nigeria.
* International efforts focus on containing the spread of the disease and providing aid to affected regions.

### Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi visits Australia and Fiji

* Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi begins a three-day visit to Australia and Fiji, aimed at strengthening ties between the nations.
* The visit is seen as part of New Delhi's efforts to expand its influence in the Asia-Pacific region.

### Turkey's Erdogan vows to restore democracy after protests

* Turkish President Recep Tayyip Erdogan vows to restore democracy in his country following widespread protests against his government's authoritarian policies.
* The protests were sparked by a corruption scandal and restrictions on social media platforms.

### North Korea executes senior officials for treason

* North Korean leader Kim Jong-un orders the execution of several high-ranking officials, including the vice premier, for allegedly plotting to overthrow him.
* The executions are seen as part of Pyongyang's efforts to maintain control and crush any perceived dissent.
