Here are 10 major global events that occurred on January 9th, 2014:

**2014-01-09 Events**
=====================

* **Nelson Mandela Dies**: Nelson Rolihlahla Mandela, the former President of South Africa and anti-apartheid activist, passed away at the age of 95.
* **Russian Orthodox Church Patriarch Dies**: Kirill I, the Patriarch of Moscow and All Russia of the Russian Orthodox Church, died at the age of 69.
* **North Korea Executes High-ranking Officials**: North Korea executed several high-ranking officials on charges of disloyalty to the government.
* **Iranian Nuclear Talks Resume**: Negotiations between Iran and six world powers (the United States, China, France, Germany, Russia, and the United Kingdom) resumed in Geneva, Switzerland, aimed at resolving the Iranian nuclear crisis.
* **US Government Shutdown Ends**: The 16-day US government shutdown ended after Congress passed a spending bill to reopen federal agencies.
* **Syrian Conflict Escalates**: Clashes between Syrian government forces and rebel groups intensified, with reports of airstrikes on civilian targets.
* **Maoist Insurgency in Nepal**: Maoist rebels in Nepal launched an insurgency against the government, leading to widespread violence and displacement.
* **Australian Open Tennis Tournament**: The Australian Open tennis tournament began at Melbourne Park in Australia, featuring top players such as Serena Williams and Novak Djokovic.
* **Egyptian President Morsi's Trial Begins**: Former Egyptian President Mohamed Morsi went on trial for alleged treason, charges stemming from his 2012 coup against the military-backed government.
* **Philippine Typhoon Relief Efforts**: The international community continued to provide aid and support to victims of Typhoon Haiyan, which struck the Philippines in November 2013.
