Important Events for 2014-04-28:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on April 28, 2014:

**April 28, 2014**
===============

### News

1. **Russia Annexes Crimea**: The Ukrainian parliament officially asked the International Court of Justice to intervene in the crisis in Ukraine, as Russian President Vladimir Putin signed a treaty to annex Crimea.
2. **US and Russia Agree on Syria**: The United States and Russia announced an agreement on Syrian chemical weapons disarmament, with a deadline for eliminating all declared chemical arms by mid-2014.

### Sports

3. **Stanley Cup Playoffs**: The Boston Bruins defeated the Detroit Red Wings 5-2 in Game 7 to win their series 4-3.
4. **NHL Draft Lottery**: The Florida Panthers won the NHL draft lottery, earning a chance to select first overall in the 2014 NHL Entry Draft.

### Culture

5. **Tony Awards Nominations**: The Tony Award nominations were announced, with "A Raisin in the Sun" and "All the Way" leading the pack.
6. **Netflix Original Series**: Netflix premiered its original series "Orange is the New Black" and "House of Cards," both of which gained critical acclaim.

### History

7. **Vesuvius Eruption Anniversary**: It was the 2,000th anniversary of the eruption of Mount Vesuvius that destroyed Pompeii and Herculaneum.
8. **Srebrenica Massacre Commemoration**: The 20th anniversary of the Srebrenica massacre, which occurred during the Bosnian War, was commemorated.

### Other

9. **Google Glass Recall**: Google announced it would recall its Explorer Edition smart glasses due to concerns over user data privacy and battery life.
10. **Higgs Boson Particle Discovery**: The ATLAS experiment at CERN's Large Hadron Collider announced the discovery of new evidence supporting the Higgs boson particle, a key component of the Standard Model of particle physics.

These events represent some of the significant news, sports, cultural, and historical happenings that took place on April 28, 2014.
