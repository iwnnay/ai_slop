Important Events for 2014-11-15:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on November 15th, 2014:

# Event 1: ISIS Seizes Control of Ramadi
=====================================================

* Date: November 15, 2014
* Location: Ramadi, Iraq
* Description: Islamic State (ISIS) fighters took control of the Iraqi city of Ramadi after a fierce battle with government forces. This marked a significant loss for the Iraqi government and a gain for ISIS.

# Event 2: European Union Approves New Emissions Targets
=====================================================

* Date: November 15, 2014
* Location: Brussels, Belgium
* Description: The European Union (EU) agreed to reduce greenhouse gas emissions by at least 40% below 1990 levels by 2030. This move aimed to combat climate change and meet international targets.

# Event 3: Turkey Launches Airstrikes Against ISIS
=====================================================

* Date: November 15, 2014
* Location: Syria/Iraq
* Description: The Turkish military launched airstrikes against Islamic State (ISIS) positions in Syria and Iraq. This marked the first time Turkey had directly engaged with ISIS.

# Event 4: FIFA Announces 2018 World Cup Host Cities
=====================================================

* Date: November 15, 2014
* Location: Moscow, Russia
* Description: The International Federation of Association Football (FIFA) announced the host cities for the 2018 FIFA World Cup. The selected cities included Moscow, St. Petersburg, Sochi, Kazan, and others.

# Event 5: NASA's Orion Spacecraft Completes First Test Flight
=====================================================

* Date: November 15, 2014
* Location: Cape Canaveral, Florida
* Description: NASA successfully completed the first test flight of its new Orion spacecraft. The mission aimed to prepare for future deep space missions.

# Event 6: China's President Xi Jinping Visits Pakistan
=====================================================

* Date: November 15, 2014
* Location: Islamabad, Pakistan
* Description: Chinese President Xi Jinping visited Pakistan as part of a regional tour. The visit focused on strengthening bilateral ties and economic cooperation between the two nations.

# Event 7: Russia Announces Suspension of Ukraine-EU Association Agreement
=====================================================

* Date: November 15, 2014
* Location: Moscow, Russia
* Description: Russia suspended its ratification process for Ukraine's Association Agreement with the European Union (EU). This move aimed to punish Ukraine for signing the agreement and to assert Russian influence in the region.

# Event 8: NASA's New Horizons Spacecraft Flies By Pluto
=====================================================

* Date: November 15, 2014
* Location: Deep space
* Description: NASA's New Horizons spacecraft flew by Pluto, providing the first close-up images of the dwarf planet. This historic flyby marked a major milestone in planetary exploration.

# Event 9: French President François Hollande Visits Ukraine
=====================================================

* Date: November 15, 2014
* Location: Kiev, Ukraine
* Description: French President François Hollande visited Ukraine to express support for the Ukrainian government and condemn Russia's actions in the region.
