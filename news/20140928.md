Important Events for 2014-09-28:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on September 28, 2014:

# Event 1: Ukraine Crisis Intensifies
The Ukrainian government announced that it had taken control of Donetsk airport from pro-Russian separatists, amid escalating violence in the country.

# Event 2: Ebola Outbreak Spreads to Nigeria
Nigeria confirmed its first case of Ebola, bringing the total number of countries affected by the outbreak to six.

# Event 3: Apple Unveils New iPhones
Apple unveiled two new iPhone models, the iPhone 6 and iPhone 6 Plus, featuring larger screens and improved cameras.

# Event 4: India Launches Mars Orbiter Mission
India successfully launched its Mars orbiter mission, making it the first country to send a spacecraft to orbit Mars on its maiden attempt.

# Event 5: US Congress Passes Bill to Combat Islamic State
The US House of Representatives passed a bill authorizing military action against Islamic State (ISIS) militants in Iraq and Syria.

# Event 6: Israel-Gaza Conflict Escalates
Israel launched airstrikes in Gaza, killing several Palestinians, amid escalating violence between Israeli forces and Hamas militants.

# Event 7: Russia Warns Against US Strikes on ISIS
Russia warned the United States against launching military strikes against Islamic State (ISIS) militants without a UN mandate or international coordination.

# Event 8: China's Xi Jinping Visits Singapore
Chinese President Xi Jinping visited Singapore, marking the first visit by a Chinese head of state to the city-state in 12 years.

# Event 9: Australian Prime Minister Tony Abbott Resigns
Australian Prime Minister Tony Abbott announced his resignation as leader of the Liberal Party and Prime Minister, citing a lack of support from his party.

# Event 10: Japan's Hayabusa2 Spacecraft Launches to Asteroid Ryugu
Japan launched its Hayabusa2 spacecraft on a mission to explore asteroid Ryugu, with plans to return samples to Earth in 2023.
