Important Events for 2014-08-06:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on August 6th, 2014:

# 1: Israel-Hamas Ceasefire
A ceasefire agreement was reached between Israeli forces and Hamas militants in the Gaza Strip, bringing an end to seven weeks of intense conflict.

# 2: Ferguson Protests Continue
Protests continued in Ferguson, Missouri, after the shooting death of Michael Brown by a police officer. Demonstrations turned violent at times, with property damage and injuries reported.

# 3: Malaysia Airlines Flight MH17 Shot Down
Malaysia Airlines Flight MH17 was shot down while flying over Ukraine, killing all 298 people on board. The incident occurred during the ongoing conflict between Ukrainian government forces and Russian-backed separatists.

# 4: Russia and China Sign Gas Deal
Russia's state-owned gas giant Gazprom signed a deal with Chinese energy company CNPC to supply liquefied natural gas (LNG) to China, worth $400 billion over 30 years.
