# Events of March 13, 2014

## Russia and Ukraine Crisis
The Russian military invaded Crimea, leading to international condemnation and sanctions.

## Malaysian Airlines Flight 370 Disappearance Investigation
A report by the Malaysian government concluded that the missing plane likely crashed in the Indian Ocean, killing all 239 on board.

## International Women's Day
Celebrations took place worldwide to mark the economic, political, and social achievements of women.

## South Korea Ferry Disaster Response
Rescue efforts continued after the sinking of the Sewol ferry, which killed over 300 people, mostly students.

## Ebola Outbreak in West Africa
The World Health Organization (WHO) declared a global health emergency as the outbreak spread to Guinea, Liberia, and Sierra Leone.
