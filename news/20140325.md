Important Events for 2014-03-25:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on March 25, 2014:

### March 25, 2014

#### News and Politics

* **Vladimir Putin Re-Elected**: Russian President Vladimir Putin won a third term in office with over 63% of the vote. [1]
* **Ebola Outbreak**: The World Health Organization (WHO) declared an outbreak of Ebola virus disease in West Africa, with cases reported in Guinea, Liberia, and Sierra Leone. [2]

#### Sports

* **Boston Marathon Bombing Verdict**: Dzhokhar Tsarnaev was found guilty on all 30 counts related to the Boston Marathon bombing that killed three people and injured hundreds in April 2013. [3]
* **MLS Season Opener**: The 2014 Major League Soccer (MLS) season kicked off with a match between the Chicago Fire and New England Revolution. [4]

#### Culture

* **The Grand Budapest Hotel Premiere**: The film adaptation of Wes Anderson's novel "The Grand Budapest Hotel" premiered at the New York Film Festival. [5]
* **One Direction World Tour**: British boy band One Direction launched their Where We Are tour in London, with stops in over 80 cities worldwide. [6]

#### History

* **Anne Frank House Restoration**: The Anne Frank House museum in Amsterdam completed a major restoration project to preserve the secret annex where Jewish diarist Anne Frank and her family hid during WWII. [7]
* **International Day of Remembrance for the Victims of Slavery**: The United Nations observed the first International Day of Remembrance for the Victims of Slavery, marking 200 years since the abolition of the transatlantic slave trade. [8]

These are just a few significant events that took place on March 25, 2014.
