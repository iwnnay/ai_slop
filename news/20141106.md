Important Events for 2014-11-06:

Here are the top 10 global events that occurred on November 6th, 2014:

# **Election Day in the United States**
The midterm elections took place across all 50 states, with voters electing members of Congress, governors, and other local officials. The Republican Party made significant gains, gaining control of the Senate and retaining control of the House of Representatives.

# **Hong Kong Pro-Democracy Protesters Clashes with Police**
Pro-democracy protesters in Hong Kong clashed with police in several areas of the city, as tensions escalated over the government's handling of democratic reforms. The protests marked a significant escalation of the movement, which had been ongoing since September.

# **NASA's Orion Spacecraft Completes First Test Flight**
The Orion spacecraft, designed to carry astronauts to Mars and other deep-space destinations, completed its first test flight. The capsule was launched into space on an Atlas V rocket and returned safely after a 4-hour, 2-minute mission.

# **Pope Francis Issues Encyclical on Climate Change**
Pope Francis issued his first encyclical, "Laudato Si'," in which he called for urgent action to address climate change. The document emphasized the need for international cooperation and individual responsibility to mitigate the effects of global warming.

# **FIFA Announces Host Cities for 2018 World Cup**
The Fédération Internationale de Football Association (FIFA) announced the host cities for the 2018 FIFA World Cup in Russia, with 11 cities confirmed: Moscow, St. Petersburg, Sochi, Kazan, Saransk, Kaliningrad, Rostov-on-Don, Nizhny Novgorod, Yekaterinburg, Samara, and Volgograd.

# **North Korea Detains American Tourist**
American tourist Otto Warmbier was detained by North Korean authorities while traveling in Pyongyang. He was later sentenced to 15 years of hard labor on charges of stealing a propaganda banner.

# **Ebola Outbreak Declared Over in Senegal**
The World Health Organization (WHO) declared the Ebola outbreak in Senegal over, bringing an end to the country's largest-ever health crisis.

# **European Union Agrees on New Climate and Energy Package**
The European Union agreed on a new climate and energy package aimed at reducing greenhouse gas emissions by 40% by 2030. The agreement marked a significant step forward for EU climate policy.

# **Apple Reports Record Q4 Sales**
Apple Inc. reported record fourth-quarter sales of $26.5 billion, driven by strong demand for its iPhone and iPad products.

# **Soccer Star Robin van Persie Announces Retirement**
Dutch soccer star Robin van Persie announced his retirement from professional football at the age of 33. Van Persie is a former captain of the Netherlands national team and has played for clubs including Arsenal, Manchester United, and Fenerbahçe.
