Important Events for 2014-09-09:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on September 9, 2014:

**September 9, 2014**

#1: **Ebola Outbreak Update**
The Ebola outbreak in West Africa had reached crisis levels, with the World Health Organization (WHO) reporting over 2,000 cases and more than 1,000 deaths. The outbreak was concentrated in Guinea, Liberia, and Sierra Leone.

#2: **NATO Summit**
The 2014 NATO Summit took place in Wales, UK, bringing together leaders from the 28-member North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO) to discuss issues such as Ukraine-Russia tensions, Afghanistan, and counter-terrorism.

#3: **Australian Federal Election**
The Australian federal election was held on September 9, resulting in a narrow victory for the Liberal Party of Australia under Prime Minister Tony Abbott. The Labor Party, led by Bill Shorten, received 55 seats to the Liberal Party's 57.

#4: **Google Fiber Launches in Louisville**
Google announced the launch of its high-speed internet service, Google Fiber, in Louisville, Kentucky, offering speeds of up to 1 gigabit per second (1 Gbps).

#5: **New Jersey Government Shutdown**
A budget impasse led to a government shutdown in New Jersey, affecting over 60,000 state employees and impacting essential services.

#6: **Pakistan vs. India Cricket Match**
The first-ever T20 cricket match between Pakistan and India took place at the Sher-e-Bangla National Stadium in Dhaka, Bangladesh, with Pakistan winning by 5 runs.

#7: **Apple Buys Beats Electronics**
Apple announced its acquisition of Beats Electronics, a headphones and music streaming company co-founded by hip-hop mogul Dr. Dre and record producer Jimmy Iovine.

#8: **UNICEF Reports on Child Labor**
The United Nations Children's Fund (UNICEF) released a report highlighting the alarming rates of child labor worldwide, with over 168 million children aged 5-14 working in various industries.

#9: **Russian Human Rights Activist Detained**
Prominent Russian human rights activist Alexei Navalny was detained and later released on September 9, amid growing concerns about political repression in Russia.

#10: **FBI Arrests Ross Ulbricht (Dread Pirate Roberts)**
The FBI arrested Ross Ulbricht, also known as Dread Pirate Roberts, the alleged mastermind behind the Silk Road online black market, which had been shut down earlier that year.
