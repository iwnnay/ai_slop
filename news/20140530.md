**May 30, 2014**
==================

### News

1. **Ukraine crisis**: Pro-Russian separatists in eastern Ukraine seized government buildings and police stations, escalating the conflict.

3. **NBA Playoffs**: The Indiana Pacers defeated the Washington Wizards 102-82 in Game 5 of their Eastern Conference semifinal series.
4. **MLB**: The St. Louis Cardinals traded outfielder Allen Craig to the Boston Red Sox in exchange for pitcher Joe Wieland and a player to be named later.

### Cultural

5. **Music**: Taylor Swift's album "Red" was certified 10x Platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA).
6. **Film**: The movie "Godzilla" was released in theaters, starring Aaron Taylor-Johnson, Elizabeth Olsen, and Bryan Cranston.

### Historical

7. **Anniversaries**: It was the 72nd anniversary of the D-Day landings during World War II.
8. **Astronomy**: NASA's Kepler space telescope observed its 3,000th exoplanet candidate.

### Business
