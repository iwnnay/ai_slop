Important Events for 2014-10-05:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on October 5th, 2014:

# Events of October 5th, 2014
=====================================================



### News

1. **Ukraine Conflict Escalates**: Pro-Russian separatists in eastern Ukraine capture key cities, including Luhansk and Donetsk, as the conflict between Ukrainian forces and pro-Russian rebels intensifies.
2. **Ebola Outbreak Spreads**: The World Health Organization (WHO) reports a significant increase in Ebola cases, with 4,766 reported cases and 2,219 deaths in Guinea, Liberia, and Sierra Leone.

### Sports

3. **MLB Playoffs**: In the National League Division Series, the San Francisco Giants defeat the Washington Nationals 3-0 to take a 2-1 lead in their best-of-five series.
4. **NASCAR**: Kevin Harvick wins the Hollywood Casino 400 at Kansas Speedway, his fourth win of the season.

### Culture

5. **Book Release**: J.K. Rowling's long-awaited novel "The Silkworm" (also known as "Career of Evil") is released in the UK.
6. **Music Awards**: The MTV Europe Music Awards take place in Amsterdam, with winners including Eminem, Katy Perry, and One Direction.

### Historical

7. **Rwanda Genocide Memorial**: A memorial to honor the victims of the 1994 Rwandan genocide is inaugurated in Kigali, Rwanda.
8. **World War I Commemoration**: The Commonwealth War Graves Commission (CWGC) launches a commemorative program to mark the centenary of World War I.

### Science and Technology

9. **NASA Launches Spacecraft**: NASA's LADEE spacecraft is launched from Cape Canaveral Air Force Station, Florida, on a mission to study the Moon's exosphere.
10. **Google Acquires Nest**: Google announces it has acquired Nest, a smart home technology company founded by Tony Fadell and Matt Rogers.
