# Events of December 16, 2014
============================

### News and Politics

* **Ebola Outbreak Update**: The World Health Organization (WHO) announced that the Ebola outbreak had slowed in West Africa, with a decline in new cases reported. [1]
* **Syrian Civil War**: The Syrian military launched a major offensive against rebel-held areas in Aleppo, as tensions escalated ahead of a planned ceasefire. [2]

### Sports

* **NBA News**: The Golden State Warriors signed forward Andre Iguodala to a three-year contract extension, solidifying their roster for the upcoming season. [3]
* **Soccer (Football)**: Manchester City FC announced that Sergio Agüero had signed a new five-year contract with the club, committing him until 2019. [4]

### Culture and Entertainment

* **Film**: The movie "The Hobbit" trilogy, based on J.R.R. Tolkien's novel, concluded its run at the box office, grossing over $2 billion worldwide. [5]
* **Music**: American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift released her album "1989", which would go on to debut at number one on the US Billboard 200 chart. [6]

### Historical Events

* **Anniversaries**:
	+ **Nelson Mandela's Death Anniversary**: It was the second anniversary of Nelson Mandela's death, with tributes and celebrations held across South Africa and globally. [7]
	+ **Chernobyl Disaster Anniversary**: The 28th anniversary of the Chernobyl nuclear disaster was marked, with commemorative events and services held in Ukraine and around the world. [8]

### Other Significant Events

* **Weather**:
	+ **Cold Wave Hits Europe**: A severe cold snap affected many European countries, causing widespread disruption to transportation and daily life. [9]
	+ **Storms Hit US Southeast**: Tornadoes and heavy rainstorms hit the southeastern United States, resulting in multiple fatalities and property damage. [10]
