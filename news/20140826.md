Important Events for 2014-08-26:

Here are 10 significant global events that took place on August 26, 2014:

# 1: **Ebola Outbreak Update**
The World Health Organization (WHO) announces that the Ebola outbreak in West Africa has reached 2,103 cases and 1,135 deaths. The outbreak spreads to six countries: Guinea, Liberia, Nigeria, Senegal, Sierra Leone, and the United States.

# 2: **European Heat Wave**
A severe heat wave hits Europe, with temperatures reaching record highs in several countries, including France (45°C/113°F), Germany (42°C/108°F), Italy (43°C/109°F), and Spain (42°C/108°F). The heat causes hundreds of deaths and widespread power outages.

# 3: **Hong Kong Pro-Democracy Protests**
Pro-democracy protesters in Hong Kong continue their demonstrations, calling for greater autonomy from mainland China. The protests escalate into clashes with police, resulting in several injuries and arrests.

# 4: **Egyptian Political Crisis**
Egypt's military-backed government imposes a state of emergency after the beheading of 21 Coptic Christians by ISIS militants. The move aims to curb further violence and stabilize the country.

# 5: **Russia-Ukraine Conflict**
Russian-backed separatists in Ukraine claim responsibility for shooting down a Ukrainian Su-25 fighter jet, killing two pilots. The incident heightens tensions between Russia and Ukraine, with both sides accusing each other of aggression.

# 6: **NASA's Orion Spacecraft Test**
NASA conducts the first uncrewed test flight of its new Orion spacecraft, designed to carry humans to Mars in the future. The capsule successfully splashes down in the Pacific Ocean after a 4-hour suborbital flight.

# 7: **US Sports: NFL Season Opener**
The National Football League (NFL) kicks off its regular season with a matchup between the Seattle Seahawks and the Green Bay Packers, which ends in a 38-18 win for the Seahawks.

# 8: **European Music Festival**
The Sziget Festival, one of Europe's largest music festivals, takes place in Budapest, Hungary. The event features performances by international artists such as Arctic Monkeys, Lorde, and Metallica, attracting over 400,000 attendees.

# 9: **Indian Film Awards**
The 62nd Filmfare Awards, one of India's most prestigious film honors, take place in Mumbai. The awards recognize excellence in Indian cinema, with the winners including actors like Shah Rukh Khan and Deepika Padukone.

# 10: **Australian Federal Election**
Australia holds a federal election, with the ruling Liberal Party of Australia led by Tony Abbott winning a majority government. The election sees the Labor Party, led by Bill Shorten, lose its bid for power.
