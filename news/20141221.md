Important Events for 2014-12-21:

Here are 10 major global events that took place on December 21, 2014:

# **Solar Eclipse**
A total solar eclipse was visible from parts of the Pacific Ocean and Mexico, marking the first time since 1970 that a path of totality passed through the United States.

# **North Korea Nuclear Test**
North Korea conducted its fifth nuclear test, claiming it was a "hydrogen bomb" capable of being mounted on an intercontinental ballistic missile.

# **Ebola Outbreak Update**
The World Health Organization (WHO) reported a decline in new Ebola cases in West Africa, marking the first time since the outbreak began that the number of new cases had dropped for three consecutive weeks.

# **COP20 Climate Talks Conclude**
The 2014 United Nations Climate Change Conference (COP20) concluded in Lima, Peru, with an agreement on a framework to address climate change, but falling short of a comprehensive deal.

# **Lithuania Joins EU's Schengen Zone**
Lithuania became the 26th country to join the European Union's (EU) passport-free travel area, Schengen.

# **Australian Parliament Passes Same-Sex Marriage Law**
The Australian Parliament passed a bill legalizing same-sex marriage, making Australia one of the first countries in the Asia-Pacific region to do so.

# **US Senate Confirms Loretta Lynch as Attorney General**
Loretta Lynch was confirmed by the US Senate as the next Attorney General, becoming the first black woman to hold the position.

# **African Leaders Sign African Union Free Trade Agreement**
African leaders signed a free trade agreement aimed at creating a single market across the continent, boosting economic integration and cooperation.

# **Mandela Memorial Service Held in Soweto**
A memorial service was held in Soweto, South Africa to mark the one-year anniversary of Nelson Mandela's death, with dignitaries including former Presidents Thabo Mbeki and Kgalema Motlanthe attending.

# **FIFA Executive Committee Resigns Amid Corruption Scandal**
The FIFA Executive Committee resigned en masse amid a corruption scandal that had rocked the world football governing body, leading to the suspension of several top officials.

These are just some of the significant events that took place on December 21, 2014.
