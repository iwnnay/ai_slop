Here are 10 major global events that took place on March 22, 2014:

**March 22, 2014**

#1: Crimea votes to secede from Ukraine
The Crimean parliament declared independence from Ukraine and sought to join Russia in a move widely condemned by the international community.

#4: EU leaders meet to discuss Ukraine crisis
European Union leaders held emergency talks to address the escalating crisis in Ukraine and impose sanctions on Russian officials responsible for the annexation of Crimea.

#5: Indian politician Anna Hazare begins fast-unto-death protest
Anti-corruption activist Anna Hazare started a hunger strike, known as a "fast unto death," to demand stronger anti-graft laws in India.

#6: North Korea executes high-ranking official on corruption charges
The government announced the execution of Ri Yong-ho, a former military chief and top government official, for corruption and other crimes.

#7: Russian annexation of Crimea officially recognized by South Ossetia
South Ossetia, a breakaway region of Georgia supported by Russia, became the first country to formally recognize Crimea's independence from Ukraine.

#8: Pakistani politician Imran Khan takes oath as PM
Cricket legend-turned-politician Imran Khan was sworn in as the 22nd Prime Minister of Pakistan, marking the end of Nawaz Sharif's five-year tenure.

#9: Chinese spacecraft enters into orbit around moon
The Chang'e 3 spacecraft, China's first lunar probe, entered into a stable orbit around the moon and began transmitting data back to Earth.

#10: Boston Celtics defeat Toronto Raptors in NBA game
In an NBA matchup, the Boston Celtics defeated the Toronto Raptors 105-99 at TD Garden in Boston, with Jeff Green scoring a season-high 31 points.
