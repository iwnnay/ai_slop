# Events of December 4, 2014
======================================================

## News

* **Ukraine crisis**: Pro-Russian separatists in eastern Ukraine seize control of the city hall and police station in Mariupol, a key port city. (Source: BBC)
* **Ebola outbreak**: The World Health Organization reports that Guinea has seen a surge in Ebola cases, with 97 new cases reported in the past week. (Source: CNN)

## Sports

* **NBA**: The Chicago Bulls trade center Joakim Noah to the New York Knicks for center Robin Lopez and guard Kevin Seraphin. (Source: ESPN)
* **Premier League**: Manchester City wins the Premier League title after a 2-0 win over Aston Villa. (Source: BBC)

## Culture

* **Music**: Taylor Swift's album "1989" is named the best-selling album of 2014 by the Recording Industry Association of America. (Source: Billboard)
* **Film**: The movie "Interstellar", directed by Christopher Nolan, is released in theaters across the United States. (Source: IMDb)

## History

* **Nobel Prizes**: The Nobel Prize in Physics is awarded to Isamu Akasaki, Hiroshi Amano, and Shuji Nakamura for their invention of efficient blue light-emitting diodes. (Source: Nobelprize.org)
* **World War II**: The last remaining survivor of the USS Arizona, Don Stratton, passes away at the age of 92. (Source: Fox News)

## Other

* **Technology**: Apple announces that it will be acquiring Beats Electronics, a headphones and music streaming company founded by Dr. Dre and Jimmy Iovine. (Source: The Verge)
* **Business**: The European Union slaps anti-dumping duties on Chinese solar panels, citing unfair trade practices. (Source: Reuters)
