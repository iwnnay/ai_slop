Here are 10 major global events that took place on December 5, 2014:

# Events of December 5, 2014
======================================================

### 1. Israel-Gaza Conflict Intensifies

The Israeli military launched airstrikes against targets in the Gaza Strip, killing at least 13 Palestinians and injuring many more. The conflict escalated after a Palestinian gunman opened fire on an Israeli bus stop, wounding several people.

### 2. European Union's Energy Union Launched

The European Commission proposed a new energy union to reduce dependence on Russian gas imports and increase renewable energy production. The plan aimed to make the EU a global leader in clean energy.

### 3. North Korea's Nuke Test Site Sealed

North Korea sealed its Punggye-ri nuclear test site, claiming it had completed all nuclear tests at the site. The move was seen as a significant step towards denuclearization.

### 4. Ukraine Rebel Leader Killed

Alexander Borodai, the self-proclaimed mayor of Donetsk, was killed in an explosion at his home. The incident marked a turning point in the conflict between Ukrainian government forces and pro-Russian separatists.

### 5. Australian PM Steps Down

Australian Prime Minister Tony Abbott announced his resignation, citing the country's economic woes and declining popularity.

### 6. NASA's Orion Spacecraft Tested

NASA conducted a successful test of its new Orion spacecraft, designed to take humans beyond low-Earth orbit for the first time since Apollo.

### 7. China's Xi Jinping Meets with US Sec'y Kerry

Chinese President Xi Jinping met with US Secretary of State John Kerry in Beijing to discuss issues like North Korea and climate change.

### 8. Japan's Emperor Akihito Abdicates

Japan's Emperor Akihito announced his decision to abdicate the throne, citing his advanced age (81) and poor health.

### 9. German Chancellor Merkel Visits Poland

German Chancellor Angela Merkel visited Poland to strengthen ties between their two countries and discuss regional security issues.

### 10. India's Narendra Modi Meets with Australian PM

Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi met with then-Australian Prime Minister Tony Abbott in Sydney, discussing bilateral trade agreements and defense cooperation.

These events highlight the significant developments that took place on December 5, 2014, covering international politics, conflict, science, leadership changes, and more.
