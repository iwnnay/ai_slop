Important Events for 2014-03-26:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on March 26, 2014:

**March 26, 2014**
=====================================================

### News and Politics

1. **Ukraine Crisis**: Pro-Russian protesters storm government buildings in Donetsk, Ukraine, amid escalating tensions between pro-Russian separatists and the Ukrainian government.
2. **South Korea Ferry Disaster**: The Sewol ferry tragedy worsens as divers recover 32 bodies from the sunken ship, with many more still missing.

### Sports

3. **NBA Playoffs**: The Indiana Pacers clinch their fourth consecutive Central Division title with a 102-79 win over the Miami Heat.
4. **MLB Opening Day**: Baseball season begins in the United States, with games played by all 30 teams, including the Boston Red Sox's 8-5 loss to the Philadelphia Phillies.

### Culture and Entertainment

5. **Film Release**: The Disney animated film "Muppets Most Wanted" is released in theaters worldwide.
6. **Music Chart**: Pharrell Williams' song "Happy" reaches number one on the US Billboard Hot 100 chart, becoming his second consecutive hit single to top the chart.

### Technology and Science

7. **Space Exploration**: NASA's Mars Atmosphere and Volatile Evolution (MAVEN) spacecraft begins its mission to study the Martian atmosphere and understand how it lost its atmosphere over time.
8. **Internet Governance**: The Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers (ICANN) approves the creation of new top-level domains, including .music, .sports, and .guru.

### History

9. **Anniversary**: It's been 45 years since the Apollo 13 mission was aborted after an explosion occurred on board, putting the lives of three astronauts at risk.
10. **Archeological Discovery**: Archaeologists in Peru announce the discovery of a 2,000-year-old ceramic jar containing evidence of ancient beer production.

These events represent some of the significant happenings around the world on March 26, 2014.
