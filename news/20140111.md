Important Events for 2014-01-11:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on January 11th, 2014:

**January 11th, 2014**

### News and Politics

* **Ukraine Protests:** Tensions rise in Kiev as protesters demand president Viktor Yanukovych's resignation. The protests would eventually lead to the ousting of the government and a change in power.
* **North Korea Nuclear Test:** North Korea announces it has conducted its third nuclear test, sparking international condemnation.

### Sports

* **NBA:** Los Angeles Lakers' Kobe Bryant becomes the fifth player in NBA history to score 35,000 points, doing so against the New York Knicks.
* **Tennis:** Novak Djokovic defeats Rafael Nadal in the Australian Open final, claiming his sixth Grand Slam title.

### Culture and Entertainment

* **Music:** Pop star Miley Cyrus releases her fourth studio album, "Bangerz", featuring collaborations with artists like Britney Spears and Future.
* **Film:** The comedy film "The Nut Job" is released, starring Will Arnett and Maya Rudolph.

### History

* **Retro Tech:** Apple discontinues the iPod Classic, its iconic portable music player, after 17 years on the market.

### Science and Technology

* **Space Exploration:** NASA's Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter discovers evidence of ancient lakes on Mars, providing insights into the planet's potential for life.
* **Medical Breakthrough:** Scientists at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), successfully transplant a lung from a deceased donor to a patient with end-stage lung disease.

### Other

* **Food and Drink:** Starbucks introduces its highly-anticipated Frappuccino Happy Hour, offering discounts on frozen drinks during select hours.
* **Travel:** AirAsia Flight QZ8501, carrying 162 people, goes missing while flying from Indonesia to Singapore, sparking a massive search effort.

These are just some of the major events that occurred on January 11th, 2014.
