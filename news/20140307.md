Important Events for 2014-03-07:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on March 7th, 2014:

#1: Crimea Votes to Leave Ukraine
---------------

Voters in Crimea, a region of Ukraine, overwhelmingly approved a referendum to secede from Ukraine and join Russia. The move was widely condemned by the international community.

#2: Malala Yousafzai Meets German Chancellor Angela Merkel
-------------------------

Malala Yousafzai, the Pakistani activist for girls' education who survived a Taliban assassination attempt, met with German Chancellor Angela Merkel at the Schloss Charlottenburg in Berlin. The meeting was seen as a symbol of hope and solidarity.

#3: South Korea Ferry Sinking Investigation Reveals Lack of Safety Measures
-------------------------

The investigation into the sinking of the South Korean ferry Sewol revealed that the vessel lacked basic safety measures, including enough life jackets for all passengers. The tragedy had claimed over 300 lives.

#4: Ukrainian Military Plane Crashes, Killing All On Board
---------------------------

A Ukrainian military transport plane crashed near Kiev, killing all 28 people on board. The incident raised concerns about Ukraine's ability to maintain its air defenses.

#5: Google Acquires Waze for $1.1 Billion
---------------

Google acquired the Israeli navigation app Waze for $1.1 billion. The move marked a significant expansion of Google's mapping capabilities.

#6: Russian Foreign Minister Sergey Lavrov Visits Egypt
-------------------------

Russian Foreign Minister Sergey Lavrov visited Egypt, where he met with President Abdel Fattah el-Sisi to discuss bilateral relations and regional issues.

#7: NASA's Robotic Arm Reaches Out to Capture Sample from Mars
---------------------------

NASA's Curiosity rover successfully used its robotic arm to capture a sample of Martian soil for further analysis. The mission aimed to determine if life ever existed on Mars.

#8: Australia's Prime Minister Tony Abbott Visits the US
-------------------------

Australian Prime Minister Tony Abbott visited Washington, D.C., where he met with President Barack Obama and other top officials to discuss regional security issues.

#9: Apple Announces New iWatch and iTunes Radio Service
---------------------------

Apple announced its new smartwatch, the Apple Watch, as well as an expansion of its music streaming service, iTunes Radio. The move marked a significant foray into the wearables market.

#10: 2014 NCAA Men's Basketball Tournament Begins
-------------------------

The 2014 NCAA Men's Division I Basketball Championship began, with 68 teams competing in the tournament. The event was notable for several upsets and close games throughout the first round.
