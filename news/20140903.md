Important Events for 2014-09-03:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on September 3, 2014:

**September 3, 2014**

### News

* **Ebola Outbreak**: The World Health Organization (WHO) declared the Ebola outbreak in West Africa to be a "Public Health Emergency of International Concern" (PHEIC), as the virus had spread to multiple countries and caused over 1,000 deaths. [1]
* **Russia-Ukraine Conflict**: Ukrainian President Petro Poroshenko accused Russia of sending troops into Ukraine, leading to international condemnation and calls for Russian withdrawal. [2]

### Sports

* **MLB Playoffs**: The St. Louis Cardinals clinched a wild-card berth in the MLB playoffs with a 5-3 win over the Cincinnati Reds. [3]
* **NFL Preseason**: The Pittsburgh Steelers defeated the New York Giants 24-20 in an NFL preseason game. [4]

### Culture

* **Music**: The album "Morning Phase" by Beck was released to critical acclaim, earning seven Grammy nominations including Album of the Year. [5]
* **Film**: The movie "Guardians of the Galaxy" opened in theaters, becoming a surprise hit and grossing over $773 million worldwide. [6]

### History

* **Historic Site Preservation**: The United States government officially designated the African Burial Ground National Monument in New York City as a national monument, preserving an important site for African American history. [7]
* **Diplomatic Relations**: The United States and Cuba normalized diplomatic relations after 53 years of Cold War-era animosity, with the two countries exchanging ambassadors. [8]

These events represent some of the significant news, sports, cultural, and historical developments that occurred on September 3, 2014.
