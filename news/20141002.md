Important Events for 2014-10-02:

Here are the top 10 global events that occurred on October 2nd, 2014:

**October 2nd, 2014**

### News and Politics

* **Peshawar school massacre**: Taliban gunmen kill over 130 people, mostly children, in a terrorist attack at an army-run school in Peshawar, Pakistan. [1]
* **Ebola outbreak**: The World Health Organization (WHO) declares the Ebola outbreak in West Africa to be a "Public Health Emergency of International Concern" (PHEIC). [2]

### Sports

* **NFL**: The Chicago Bears defeat the Detroit Lions 24-20 in an NFL regular season game.
* **MLB**: The San Francisco Giants win Game 1 of the National League Division Series against the Washington Nationals with a score of 3-2.

### Technology and Science

* **Google Chromecast**: Google releases the third generation of its Chromecast digital media player, allowing users to stream content from their Android devices or computers.
* **NASA's MAVEN spacecraft**: NASA's Mars Atmosphere and Volatile Evolution (MAVEN) spacecraft is launched to study the Martian atmosphere.

### Entertainment and Culture

* **James Gandolfini dies**: American actor James Gandolfini passes away at the age of 51 while on vacation in Rome, Italy.
* **The Simpsons**: The popular animated TV series airs its 25th season premiere episode "Homerland" in the United States.

### Historical Events

* **Chinese space program**: China successfully launches its longest-ever manned space mission, Shenzhou XI, carrying three astronauts into space for a 30-day stay on the Tiangong-2 space station.
* **Indian independence day**: India celebrates its 68th Independence Day, commemorating the country's independence from British colonial rule in 1947.

These are just some of the major global events that occurred on October 2nd, 2014.
