# September 17, 2014: Major Global Events

### News and Politics

1. **Ukraine Crisis**: Pro-Russian separatists in Ukraine's Donetsk region declare independence from Kiev, amid ongoing conflict with Ukrainian government forces.
2. **Ebola Outbreak**: The World Health Organization (WHO) confirms 3,000 cases of Ebola in West Africa, with over 1,500 deaths.

### Sports

1. **MLB Playoffs**: The St. Louis Cardinals eliminate the Los Angeles Dodgers from the National League Division Series, advancing to the NLCS.
2. **NFL Week 2**: In a Sunday Night Football matchup, the Seattle Seahawks defeat the San Francisco 49ers 29-3 at CenturyLink Field.

### Culture and Entertainment

1. **Music Release**: American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift releases her fifth studio album, "1989", featuring hit singles like "Shake It Off" and "Blank Space".
2. **Film Premiere**: The sci-fi adventure film "The Maze Runner" is released in theaters worldwide, based on James Dashner's bestselling novel.

### History

1. **9/11 Memorial**: The 13th anniversary of the September 11 attacks is marked with memorial services and ceremonies around the world.
2. **Pearl Harbor Remembrance**: The 73rd anniversary of the Pearl Harbor attack is observed in Hawaii, remembering the lives lost on December 7, 1941.
