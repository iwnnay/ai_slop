Important Events for 2014-10-20:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on October 20, 2014:

**October 20, 2014**
=====================



### Global News

* **Ebola Outbreak**: The World Health Organization (WHO) declared the Ebola outbreak in West Africa to be a Public Health Emergency of International Concern (PHEIC), as the virus had spread to several countries including Guinea, Liberia, and Sierra Leone.
* **North Korea Nuclear Test**: North Korea announced that it had conducted its fifth nuclear test, sparking international condemnation and concerns over the potential consequences for regional stability.

### Sports

* **NFL**: The Philadelphia Eagles defeated the Arizona Cardinals 31-21 in a Monday Night Football game at Lincoln Financial Field.
* **NBA**: The Chicago Bulls traded away Luol Deng to the Cleveland Cavaliers in exchange for Andrew Bynum, a move seen as a significant shake-up in the NBA landscape.

### Cultural

* **Film Premiere**: The film "Fury", starring Brad Pitt and Shia LaBeouf, premiered at the Zurich Film Festival.
* **Music Release**: Country music superstar Taylor Swift released her highly anticipated album "1989" to critical acclaim and commercial success.

### Historical

* **D-Day Anniversary**: The 70th anniversary of the Allied invasion of Normandy during World War II was commemorated with ceremonies in France, the United States, and other countries.
* **FIFA U-17 World Cup**: The FIFA Under-17 Women's World Cup began in Costa Rica, featuring teams from around the world competing for the championship.

These are just a few of the major events that occurred on October 20, 2014.
