# Events of August 9th, 2014
===============

* **Ebola outbreak**: The World Health Organization (WHO) reported that the Ebola virus had spread to three countries in West Africa: Guinea, Liberia, and Sierra Leone. This marked a significant escalation of the epidemic.
* **Israel-Gaza conflict**: Israeli airstrikes targeted several areas in the Gaza Strip, including the Shujaiya neighborhood, where Palestinian militants were hiding. The conflict had been ongoing since July 8th.
* **Stock market news**: The Dow Jones Industrial Average fell by 143 points, closing at 16,524.54, due to concerns about economic growth and interest rates.
* **Russia-Ukraine conflict**: Pro-Russian separatists in Ukraine's Donetsk region claimed that Ukrainian forces had shelled a residential area, killing several civilians. This was part of the ongoing conflict between Ukraine and Russia-backed rebels.
* **Weather news**: A powerful storm system brought severe thunderstorms and tornadoes to the Midwest United States, resulting in at least 12 reported fatalities.
* **Sports**: The Chicago Cubs defeated the Pittsburgh Pirates 5-2 in a Major League Baseball (MLB) game. This marked the Cubs' fifth consecutive win.
* **Entertainment news**: The movie "Guardians of the Galaxy" was released on DVD and Blu-ray, becoming one of the top-grossing movies of the year.
* **Technology news**: Apple announced that it would be discontinuing its iPod Classic music player, a product that had been popular since 2001.
* **Space exploration**: NASA's Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter captured stunning images of the Martian surface, providing valuable insights into the planet's geology and potential for life.
* **Arts news**: The Metropolitan Museum of Art (The Met) in New York City opened an exhibit featuring works by French Impressionist artist Claude Monet, including some of his most famous paintings.
