Important Events for 2014-05-14:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on May 14, 2014:

**2014-05-14 Events**
======================================================

### 1. Ukrainian Presidential Election

The second round of Ukraine's presidential election took place, with Petro Poroshenko declared the winner. This marked a significant turning point in Ukraine's struggle for independence from Russia.

### 2. NBA Playoffs: Eastern Conference Semifinals

The Indiana Pacers defeated the Washington Wizards 95-92 in Game 5 of their NBA playoff series, taking a 3-2 lead.

### 3. Apple Acquires Beats Electronics

Apple acquired Beats Electronics, a headphones and music streaming company founded by Dr. Dre and Jimmy Iovine, for $3 billion.

### 4. Russia-Western Tensions Rise

Russia's annexation of Crimea in March had escalated tensions with Western nations, including the United States and European Union. On May 14, NATO Secretary-General Anders Fogh Rasmussen warned that Russia's actions were "a grave threat" to European security.

### 5. Ebola Outbreak Reaches Senegal

The deadly Ebola virus outbreak spread from Guinea to neighboring Senegal, prompting international health organizations to increase their response efforts.

### 6. Turkish Referendum on Kurdish Rights

Turkish voters approved a constitutional amendment granting greater autonomy to the country's Kurdish minority, seen as a key step towards resolving the country's long-standing Kurdish conflict.

### 7. Indian Politician Anna Hazare Arrested

Indian social activist Anna Hazare was arrested for protesting against government corruption and demanding passage of the Jan Lokpal Bill (Citizens' Ombudsman Bill).

### 8. World Health Organization Ebola Guidelines Released

The WHO released guidelines for managing Ebola outbreaks, emphasizing the importance of early detection, isolation, and contact tracing to contain the spread of the virus.

### 9. South Korea Ferry Sinks

A passenger ferry carrying over 476 people, mostly high school students, sank off the coast of South Korea, resulting in one of the country's worst maritime disasters in decades.

### 10. Google Acquires Skybox Imaging

Google acquired Skybox Imaging, a satellite imaging company, for $500 million, expanding its capabilities in Earth observation and imagery analysis.

These events represent some of the most significant global happenings on May 14, 2014.
