# **Economic**: The European Union and Ukraine sign an association agreement, strengthening economic ties between the two nations.

# **Science**:
- NASA's New Horizons spacecraft passes within 27,000 miles (43,000 km) of Pluto's surface, providing the first close-up images of the dwarf planet.

# **Technology**:

# **Health**:
- A study published in the journal The Lancet finds that antiretroviral therapy can significantly reduce the transmission of HIV from mother to child during pregnancy and childbirth.
- The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) reports a surge in cases of enterovirus D68, a virus that causes respiratory illness.
