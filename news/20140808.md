# Events of August 8th, 2014
=====================================

1. **Ebola Outbreak**: The World Health Organization (WHO) confirms the first case of Ebola in Nigeria, bringing the total number of cases to 337 and deaths to 187.

2. **Israel-Hamas Ceasefire**: A temporary ceasefire between Israel and Hamas comes into effect, following a week-long conflict that has killed hundreds and displaced thousands.

3. **Tiananmen Square Incident**: It's been 25 years since the Chinese military cracked down on pro-democracy protests in Tiananmen Square, killing hundreds and arresting thousands.

4. **NASA's LADEE Mission Ends**: The Lunar Atmosphere and Dust Environment Explorer (LADEE) spacecraft, which studied the moon's thin atmosphere, crashes into the lunar surface after a successful 390-day mission.

5. **Robin Williams Found Dead**: Beloved actor Robin Williams is found dead in his home in California, with an investigation later revealing that he died from asphyxia due to hanging.

6. **Ferguson Unrest**: Protests erupt in Ferguson, Missouri, following the shooting death of unarmed black teenager Michael Brown by police officer Darren Wilson, sparking national debate on racial tensions and police brutality.

7. **India Floods**: Heavy rainfall causes devastating floods in India's southern state of Tamil Nadu, killing over 250 people and displacing thousands more.

8. **European Central Bank Rate Cut**: The European Central Bank cuts its benchmark interest rate to a record low of 0.5% to boost the struggling eurozone economy.

9. **Tiger Woods' PGA Championship Withdrawal**: Tiger Woods withdraws from the PGA Championship due to back pain, marking his second consecutive major tournament withdrawal.

10. **Sochi Olympics Re-Evaluation**: The International Olympic Committee announces a re-evaluation of the 2014 Sochi Winter Olympics, following allegations of corruption and human rights violations in Russia's preparation for the games.
