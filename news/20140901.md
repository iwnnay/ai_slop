Here are 10 major events that occurred on September 1st, 2014:

**September 1st, 2014:**

### International News

* **Taliban attack kills 19 in Afghanistan**: A Taliban attack on a guesthouse in Kabul, Afghanistan killed at least 19 people and wounded several others. [Source: CNN](https://www.cnn.com/2014/09/01/world/taliban-attack-kills-19-in-kabul/index.html)
* **Russia-Ukraine conflict escalates**: Ukrainian forces claimed to have repelled a Russian-backed separatist attack on the eastern city of Donetsk, as tensions continued to rise in the region. [Source: BBC News](https://www.bbc.com/news/world-europe-29014422)
