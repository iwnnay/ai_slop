Important Events for 2014-01-29:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on January 29, 2014:

**2014-01-29 Events**
=====================



### News

* **Snowden asylum offer**: Russia says it will grant temporary asylum to former US spy Edward Snowden, allowing him to live in the country legally.
* **US Senate confirms Yellen**: The US Senate confirms Janet Yellen as the first female Chairman of the Federal Reserve.

### Sports

* **NFL Pro Bowl**: The NFL Pro Bowl is played at Aloha Stadium in Honolulu, Hawaii. The AFC defeats the NFC 25-17.

### Culture

* **Grammy Awards nominations**: The nominees for the 56th Annual Grammy Awards are announced.
* **Banksy's 'Dismaland' opens**: British street artist Banksy unveils his theme park, "Dismaland", in Weston-super-Mare, England.

### History

* **Pope Benedict XVI turns 88**: Former Pope Benedict XVI celebrates his 88th birthday.
* **US President George H.W. Bush's birthday**: The late US President George H.W. Bush would have celebrated his 91st birthday if he were alive.

### Other

* **India's Telangana state formed**: India creates its 29th state, Telangana, carved out of Andhra Pradesh.
* **South Sudan peace talks stall**: Peace talks between the South Sudanese government and rebel forces break down in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia.

These events are significant because they reflect major developments in international relations (Snowden asylum), economics (Yellen's confirmation), sports (Pro Bowl), culture (Grammy Awards and Banksy's theme park), history (Pope Benedict XVI's birthday and the formation of Telangana state), and global politics (South Sudan peace talks).
