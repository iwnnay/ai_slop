# October 13, 2014 - Major Global Events
======================================================

## Russia-Ukraine Crisis: Pro-Russia Rebels Gain Control of Donetsk Airport

* Ukrainian government forces were forced to withdraw from Donetsk International Airport after a prolonged battle with pro-Russian separatists.
* The move marked a significant setback for the Ukrainian military and a victory for the rebels.

## Ebola Outbreak Update: Cases Surpass 7,000 in West Africa

* The World Health Organization (WHO) reported that the number of Ebola cases had surpassed 7,000 in West Africa, with over 3,400 deaths.
* The outbreak continued to spread rapidly across Guinea, Liberia, and Sierra Leone.

## Germanwings Flight 9525 Crashes in France: All 150 On Board Killed

* A Germanwings Airbus A320 crashed into the French Alps, killing all 150 people on board.
* The cause of the crash was later attributed to deliberate actions by the co-pilot, who intentionally plunged the plane into the mountains.

## Russia Sanctions: European Union Expands Economic Restrictions

* The European Union expanded its economic sanctions against Russia in response to its continued support for pro-Russian separatists in Ukraine.
* The new measures targeted key Russian industries, including defense and energy sectors.

## Hong Kong Pro-Democracy Protests Continue

* Thousands of pro-democracy protesters occupied major streets in Hong Kong, demanding greater autonomy from China.
* The protests were sparked by the Chinese government's decision to limit electoral reforms in the territory.

## Ebola Vaccine Trials Begin in US and Europe

* The first human trials for an Ebola vaccine began in the United States and Europe, as researchers worked to develop a vaccine against the deadly virus.

## Nobel Prize in Physics: Isamu Akasaki, Hiroshi Amano, and Shuji Nakamura Awarded

* The Nobel Prize in Physics was awarded to Isamu Akasaki, Hiroshi Amano, and Shuji Nakamura for their work on blue LEDs.
* Their invention has enabled the widespread use of energy-efficient lighting.

## MLB Playoffs: Kansas City Royals Defeat Baltimore Orioles 8-2

* The Kansas City Royals defeated the Baltimore Orioles 8-2 in Game 3 of the American League Championship Series.
* The win gave the Royals a 2-1 lead in the series.

## China's Xi Jinping Meets with Japanese PM Shinzo Abe: Tensions Ease

* Chinese President Xi Jinping met with Japanese Prime Minister Shinzo Abe, marking a rare high-level meeting between the two nations.
* The meeting helped to ease tensions between the two countries over territorial disputes and historical issues.

## FIFA Corruption Scandal: Sepp Blatter's Re-Election Challenged

* The re-election of FIFA President Sepp Blatter was challenged due to allegations of corruption within the organization.
* The scandal led to a major overhaul of FIFA leadership and governance structures.
