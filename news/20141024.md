Important Events for 2014-10-24:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on October 24, 2014:

# Global Events
October 24, 2014

* **Ukraine conflict**: Pro-Russian separatists in eastern Ukraine captured three Ukrainian border guards and accused them of being spies. [1]
* **Ebola outbreak**: The World Health Organization (WHO) reported that the Ebola virus had spread to a total of six countries: Guinea, Liberia, Sierra Leone, Senegal, Mali, and the United States. [2]
* **Climate change**: A UN report warned that global warming could cause catastrophic sea-level rises by 2050 if greenhouse gas emissions continue to rise. [3]
* **Syria war**: The US and its allies launched airstrikes against ISIS targets in Syria, amid concerns over the terrorist group's territorial gains. [4]
