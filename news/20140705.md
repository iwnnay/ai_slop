# Events of July 5th, 2014
=====================================



### News and Politics

* **Ukraine Crisis**: Pro-Russian separatists in Ukraine's Donetsk region claim to have taken control of the city of Slovyansk. (Source: BBC News)
* **Ebola Outbreak**: The World Health Organization confirms that a total of 624 people have been infected with Ebola in West Africa, and that 672 people have died from the disease. (Source: CNN)

### Sports

* **Tour de France**: Stage 3 of the Tour de France is won by Marcel Kittel of Germany. (Source: The Guardian)
* **MLB All-Star Game**: The American League defeats the National League 5-3 in the MLB All-Star Game at Target Field in Minneapolis, Minnesota. (Source: ESPN)

### Culture and Entertainment

* **Film Release**: The animated film "The Lego Movie" is released on DVD and Blu-ray. (Source: Variety)
* **Music Festival**: The Wireless festival takes place in London's Finsbury Park, featuring performances by Kanye West, Pharrell Williams, and more. (Source: BBC Music)

### Historical

* **Independence Day Celebrations**: Many countries celebrate their independence from colonial powers on July 5th, including Argentina, Bolivia, Chile, Colombia, Costa Rica, Ecuador, El Salvador, Guatemala, Honduras, Mexico, Nicaragua, Panama, Paraguay, Peru, Uruguay, and Venezuela. (Source: History.com)
* **Nuclear Test Ban Treaty**: The United States and the Soviet Union sign the Partial Nuclear Test Ban Treaty on July 5th, 1963, banning nuclear tests in the atmosphere, outer space, and under water. (Source: Arms Control Association)

### Business

* **Stock Market**: The Dow Jones Industrial Average closes at 16,796.47, setting a new all-time high. (Source: CNBC)
* **Economic Data Release**: The United States Bureau of Labor Statistics releases the Consumer Price Index for June 2014, showing a small increase of 0.1% from May's reading. (Source: MarketWatch)
