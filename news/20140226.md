Important Events for 2014-02-26:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on February 26, 2014:

# Events of February 26, 2014
=====================================================



1. **Malaysia Airlines Flight 370 Disappearance**: Malaysia Airlines Flight MH370, carrying 239 people, disappears while en route from Kuala Lumpur to Beijing. The cause of the disappearance and subsequent crash is still unknown.

2. **Winter Olympics in Sochi Open**: The XXII Olympic Winter Games open in Sochi, Russia, with a ceremony featuring Russian pop star Philipp Kirkorov.

3. **Egyptian President Hosni Mubarak Dies**: Former Egyptian President Hosni Mubarak dies at the age of 86, after serving as president from 1981 to 2011.

4. **Australian Open Tennis Tournament Begins**: The Australian Open, one of the four Grand Slam tennis tournaments, begins in Melbourne, Australia.

5. **NASA's Orion Spacecraft Completes Test Flight**: NASA's Orion spacecraft completes its first test flight, known as EFT-1 (Exploration Flight Test-1), reaching an altitude of 3,600 meters and a maximum speed of 4,000 kilometers per hour.

6. **Russian President Vladimir Putin Visits Ukraine**: Russian President Vladimir Putin visits Ukraine for talks with Ukrainian leaders amid rising tensions over the situation in eastern Ukraine.

7. **European Union Extends Sanctions on Russia**: The European Union extends sanctions on Russian individuals and companies in response to the crisis in Ukraine.

8. **Astronauts Perform Spacewalk at International Space Station**: Astronauts Chris Cassidy, Tom Marshburn, and Mike Hopkins perform a spacewalk at the International Space Station to upgrade the station's solar panels.

9. **Pope Francis Meets with Ukrainian Leaders**: Pope Francis meets with leaders from Ukraine, including President Viktor Yanukovych and opposition leader Yulia Tymoshenko, to discuss the situation in Ukraine.

10. **New Zealand Signs Climate Change Agreement**: New Zealand signs a climate change agreement with the United States, Canada, Australia, and the Pacific Islands to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and mitigate the effects of climate change.
