# May 5th, 2014: 10 Major Global Events
================================================================

### 1. **Buddhist Holy Day**: Vesak Day Celebrations Across Asia

Millions of Buddhists around the world celebrated Vesak Day, a holy day that marks the birth, enlightenment, and death of Gautama Buddha. The day is observed on the full moon day of May (Vesak) in Buddhist calendar.

### 2. **Nobel Prize Winner**: James Rothman's Autobiography Released

James E. Rothman, an American neurobiologist and Nobel laureate, released his autobiography, "Partly Cloudy: Learning to be a Scientist". The book provides insight into his life and research, including the discovery of vesicle-mediated protein transport.

### 3. **Sports**: Real Madrid Wins La Liga Title

Real Madrid won their 32nd La Liga title after defeating Granada 4-0 in their final match of the season. This marked their third consecutive league championship under coach Carlo Ancelotti.

### 4. **Space Exploration**: NASA's Orion Spacecraft Module Assembled

NASA completed the assembly of its Orion spacecraft module, designed for deep space missions. The capsule will carry astronauts to destinations like Mars and beyond.

### 5. **Environmental Crisis**: Massive Oil Spill in South Dakota

A major oil spill occurred on May 5th in North Dakota's Bakken region, contaminating a significant area of land and water. The incident highlighted concerns over the environmental impact of unconventional oil production.

### 6. **International Relations**: Japan-US Trade Deal Announced

Japan and the United States announced a trade deal aimed at reducing tariffs and increasing trade between the two nations. The agreement was seen as a major step in strengthening economic ties between Japan and the US.

### 7. **Food Security**: UN Launches Global Food Security Initiative

The United Nations launched its global food security initiative, aiming to address hunger, malnutrition, and sustainable agriculture practices worldwide. The program aimed to achieve Zero Hunger by 2030.

### 8. **Technology**: Amazon Acquires Twitch for $970 Million

Amazon acquired the live-streaming platform Twitch Interactive for $970 million. This significant deal marked a major expansion of Amazon's presence in the gaming and e-sports industries.

### 9. **Healthcare**: FDA Approves New Cancer Treatment

The US Food and Drug Administration (FDA) approved a new cancer treatment, pembrolizumab, developed by Merck & Co. The drug targets advanced melanoma patients with specific genetic mutations.

### 10. **Education**: UNESCO Releases Global Education Report

The United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization (UNESCO) released its global education report, highlighting the state of education worldwide. The report emphasized the need for improved access to quality education, especially in developing countries.

These events represent significant news, sports, cultural, and historical developments that occurred on May 5th, 2014.
