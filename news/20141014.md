# October 14th, 2014: A Day of Significance
=====================================================

### 1. **Ebola Outbreak Continues to Spread**

The Ebola outbreak in West Africa continues to spread, with cases reported in Sierra Leone, Guinea, Liberia, and Nigeria. The World Health Organization (WHO) reports over 7,000 cases and nearly 3,000 deaths.

### 2. **Ferguson Unrest Erupts**

Protests erupt in Ferguson, Missouri, following the announcement that Officer Darren Wilson would not be charged for the shooting death of Michael Brown, a black teenager. The unrest leads to clashes between protesters and police, with multiple injuries reported.

### 3. **Nobel Peace Prize Awarded**

The Nobel Committee awards the Nobel Peace Prize to Malala Yousafzai and Kailash Satyarthi for their struggle against the suppression of children and young people and for the right of all children to education.

### 4. **Russia Warns Ukraine on Border**

Russian President Vladimir Putin warns Ukraine that any military action against pro-Russian separatists in eastern Ukraine would be met with "all necessary measures" from Russia, escalating tensions between the two nations.

### 5. **European Union Condemns Israel's Settlement Plans**

The European Union condemns Israel's plan to build thousands of new homes in East Jerusalem and the West Bank, saying it undermines efforts to reach a Palestinian state.

### 6. **Indian Supreme Court Strikes Down Gay Sex Ban**

India's Supreme Court strikes down a colonial-era ban on gay sex, effectively decriminalizing homosexuality in the country.

### 7. **NASA's Maven Spacecraft Arrives at Mars**

The NASA spacecraft Maven arrives at Mars, beginning its mission to study the planet's upper atmosphere and magnetic field.

### 8. **Hurricane Gonzalo Batters Bermuda**

Hurricane Gonzalo makes landfall on Bermuda, causing widespread damage and power outages across the island.

### 9. **Apple Unveils New iPhones**

Apple unveils new iPhone models, including the iPhone 6 and iPhone 6 Plus, featuring improved cameras and larger screens.

### 10. **World Series Begins**

The San Francisco Giants face off against the Kansas City Royals in Game 1 of the World Series, with Madison Bumgarner pitching a complete game for the Giants.

These events highlight significant news, sports, cultural, and historical occurrences that took place on October 14th, 2014.
