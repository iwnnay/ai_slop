Important Events for 2014-07-31:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on July 31, 2014:

**July 31, 2014**
=====================

### News and Politics

* **Russia-UEFA Agreement**: Russia's sports minister, Vitaly Mutko, announced an agreement with UEFA (Union of European Football Associations) to allow the Russian national football team to play their home games in Kazan, rather than Moscow.
* **Greek Parliament Elects New President**: Greece's parliament elected Prokopis Pavlopoulos as the country's new president, replacing Karolos Papoulias.

### Sports

* **Tour de France Stage 20**: The final stage of the Tour de France saw Vincenzo Nibali (Astana) win the overall title, his second consecutive victory.
* **US Open Tennis**: In women's tennis, Serena Williams defeated Angelique Kerber to reach the quarterfinals.

### Culture and Entertainment

* **Comic-Con International: San Diego**: The annual comic book convention kicked off in San Diego, featuring panels, screenings, and appearances by celebrities like Stan Lee and Benedict Cumberbatch.
* **VMA Awards Nominees Announced**: MTV announced the nominees for the 2014 Video Music Awards, with Iggy Azalea leading the pack.

### History

* **NASA's LADEE Lunar Impact**: NASA's LADEE (Lunar Atmosphere and Dust Environment Explorer) spacecraft impacted the Moon's surface after completing its mission to study the lunar atmosphere.
* **The Great Barrier Reef Turns 25**: The Australian government celebrated the 25th anniversary of the World Heritage listing for the Great Barrier Reef, one of the world's most biologically diverse ecosystems.

These events took place on July 31, 2014.
