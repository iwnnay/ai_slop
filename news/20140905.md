Important Events for 2014-09-05:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on September 5th, 2014:

**September 5, 2014**

### News and Politics

1. **Ukraine crisis**: Russian President Vladimir Putin announced that Russia would be withdrawing its troops from Ukraine's border, easing tensions in the region. (Source: BBC)
2. **Ebola outbreak**: The World Health Organization (WHO) reported a significant increase in Ebola cases, with 2,615 confirmed cases and 1,427 deaths in West Africa. (Source: WHO)

### Sports

3. **NFL**: The Chicago Bears defeated the San Francisco 49ers 28-17 in an NFL preseason game.
4. **US Open**: In tennis, Marin Čilić won the men's singles title at the US Open, defeating Kei Nishikori in four sets.

### Entertainment and Culture
