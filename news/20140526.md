Here are 10 major global events that occurred on May 26, 2014:

# **May 26, 2014 Events**

## **1.** [**Australian Federal Election**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Australian_federal_election,_2013)
The Australian federal election was held to elect members of the Australian Parliament. The Liberal Party of Australia, led by Tony Abbott, won a majority government.

## **2.** [**European Union Referendum in Ireland**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/European_Union_referendum,_2014_(Ireland))
A referendum was held in Ireland on whether to approve the European Union's fiscal treaty. The treaty was approved with 60.3% of voters supporting it.

## **3.** [**NBA Playoffs: Spurs defeat Thunder in Game 6**](https://www.nba.com/games/20140526/GM6OKCvsSAS/)
The San Antonio Spurs defeated the Oklahoma City Thunder 112-102 in Game 6 of their Western Conference semifinal series.

## **4.** [**Pope Francis's Apostolic Exhortation on Family Life**](https://www.vaticannews.va/en/news/2014-05-26/pope-francis-issues-apostolic-exhortation-on-family-life.html)
Pope Francis issued an apostolic exhortation, "The Joy of Love" (Amoris Laetitia), which explored the Catholic Church's teaching on family life and marriage.

## **5.** [**Malaysian Airlines Flight 370 Search Efforts Continue**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Malaysia_Airlines_Flight_370)
Search efforts for Malaysia Airlines Flight 370, which went missing in March 2014, continued with no signs of the plane's wreckage being found.

## **6.** [**UN Security Council Condemns Ukraine Violence**](https://www.un.org/apps/news/story.asp?NewsID=47813&CrUkraine&Cr1=)
The United Nations Security Council condemned violence in Ukraine and called for a peaceful resolution to the crisis.

## **7.** [**Indian Politician Jayalalithaa Dies at 68**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/J._Jayalalithaa)
J. Jayalalithaa, the Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu, India, died at the age of 68 after a prolonged illness.

## **8.** [**FIFA World Cup Qualifying Matches**](https://www.fifa.com/worldcup/matches/index.html)
Various FIFA World Cup qualifying matches took place on May 26, 2014, with teams like Brazil, Spain, and Germany securing victories.

## **9.** [**Russian President Vladimir Putin's Meeting with Ukrainian Leader Oleksandr Turchynov**](https://www.reuters.com/article/2014/05/26/russia-ukraine-idUSL1N0OQ0WZ20140526)
Russian President Vladimir Putin met with Ukrainian acting President Oleksandr Turchynov to discuss the ongoing crisis in Ukraine.

## **10.** [**NASA's Kepler Space Telescope Runs Out of Fuel**](https://www.nasa.gov/press-release/nasa-kepler-space-telescope-reaches-end-of-mission/)
The NASA Kepler space telescope, which had been monitoring exoplanets since 2009, ran out of fuel and ceased operations.
