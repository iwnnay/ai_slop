Important Events for 2014-11-24:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on November 24, 2014:

### Global Events

#### Ebola Outbreak Update
The World Health Organization (WHO) reported that the number of new Ebola cases in West Africa had declined for the first time since the outbreak began.

#### US-China Climate Agreement
US Secretary of State John Kerry and Chinese Foreign Minister Wang Yi announced a joint agreement to combat climate change, with both countries committing to reduce greenhouse gas emissions.

#### Ukrainian Election
Ukraine held presidential elections, with Petro Poroshenko winning in the first round with 54% of the vote.

#### Gaza Ceasefire
Israel and Hamas agreed to a ceasefire after seven weeks of conflict, resulting in significant humanitarian crisis.

#### European Space Agency's Gaia Mission
The European Space Agency (ESA) launched its Gaia mission, aiming to create a 3D map of the Milky Way galaxy using data from over one million stars.

#### South Korea Ferry Sinking
The Sewol ferry sank off the coast of South Korea, killing 304 people, mostly high school students.
