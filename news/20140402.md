Important Events for 2014-04-02:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on April 2, 2014:

# Event 1: Ukraine Crisis
On April 2, 2014, Ukrainian President Viktor Yanukovych fled Kiev after weeks of protests and violence. This marked the end of his presidency.

# Event 2: Boston Marathon Bombing Verdict
On April 2, 2014, Dzhokhar Tsarnaev was found guilty on all 30 counts related to the Boston Marathon bombing that killed three people and injured hundreds in 2013.

# Event 3: Malaysia Airlines Flight 370 Investigation
On April 2, 2014, Malaysian authorities officially concluded their investigation into the disappearance of Flight MH370, which went missing on March 8, 2014. The cause of the disappearance was still unknown at this point.

# Event 4: Syria Crisis
On April 2, 2014, UN Secretary-General Ban Ki-moon condemned the Syrian government's use of chemical weapons in a recent attack that killed hundreds of civilians.

# Event 5: US Federal Budget Cuts
On April 2, 2014, the United States Congress passed a budget deal that reduced federal spending by $85 billion. This marked the third year of sequestration cuts since 2011.

# Event 6: European Parliament Elections
On April 2-25, 2014, elections for the European Parliament took place across the EU. The results saw the European People's Party (EPP) emerge as the largest group.

# Event 7: Chinese Vice President Xi Jinping Visit to US
On April 2, 2014, Chinese Vice President Xi Jinping arrived in the United States for a state visit aimed at strengthening ties between the two nations.

# Event 8: South Korea Ferry Sinking
On April 2, 2014, the Sewol ferry sank off the coast of South Korea, killing over 300 people and injuring many more. This disaster was one of the deadliest in South Korean history.

# Event 9: NBA Playoffs
On April 2, 2014, the NBA playoffs began with several teams securing their positions in the post-season. The Golden State Warriors, Chicago Bulls, and Portland Trail Blazers were among the teams that started their playoff runs.

# Event 10: New York City St. Patrick's Day Parade
On April 2, 2014, the annual New York City St. Patrick's Day Parade took place, featuring floats, marching bands, and thousands of participants in green attire. The parade was a celebration of Irish heritage in the city.
