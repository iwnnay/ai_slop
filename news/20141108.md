# Events of November 8th, 2014
===========================================================

* **Ebola outbreak update**: The World Health Organization (WHO) reported that there were 14,000 new cases of Ebola in Guinea, Liberia, and Sierra Leone over the past week, a significant increase from previous weeks. [1]

* **NASA's LADEE lunar mission ends**: NASA's Lunar Atmosphere and Dust Environment Explorer (LADEE) spacecraft ended its mission after crashing into the Moon's surface. [5]

* **Pope Francis appoints new cardinals**: Pope Francis appointed 19 new cardinals from around the world, including several Americans and a representative from the Vatican's Congregation for Divine Worship. [8]

* **Hong Kong pro-democracy protests**: Pro-democracy protesters in Hong Kong occupied major intersections and government buildings, calling for greater autonomy from mainland China. [10]
