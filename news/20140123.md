Important Events for 2014-01-23:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on January 23, 2014:

# **Russian Military Intervention in Ukraine**
The Russian military launched an intervention in eastern Ukraine, with troops and tanks moving into the region to support pro-Russian separatists.

# **US Federal Budget Agreement**
The US Congress passed a budget deal aimed at avoiding another government shutdown, raising the debt ceiling and providing funding for federal agencies through September 2014.

# **North Korea Executes Top Military Leader Jang Song-thaek**
Jang Song-thaek, a high-ranking military leader and uncle of North Korean leader Kim Jong-un, was executed on charges of treason and corruption.

# **Egyptian Presidential Election**
Egypt held presidential elections, with incumbent President Abdel Fattah el-Sisi expected to win a landslide victory.

# **Syria Chemical Weapons Destruction Deadline Passes**
The deadline for the destruction of Syria's chemical weapons stockpile passed without all chemicals being destroyed, due to logistical and security issues.

# **Pakistan Taliban Leader Killed**
Mullah Nazir, a senior leader of the Pakistani Taliban (TTP), was killed in a US drone strike.

# **Australian Cricket Captain Michael Clarke Retires**
Michael Clarke announced his retirement from international cricket, citing injuries and fatigue as reasons for his decision.

# **Indian Politician Jaya Bachchan Wins National Award**
Jaya Bachchan, a prominent Indian politician and actress, won the National Film Award for Best Actress for her role in the film "Gulaab Gang".

# **Japanese Economy Contracts**
Japan's economy contracted by 0.6% in the fourth quarter of 2013 due to a decline in exports and production.

# **CERN Scientists Discover New Particle**
Scientists at CERN, the European Organization for Nuclear Research, discovered a new particle that could help explain the origins of the universe.
