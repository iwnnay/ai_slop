Here are 10 major global events that occurred on February 14th, 2014:

# **February 14, 2014**
### Major Global Events

#### 1. Valentine's Day Attacks in Pakistan
On this day in 2014, multiple bomb attacks targeted Christians and couples celebrating Valentine's Day in Pakistan, killing at least 7 people and injuring over 70 others.

#### 2. Russia's Anti-Gay Law Enforcement Begins
In response to international pressure, Russia began enforcing its controversial anti-gay "propaganda" law, criminalizing any public display of same-sex relationships or LGBTQ+ advocacy.

#### 3. North Korea Conducts Third Nuclear Test
North Korea announced that it had conducted its third nuclear test, sparking international condemnation and increased tensions on the Korean Peninsula.

#### 4. Ukrainian Protests Intensify Against Yanukovych
As Ukraine's political crisis deepened, protests intensified against President Viktor Yanukovych, who would eventually flee to Russia in February 2014.

#### 5. Pope Francis Visits the Philippines
Pope Francis embarked on a five-day visit to the Philippines, meeting with survivors of Typhoon Haiyan and advocating for social justice.

#### 6. NBA Trade Deadline
The 2014 NBA trade deadline saw significant player transactions, including deals involving Kevin Love (Minnesota Timberwolves), Pau Gasol (Los Angeles Lakers), and Rajon Rondo (Boston Celtics).

#### 7. Egyptian President Morsi Sentenced to Death
Former Egyptian President Mohamed Morsi was sentenced to death for his role in the deaths of protesters during his rule.

#### 8. French Presidential Election Primary Results Announced
The first round of the French presidential election primary saw Alain Juppé and François Fillon emerge as top contenders, setting the stage for a contentious campaign.

#### 9. European Parliament Approves EU-Ukraine Association Agreement
The European Parliament approved the EU-Ukraine Association Agreement, further solidifying Ukraine's ties with the European Union amidst growing tensions with Russia.

#### 10. NASA's Dawn Spacecraft Enters Ceres Orbit
NASA's Dawn spacecraft entered into orbit around dwarf planet Ceres, providing unprecedented insights into the asteroid belt's largest object and a potential candidate for future human exploration.
