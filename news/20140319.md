Here are 10 major global events that occurred on March 19, 2014:

# March 19, 2014: A Day of Global Significance
=====================================================

1. **Sarajevo Hostage Crisis**: A gunman took several people hostage at the Sarajevo City Hall in Bosnia and Herzegovina. The standoff was eventually resolved after the police negotiated with the hostage-taker and freed the hostages.
2. **Ebola Outbreak**: The World Health Organization (WHO) confirmed that a total of 158 cases of Ebola had been reported in Guinea, Liberia, and Sierra Leone, including 108 deaths. This was during the early stages of the outbreak which would eventually spread to other countries and become one of the deadliest in history.
3. **Crisis in Ukraine**: Pro-Russian protests continued in Ukraine's Crimean Peninsula as Russia tightened its grip on the region. The Ukrainian government declared that Crimea had been "occupied" by Russian forces.
4. **Syrian Civil War**: Rebel groups announced a new alliance to fight against President Bashar al-Assad's regime, amid reports of intense fighting and airstrikes around the city of Aleppo.
5. **NHL Playoffs**: The Anaheim Ducks defeated the Los Angeles Kings 2-1 in Game 3 of their Western Conference Quarterfinals series, taking a 2-1 lead in the best-of-seven matchup.
6. **Tennis**: Novak Djokovic won his sixth Indian Wells Masters title by defeating Roger Federer 3-6, 6-3, 7-6 (7-4) in the men's singles final.
7. **Egyptian Politics**: The Egyptian military-backed government announced a state of emergency and imposed curfews in several cities after violent clashes between protesters and security forces left dozens dead or injured.
8. **Space Exploration**: NASA's Kepler space telescope, which had discovered thousands of exoplanets during its nine-year mission, was declared officially retired and would no longer continue to search for new worlds.
9. **Economic News**: The International Monetary Fund (IMF) approved a $490 million loan package for Ukraine to help the country recover from economic crisis and stabilize its financial system.
10. **Environmental Concerns**: The European Union's highest court ruled that the EU had the power to regulate the use of neonicotinoid pesticides, which have been linked to bee deaths and environmental damage.

These events give you an idea of some of the significant news stories that took place on March 19, 2014.
