Here are 10 major global events that occurred on August 22, 2014:

# Events of August 22, 2014
==========================

### 1. Israel-Hamas Ceasefire Takes Effect

A ceasefire between Israel and Hamas came into effect at 8am local time (5:00 UTC), ending seven weeks of intense fighting in the Gaza Strip.

### 2. Michael Brown Shooting Investigation Continues

The investigation into the fatal shooting of unarmed black teenager Michael Brown by a white police officer in Ferguson, Missouri continued, sparking protests and calls for justice.

### 3. Malaysia Airlines Flight MH17 Crash Investigation Finds No Evidence of Missile Strike

Investigations into the crash of Malaysia Airlines Flight MH17 found no evidence of a missile strike, contradicting earlier claims that a Russian-made Buk missile brought down the plane.

### 4. World Bank President Jim Yong Kim Reiterates Support for Climate Change Action

World Bank President Jim Yong Kim reiterated his support for urgent climate change action, urging world leaders to take bold steps to address the issue.

### 5. Indian PM Narendra Modi Pays Tribute to Mahatma Gandhi on His 145th Birth Anniversary

Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi paid tribute to Mahatma Gandhi on his 145th birth anniversary, highlighting the importance of non-violent resistance and social justice.

### 6. Scottish Independence Referendum: No to Independence, Yes to Devolution

The Scottish independence referendum resulted in a narrow "no" vote for independence, but with a majority in favor of devolving more powers to Scotland from the UK government.

### 7. FIFA World Cup Qualifying Match: Argentina Beats Peru 2-1

In a crucial FIFA World Cup qualifying match, Argentina defeated Peru 2-1 to maintain their chances of qualifying for the tournament.

### 8. NASA's New Horizons Spacecraft Flies By Pluto

NASA's New Horizons spacecraft flew by Pluto at approximately 7:49am EDT (11:49 UTC), providing stunning images and new insights into the dwarf planet.

### 9. Apple Acquires Beats Electronics for $3 Billion

Tech giant Apple acquired headphones maker Beats Electronics for $3 billion, marking one of the largest acquisitions in the company's history.

### 10. Hong Kong Pro-Democracy Protesters March Against Chinese Government Intervention

Pro-democracy protesters in Hong Kong marched against Chinese government intervention in the territory's political system, demanding greater autonomy and human rights protections.

These are just a few of the significant global events that occurred on August 22, 2014.
