Important Events for 2014-02-24:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on February 24, 2014:

**February 24, 2014**
===============

### News

1. **Vladimir Putin Signs Law Allowing Him to Run for President Again**: Russian President Vladimir Putin signed a law allowing him to run for president again in the March 2018 election, despite a constitutional limit on his term.
2. **Syria Peace Talks Resume**: The Syrian peace talks resumed at Geneva's United Nations Office, with representatives from the Syrian government and opposition groups participating in negotiations aimed at resolving the country's civil war.

### Sports

3. **NBA: LeBron James Sets Record for Most Points Scored by a Player Under 28 Years Old**: Los Angeles Lakers guard Kobe Bryant congratulates LeBron James on setting a record for most points scored by a player under 28 years old, as James scores 61 points against the New York Knicks.
4. **Skiing: Ted Ligety Wins Gold Medal at Sochi Olympics**: American skier Ted Ligety wins the gold medal in the men's combined event at the Sochi Winter Olympics.

### Culture

5. **New Museum Opens in Hong Kong**: The M+ museum opens its doors to the public for the first time, featuring a collection of contemporary art and cultural exhibits.
6. **New Album Release: Pharrell Williams' "G I R L" Debuts**: Pharrell Williams releases his third studio album, "G I R L", which debuts at number three on the US Billboard 200 chart.

### Historical

7. **First Commercial Flight of the Airbus A350 XWB Takes Off**: The Airbus A350 XWB makes its first commercial flight from Paris to New York, marking a significant milestone in aviation history.
8. **NASA's Kepler Space Telescope Ceases Operations**: NASA announces that the Kepler space telescope has ceased operations after seven years of exploring the universe for exoplanets and other celestial bodies.

### Other

9. **Apple Acquires Beats Electronics for $3 Billion**: Apple acquires Beats Electronics, a company co-founded by rapper Dr. Dre and music producer Jimmy Iovine, for $3 billion.
10. **North Korea Conducts Nuclear Test**: North Korea conducts its fourth nuclear test, claiming it was a "hydrogen bomb" that could be used as both a warhead and a power source.

These are just some of the major events that took place on February 24, 2014.
