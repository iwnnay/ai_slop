Important Events for 2014-08-07:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on August 7, 2014:

# Event 1: Israel-Hamas Ceasefire Announced
A ceasefire agreement was announced between Israeli forces and Hamas militants in the Gaza Strip, bringing an end to a 50-day conflict that had killed over 2,000 people.

# Event 2: Ukraine Crisis Escalates
Pro-Russian separatists launched a surprise attack on Ukrainian troops in eastern Ukraine, killing at least 17 people. The incident marked a significant escalation of violence in the region.

# Event 3: Scotland Votes to Stay in the UK
In a historic referendum, Scottish voters chose to remain part of the United Kingdom rather than becoming an independent nation. The "No" campaign won by a margin of 55.3% to 44.7%.

# Event 4: Malaysia Airlines Flight MH370 Search Efforts Continue
Malaysian authorities continued their search efforts for missing flight MH370, which disappeared on March 8, 2014. Despite extensive searches, no wreckage or bodies had been found.

# Event 5: Russian President Vladimir Putin Visits Ukraine
Russian President Vladimir Putin visited Ukraine's capital city of Kiev to meet with Ukrainian leaders and discuss the ongoing crisis in eastern Ukraine.

# Event 6: China Launches First Female Astronaut into Space
China launched its first female astronaut, Wang Yaping, into space aboard the Shenzhou 11 spacecraft. The mission aimed to spend 30 days conducting scientific experiments on board the International Space Station.

# Event 7: US Open Golf Tournament Concludes
The 114th edition of the US Open golf tournament concluded at the Pine Valley Golf Club in New Jersey. Martin Kaymer won his first major title by a single stroke over Erik Compton.

# Event 8: Ebola Outbreak Spreads to Sierra Leone
Sierra Leone confirmed its first case of Ebola, bringing the total number of countries affected by the outbreak to four. The World Health Organization (WHO) warned that the virus could spread rapidly if not contained.

# Event 9: FIFA World Cup Qualifying Matches Conclude
The second round of qualifying matches for the 2018 FIFA World Cup concluded, with many teams securing their places in the tournament.

# Event 10: NASA's Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter Discovers Evidence of Water on Mars
NASA's Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter (MRO) provided new evidence that liquid water once flowed on Mars. The discovery had significant implications for the search for life beyond Earth.
