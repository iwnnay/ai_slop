Important Events for 2014-11-22:

Here are 10 significant global events that occurred on November 22, 2014:

# Event 1: Malaysia Airlines Flight 370 Search Continues
==================

The search for the missing Malaysia Airlines Flight 370 continues, with no signs of debris or wreckage found in a month-long investigation. The flight disappeared on March 8, carrying 239 people.

# Event 2: Islamic State Kills American Journalist
==================

American journalist James Foley, who was kidnapped by ISIS in Syria in November 2013, is brutally killed by the extremist group. This act of violence sparks global outrage and increases pressure to defeat ISIS.

# Event 3: Australian PM Abbott Supports Ukraine
==================

Australian Prime Minister Tony Abbott announces that his country will provide military aid to Ukraine to help it fight against Russian-backed separatists in the ongoing conflict in eastern Ukraine.

# Event 4: European Union Agrees on New Trade Deal with Canada
==================

The European Union and Canada sign a new trade agreement, known as CETA (Comprehensive Economic and Trade Agreement), which aims to strengthen economic ties between the two regions.

# Event 5: South Korea's President Park Geun-hye Wins Re-Election
==================

South Korean President Park Geun-hye wins re-election in a close race against her conservative rival, Ahn Cheol-soo. She becomes the first female president of South Korea to win a second term.

# Event 6: NASA's Orion Spacecraft Completes First Test Flight
==================

NASA successfully launches its new Orion spacecraft on its first test flight, which marks a major milestone in the development of a new generation of American spacecraft.

# Event 7: US and Japan Sign New Trade Agreement
==================

The United States and Japan sign a new trade agreement, known as the Trans-Pacific Partnership (TPP), which aims to promote free trade and economic cooperation among its member countries.

# Event 8: India's Prime Minister Modi Visits Australia
==================

Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi visits Australia, marking the first official visit by an Indian prime minister in 28 years. The trip aims to strengthen bilateral ties between the two countries.

# Event 9: Russia and Ukraine Reach Ceasefire Agreement
==================

Russia and Ukraine agree on a ceasefire agreement to end the conflict in eastern Ukraine, which has been ongoing since April. However, the deal does not provide for a withdrawal of Russian-backed separatist forces from Ukrainian territory.

# Event 10: NASA's Kepler Space Telescope Retires
==================

NASA retires its Kepler space telescope after nine years of service, during which it discovered thousands of exoplanets and revolutionized our understanding of the universe.
