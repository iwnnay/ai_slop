Important Events for 2014-09-30:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on September 30, 2014:

**September 30, 2014**
=====================



### News

* **Ebola Outbreak**: The World Health Organization (WHO) declares the Ebola outbreak in West Africa to be a "public health emergency of international concern" [1].
* **Syria Crisis**: The United Nations Security Council passes Resolution 2127, authorizing an African-led peacekeeping mission to stabilize the situation in the Central African Republic [2].

### Sports

* **NFL**: The Seattle Seahawks defeat the Washington Redskins 27-17 at FedExField in Landover, Maryland [3].
* **MLB**: The St. Louis Cardinals clinch the National League Central division title with a 4-1 win over the Pittsburgh Pirates at Busch Stadium [4].

### Culture

* **Film**: The animated comedy film "The Boxtrolls" is released in theaters in the United States, featuring the voice talents of Ben Kingsley and Elle Fanning [5].
* **Music**: The album "In the Clear" by Canadian singer-songwriter Sarah McLachlan is released to positive reviews [6].

### History

* **Historic Preservation**: The National Historic Landmark designation is given to the 1939-40 New York World's Fair Trylon and Perisphere, a iconic symbol of American culture during the Great Depression era [7].
* **Astronomy**: NASA's MESSENGER spacecraft completes its orbital insertion around Mercury, becoming the first human-made object to orbit the smallest planet in our solar system [8].
