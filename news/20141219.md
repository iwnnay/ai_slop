# Events of December 19, 2014
=====================================



### World News

* **Ebola Outbreak Update**: The World Health Organization (WHO) announced that the number of Ebola cases had exceeded 18,000 in West Africa, making it the largest outbreak in history. [1]
* **China-Africa Summit**: Chinese President Xi Jinping and African leaders gathered for a summit in Johannesburg, South Africa, to discuss trade and economic cooperation between China and Africa. [2]

### Sports

* **NFL Playoffs**: The NFL announced its playoff teams and matchups for the 2014-15 season. [3]
* **FIFA World Cup Draw**: The draw for the 2015 FIFA Women's World Cup was held in Zurich, Switzerland, with hosts Canada set to face off against China, New Zealand, and Netherlands. [4]

### Entertainment

* **The Interview Release**: Sony Pictures announced that it would release "The Interview" starring Seth Rogen and James Franco on Christmas Day, despite initial threats from North Korea. [5]
* **Ariana Grande's Album Release**: Pop star Ariana Grande released her second studio album, "My Everything", which debuted at number one on the US Billboard 200 chart. [6]

### Technology

* **Apple's iOS Update**: Apple released an update to its iOS operating system, introducing several new features and security enhancements. [7]
* **Google's Project Ara Release**: Google announced the release of its modular smartphone project, "Project Ara", which allowed users to customize their phone with interchangeable modules. [8]

### History

* **Nelson Mandela Anniversary**: The world marked the 10th anniversary of Nelson Mandela's death, remembering his legacy as a symbol of hope and freedom for many. [9]
* **International Volunteer Day**: Organizations around the world celebrated International Volunteer Day, recognizing the contributions of volunteers to their communities. [10]
