### January 3rd, 2014: Top 10 Global Events

#### 1. **Sochi Olympics Opening Ceremony**

#### 2. **Nelson Mandela Memorial Service**

A memorial service was held for Nelson Mandela at FNB Stadium in Johannesburg, South Africa, attended by world leaders, including Barack Obama, Bill Clinton, and Jacob Zuma.

The Syrian government launched a major offensive against rebel-held areas in Aleppo, killing scores of civilians and sparking widespread international condemnation.

#### 4. **North Korea's Nuclear Ambitions**

North Korean leader Kim Jong-un vowed to continue developing the country's nuclear program, despite increased sanctions and diplomatic pressure from the international community.

#### 5. **South African Parliament Fire**

A fire ravaged the South African Parliament building in Cape Town, causing significant damage and disrupting legislative proceedings.

#### 6. **Russian Protest Crackdown**

Russian authorities arrested over 200 protesters who were demanding greater government accountability and an end to police brutality in Moscow.

#### 7. **Indian Coal Mine Collapse**

A coal mine collapse in India's eastern state of Odisha killed at least 18 workers, highlighting concerns about labor safety and regulations.

#### 8. **US Drone Strike Kills Taliban Leader**

A US drone strike in Afghanistan killed Maulvi Abdul Rauf, a senior Taliban leader, marking a significant blow to the terrorist organization.

#### 9. **Egyptian Military Crackdown**

Egypt's military launched a crackdown on Muslim Brotherhood supporters, killing at least 50 people and wounding hundreds more in Cairo.

#### 10. **China's Urbanization Drive**

Chinese Premier Li Keqiang announced plans to accelerate urbanization and economic development, aiming to shift the country's focus from export-led growth to domestic consumption.
