Important Events for 2014-01-01:

January 1st, 2014 was a notable day with several significant events around the world. Here are 3-5 major events that took place:

1. **New Year's Day Honors**: In the United Kingdom, Prince William and Kate Middleton (Duke and Duchess of Cambridge) attended a New Year's Day service at Westminster Abbey in London to mark the start of the new year.

2. **Iran Nuclear Deal**: The Iranian government announced its agreement with the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) to implement an Additional Protocol, a key step towards resolving concerns over Iran's nuclear program and paving the way for economic sanctions relief.

3. **New Year's Day Fireworks Display**: In the United Arab Emirates, Dubai hosted a spectacular New Year's Eve fireworks display at the Burj Khalifa, the world's tallest building. The 5-minute show was accompanied by a concert featuring international artists.

4. **Aung San Suu Kyi Re-Liberation**: In Myanmar (Burma), opposition leader Aung San Suu Kyi was released from house arrest after seven years, marking a significant step towards democratic reforms in the country.

5. **NFL Wild Card Weekend Kicks Off**: In the United States, the National Football League (NFL) began its 2014 postseason with Wild Card Weekend games, featuring six teams competing for a spot in the Divisional Round.

These events represent some of the most notable global happenings on January 1st, 2014.
