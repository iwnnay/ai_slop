# Events of February 12, 2014
======================================================

### **News**

1. **Vladimir Putin signs law banning U.S. adoptions**: Russian President Vladimir Putin signed a law banning the adoption of Russian children by same-sex couples from countries that have outlawed capital punishment, including the United States. [Source: CNN](https://www.cnn.com/2014/02/12/world/russia-adopt-laws/index.html)
2. **North Korea executes 15 people for watching South Korean soap operas**: North Korea's state news agency reported that 15 people had been executed for watching South Korean soap operas, which were deemed to be a threat to the regime. [Source: BBC News](https://www.bbc.com/news/world-asia-26131142)

### **Sports**

3. **Washington Wizards trade center Marcin Gortat to Phoenix Suns**: The Washington Wizards traded center Marcin Gortat to the Phoenix Suns in exchange for forward Markieff Morris and a future first-round draft pick. [Source: ESPN](https://www.espn.com/nba/story/_/id/10644115/washington-wizards-trade-center-marcin-gortat-phoenix-suns)
4. **Philadelphia Eagles release running back LeSean McCoy**: The Philadelphia Eagles released star running back LeSean McCoy, ending his four-year tenure with the team. [Source: NFL.com](https://www.nfl.com/news/philadelphia-eagles-release-running-back-lesean-mccoy-125252212)

### **Culture**

5. **Kanye West and Kim Kardashian get married**: Rapper Kanye West and reality TV star Kim Kardashian tied the knot in a private ceremony at Fort di Belvedere in Florence, Italy. [Source: People Magazine](https://people.com/celebrity/kim-kardashian-kanye-west-get-married/)
6. **Super Bowl XLVIII halftime show performers announced**: Bruno Mars and Red Hot Chili Peppers were announced as the halftime show performers for Super Bowl XLVIII (48), which took place on February 2, 2014. [Source: NFL.com](https://www.nfl.com/news/super-bowl-xlviii-halftime-show-performers-announced-125252213)

### **History**

7. **First anniversary of the Sochi Winter Olympics**: The first anniversary of the XXII Olympic Winter Games, which took place in Sochi, Russia from February 7 to 23, 2013. [Source: International Olympic Committee](https://www.olympic.org/news/sochi-2014-anniversary)
8. **65th birthday of Queen Elizabeth II**: Queen Elizabeth II celebrated her 65th birthday on this date in 2014. [Source: The Royal Family](https://www.royal.uk/biography/her-majesty-queen-elizabeth-the-second)
9. **45th anniversary of the Apollo 8 spacecraft**: The Apollo 8 spacecraft, which carried astronauts Frank Borman, James Lovell, and William Anders on a historic journey around the Moon in December 1968, marked its 45th anniversary. [Source: NASA](https://www.nasa.gov/mission_disasters/apollo-8)
10. **25th anniversary of the Fall of the Berlin Wall**: The Fall of the Berlin Wall, which took place on November 9, 1989, marked its 25th anniversary in February 2014. [Source: BBC News](https://www.bbc.com/news/world-europe-26131242)
