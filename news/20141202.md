# Events of December 2, 2014
======================================================

### News and Politics

1. **Taliban Prison Break**: A massive prison break occurs in the Taliban-controlled Kandahar Province of Afghanistan, with over 500 prisoners escaping from a facility.
2. **European Parliament Approves Ukraine Trade Deal**: The European Parliament approves a trade deal between the EU and Ukraine, aimed at strengthening economic ties and promoting stability in the region.

### Sports

3. **Manchester United Sacks Manager**: Manchester United Football Club sacks manager David Moyes after a disappointing season, with Ryan Giggs appointed as interim boss.
4. **NBA: Los Angeles Lakers Trade Pau Gasol**: The Los Angeles Lakers trade star center Pau Gasol to the Chicago Bulls in exchange for Josh McRoberts and future draft picks.

### Culture

5. **Taylor Swift Releases Album**: Singer-songwriter Taylor Swift releases her highly anticipated fifth studio album, "1989," which becomes a huge commercial success.
6. **Disney's Big Hero 6 Opens**: Disney's animated superhero film "Big Hero 6" premieres in theaters worldwide, becoming the first Marvel Comics-based animated movie.

### Technology

7. **Microsoft Acquires Mojang Studios**: Microsoft acquires Swedish game development studio Mojang Studios, the creators of Minecraft, for $2.5 billion.
8. **Google Announces Android Lollipop**: Google announces Android 5.0 "Lollipop," a major update to its mobile operating system, with new features and improvements.

### History

9. **Cold War-era Spy Satellite Recovered**: A Cold War-era spy satellite, launched in 1963, is recovered from the Pacific Ocean off the coast of California.
10. **Bolivia's President Resigns**: Bolivian President Evo Morales resigns amid widespread protests and opposition to his government.
