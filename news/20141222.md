Important Events for 2014-12-22:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on December 22, 2014:

# Events of December 22, 2014
=====================================================

## News
### Russia's Anti-Gay Law Takes Effect

On this day in 2014, Russia's law prohibiting "gay propaganda" among minors took effect. The law aimed to prevent the spread of what authorities considered "non-traditional sexual relationships."

### Ukraine Crisis Escalates

Tensions continued to rise between pro-Russian separatists and Ukrainian forces in Eastern Ukraine. Clashes broke out in Donetsk and Luhansk, leaving several people dead or injured.

## Sports
### NFL Playoffs: Green Bay Packers Advance

The Green Bay Packers defeated the Dallas Cowboys 26-21 in an NFC Wild Card Game, advancing to the Divisional Round of the playoffs.

### Tennis: Serena Williams Wins Tournament of Champions

Serena Williams won her first title since having a baby, capturing the WTA's Tournament of Champions with a straight-set victory over Lucie Safarova.

## Culture
### NASA's New Horizons Spacecraft Passes Pluto

The New Horizons spacecraft flew by Pluto, providing the first close-up images of the dwarf planet and its moons. The mission marked a major milestone in exploring our solar system.

### 'Into the Woods' Opens on Broadway

The musical "Into the Woods," based on the Stephen Sondheim and James Lapine collaboration, opened on Broadway at the Richard Rodgers Theatre.

## History
### 12th Anniversary of the Fall of Saddam Hussein's Statue

On this day in 2014, it was exactly 12 years since a crowd toppled a statue of Saddam Hussein in Baghdad's Firdos Square, marking the end of his rule.
