**March 5, 2014**
===============

### News and Politics

* **Nelson Mandela dies**: Nelson Mandela, the former President of South Africa and anti-apartheid activist, passes away at the age of 95. [1]
* **Vladimir Putin announces partial annexation of Crimea**: Russian President Vladimir Putin signs a decree to absorb Crimea into Russia, in response to Ukraine's new government not aligning with Moscow's interests. [2]

### Sports

* **NFL free agency begins**: The 2014 NFL free agency period starts, allowing teams to negotiate contracts with players who are not under contract. [3]
* **UFC 170: Rousey vs. McMann**: Women's bantamweight champion Ronda Rousey successfully defends her title against Sara McMann at the Mandalay Bay Events Center in Las Vegas.

### Culture and Entertainment

* **The Lego Movie premieres**: The animated comedy film, featuring the voice talents of Chris Pratt and Elizabeth Banks, is released in theaters worldwide. [4]
* **Miley Cyrus cancels tour dates due to illness**: Pop star Miley Cyrus announces that she will be canceling several concert dates on her "Bangerz" tour due to a vocal cord infection.
