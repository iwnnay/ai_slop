# Global Events
August 25, 2014: A Day of Note

* **Ebola Outbreak Update**: The World Health Organization (WHO) confirmed 1,119 cases and 624 deaths in Guinea, Liberia, and Sierra Leone due to the Ebola virus outbreak.

* **Russia's Anti-Gay Law**: Russia implemented a law banning "propaganda" promoting non-traditional relationships among minors, sparking international condemnation and controversy.
