Important Events for 2014-04-10:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on April 10th, 2014:

# Event 1: **South Korean Ferry Sinks**
The Sewol ferry carrying over 476 people, mostly high school students, capsizes and sinks off the coast of South Korea. The tragedy would go on to claim the lives of 304 people.

# Event 2: **Russian Intervention in Ukraine**
Pro-Russian protesters seize government buildings in several cities across eastern Ukraine, as tensions between Ukraine and Russia escalate.

# Event 3: **Australian Federal Election**
The Australian federal election takes place, with the Labor Party led by Kevin Rudd defeating the incumbent Liberal Party led by Tony Abbott.

# Event 4: **Boston Marathon Bombing Trial**
The trial of Dzhokhar Tsarnaev begins in Boston, Massachusetts. Tsarnaev is accused of carrying out the April 15, 2013 Boston Marathon bombing that killed three people and injured hundreds.

# Event 5: **NBA Playoffs Begin**
The NBA playoffs begin, with the Indiana Pacers defeating the Atlanta Hawks 101-93 in Game 1 of their first-round series.

# Event 6: **Gaza Border Tensions Escalate**
Tensions escalate along the Gaza-Israel border as Palestinian protesters clash with Israeli soldiers, leading to several injuries and arrests on both sides.

# Event 7: **US Federal Budget Passes**
The US Congress passes a $1.01 trillion federal budget for 2015, ending a three-day government shutdown that began when lawmakers failed to agree on spending levels.

# Event 8: **UK Government Reforms**
British Prime Minister David Cameron announces plans to reform the country's welfare system and increase the minimum wage to £7.50 an hour by 2016.

# Event 9: **French Presidential Election Debate**
The first debate between French presidential candidates François Hollande, Marine Le Pen, and Jean-François Copé takes place, ahead of the April 22nd runoff election.

# Event 10: **Chinese Space Station Launches**
China launches its Tiantan-1 space station, a key step in the country's plans to develop a modular space station capable of accommodating astronauts for extended periods.
