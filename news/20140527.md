Important Events for 2014-05-27:

Here are the top 10 global events that occurred on May 27, 2014:

# Event 1: World War II Veteran Dies at Age 107
World War II veteran and former prisoner of war, Desmond Doss, passed away at the age of 107. He was known for being a conscientious objector who served as a medic during the war.

# Event 2: European Union Approves Sanctions on Ukraine
The European Union approved sanctions against several Ukrainian officials in response to the crisis in Ukraine. The sanctions aimed to pressure President Viktor Yanukovych's government to take a more inclusive approach.

# Event 3: Ebola Outbreak Spreads to Guinea
Guinea confirmed its first case of Ebola, bringing the total number of countries affected by the outbreak to three (Sierra Leone and Liberia being the other two). The World Health Organization (WHO) declared the outbreak a Public Health Emergency of International Concern (PHEIC).

# Event 4: Japan's Prime Minister Visits Yasukuni Shrine
Japanese Prime Minister Shinzo Abe visited Yasukuni Shrine, which honors Japanese war dead including those who fought in World War II. This visit sparked controversy and concerns about Abe's historical revisionism.

# Event 5: Israeli-Palestinian Conflict Erupts into Violence
Violence broke out between Israelis and Palestinians in Jerusalem after a Palestinian teenager was found murdered. The incident marked the latest escalation in tensions between the two sides.

# Event 6: Chinese Premier Visits Taiwan
Chinese Premier Li Keqiang visited Taiwan, marking the first high-level visit from Beijing to Taipei since 2005. The trip aimed to improve relations and boost economic ties between the two nations.

# Event 7: World's Largest Plane Lands in Australia
The world's largest plane, the Antonov An-225 Mriya, landed at a small airfield in Australia after completing its maiden flight from Ukraine. The massive aircraft has a wingspan of over 290 feet and can carry up to 200 tons.

# Event 8: Chinese Astronauts Return from Space Station Mission
Chinese astronauts Huang Kunming, Wang Zui, and Liu Yang returned from their space station mission aboard the Shenzhou-11 spacecraft. This marked China's longest-ever manned spaceflight mission.

# Event 9: Russian President Visits Ukraine Border
Russian President Vladimir Putin visited the Ukrainian border, where he reiterated his support for pro-Russian separatists in eastern Ukraine. The visit was seen as a show of military force and a warning to Ukraine and its Western allies.

# Event 10: New York Yankees Trade Pitcher Ivan Nova
The New York Yankees traded starting pitcher Ivan Nova to the Pittsburgh Pirates for minor league prospect Abel Flores. This marked the third time Nova had been traded in his career, after previously being dealt from the Yankees to the Philadelphia Phillies.
