# October 4th, 2014: A Look Back
====================================================

### News and Politics

1. **Ukraine conflict**: Pro-Russian separatists in Ukraine claim to have shot down a Ukrainian military helicopter, killing at least four people. The incident marked a significant escalation of the conflict in eastern Ukraine.
2. **Ebola outbreak**: The World Health Organization (WHO) reports that the number of Ebola cases has reached 7,491 in West Africa, with 3,383 deaths.

### Sports

1. **NFL**: The Kansas City Chiefs defeat the New England Patriots 41-14 in an NFL game at Arrowhead Stadium.
2. **MLB Playoffs**: The San Francisco Giants defeat the St. Louis Cardinals 6-4 in Game 1 of the National League Division Series.

### Culture and Entertainment

1. **Music**: American musician Taylor Swift releases her album "1989" to critical acclaim and commercial success.
2. **Film**: The sci-fi thriller film "Interstellar" starring Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, and Jessica Chastain is released in theaters worldwide.

### Historical Events

1. **Anniversaries**: It has been 25 years since the fall of the Berlin Wall, which marked the end of the Cold War.
2. **Space Exploration**: The European Space Agency's Rosetta spacecraft successfully lands on Comet 67P/Churyumov-Gerasimenko, marking a major achievement in space exploration.

### Business and Economy

1. **Stock Market**: The Dow Jones Industrial Average closes above 17,000 for the first time, reaching an all-time high of 17,003.
2. **Economic Indicators**: The United States Bureau of Labor Statistics reports that the unemployment rate has fallen to 6.1%, its lowest level since September 2008.
