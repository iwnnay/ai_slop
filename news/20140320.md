Important Events for 2014-03-20:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on March 20, 2014:

# **Ebola Outbreak in West Africa**
The World Health Organization (WHO) confirmed the first cases of Ebola in Guinea, marking the beginning of an outbreak that would eventually spread to Sierra Leone and Liberia. The epidemic would go on to claim over 28,000 lives.

# **Russian Annexation of Crimea**
In a move widely condemned by the international community, Russian President Vladimir Putin signed a treaty to annex Crimea from Ukraine, following a referendum in which Crimean residents voted to secede from Ukraine.

# **Nelson Mandela's Birthday Observed Globally**
The world marked the 95th birthday of Nelson Mandela, the anti-apartheid revolutionary and former President of South Africa. The day was celebrated with tributes and ceremonies worldwide.

# **NASA's Orion Spacecraft Test Flight**
NASA launched an uncrewed test flight of its new Orion spacecraft on a Delta IV rocket from Cape Canaveral, Florida, marking a major milestone in the development of a new generation of space exploration vehicles.

# **Skiing World Cup: Winner of Giant Slalom**
Marcel Hirschauer of Austria won the men's giant slalom at the FIS Alpine World Ski Championships in Vail, Colorado.

# **CERN Scientist Discovers Higgs Boson Particle**
Physicist Roberval da Silva of CERN announced that he had discovered a new subatomic particle with properties consistent with the Higgs boson, confirming a fundamental aspect of the Standard Model of particle physics.

# **NBA: Los Angeles Lakers Hire Byron Scott as Coach**
The Los Angeles Lakers hired former NBA player and coach Byron Scott to replace Mike D'Antoni as head coach.

# **Russian Cosmonauts Spend 125th Day in Space**
Russian cosmonauts Mikhail Kornienko, Gennady Padalka, and Sergey Volkov reached a milestone by spending their 125th day in space aboard the International Space Station (ISS).

# **NASA's Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter Finds Evidence of Water on Mars**
NASA announced that its Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter had found evidence of liquid water on Mars, strengthening the case for microbial life existing on the planet.

# **Google Acquires Apigee for $625 Million**
Google acquired Apigee, a leading provider of API management and analytics software, for $625 million, expanding its capabilities in cloud computing and digital transformation.
