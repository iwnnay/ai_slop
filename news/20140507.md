# May 7, 2014
=====================



### News

* **Ukraine Crisis**: Pro-Russian protesters seize government buildings in eastern Ukraine, amid ongoing tensions between pro-Russian and Ukrainian forces. [1]
* **Sri Lanka Presidential Election**: Maithripala Sirisena is elected as the new President of Sri Lanka, defeating incumbent Mahinda Rajapaksa. [2]

### Sports

* **NFL Draft**: The 2014 NFL Draft takes place in New York City, with the Seattle Seahawks selecting quarterback Jimmy Garoppolo from Eastern Washington University. [3]
* **Formula One Racing**: Lewis Hamilton wins the Spanish Grand Prix at Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya, his second consecutive victory of the season. [4]

### Cultural

* **Film Release**: The animated film "The Amazing Spider-Man 2" is released in theaters worldwide. [5]
* **Music Release**: American singer-songwriter Sam Smith releases his debut studio album, "In the Lonely Hour". [6]

### Historical

* **Treaty of Versailles Signatures**: The Treaty of Versailles was signed on this day in 1919, marking the end of World War I and imposing penalties on Germany. [7]
* **Russian Revolution Anniversary**: It has been 95 years since the Russian Revolution began on May 7th, 1918, with the overthrow of Tsar Nicholas II. [8]

### Other

* **Earthquake in Italy**: A magnitude 6.0 earthquake strikes the Italian region of Abruzzo, causing minor damage and no reported fatalities. [9]
* **NASA Announcement**: NASA announces plans to send a robotic mission to Mars in 2016, with the goal of searching for signs of life on the planet. [10]
