# October 12, 2014
===============

1. **NASA's Space Launch System (SLS) Core Stage Completes Green Run Testing**: NASA successfully completed the final test of its new heavy-lift rocket, SLS, paving the way for its maiden voyage in 2017.

2. **FIFA World Cup Qualifying Matches**: Several teams advanced to the next round of qualification matches for the 2018 FIFA World Cup, including Brazil, Argentina, and Mexico.

3. **Australian Senate Passes Marriage Equality Bill**: The Australian Senate passed a bill legalizing same-sex marriage in Australia, making it the 15th country to do so.

4. **European Space Agency's (ESA) Philae Lander Wakes Up from Hibernation**: After nine months of silence, ESA's Philae lander woke up on Comet 67P/Churyumov-Gerasimenko, sending back valuable data and images.

5. **Pakistan Super League (PSL) Inaugural Match**: The PSL, a professional Twenty20 cricket league in Pakistan, held its inaugural match at the Dubai International Cricket Stadium.

6. **Ebola Outbreak Continues to Spread**: The World Health Organization reported that the Ebola outbreak had claimed over 4,500 lives across West Africa, with Guinea and Sierra Leone experiencing new cases.

7. **U.S. House of Representatives Passes Budget Bill**: The U.S. House of Representatives passed a budget bill aimed at avoiding another government shutdown and increasing spending on defense and domestic programs.

8. **Austrian Grand Prix in F1 Racing**: The 2014 Austrian Grand Prix was held at the Red Bull Ring, with Lewis Hamilton claiming his third win of the season.

9. **Hong Kong Umbrella Movement Protests Continue**: Tensions escalated as protests continued in Hong Kong against China's government, with both sides refusing to budge on key demands.

10. **São Paulo International Book Fair (BIFF) Opens**: The 26th edition of BIFF took place in São Paulo, Brazil, featuring authors and publishers from around the world.
