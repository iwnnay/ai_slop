Important Events for 2014-04-11:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on April 11, 2014:

# Event 1: Ukraine Crisis
The Ukrainian government announced plans to hold early presidential elections, amid ongoing protests and violence in the country.

## Ukraine crisis continues

# Event 2: NASA's Orion Spacecraft Test Flight
NASA successfully conducted a test flight of its new Orion spacecraft, designed for deep space missions.

## First flight of NASA's Orion spacecraft

# Event 3: Boston Marathon Bombing Verdict
A jury delivered a guilty verdict to Dzhokhar Tsarnaev, the suspect in the 2013 Boston Marathon bombing that killed three people and injured hundreds.

## Tsarnaev convicted of Boston Marathon bombing

# Event 4: Google Announces Android L Release Date
Google announced that its next-generation Android operating system, dubbed "Android L," would be released to manufacturers in June 2014.

## Android L release date announced

# Event 5: Malaysia Airlines Flight MH370 Search Continues
The search for missing Malaysia Airlines Flight MH370 continued, with no signs of the plane's wreckage found despite extensive efforts.

## Search for MH370 continues

# Event 6: Toronto Mayor Rob Ford Takes Leave
Toronto Mayor Rob Ford took a leave of absence from his duties following revelations about his personal life and potential health issues.

## Toronto Mayor Rob Ford takes leave

# Event 7: Russian President Putin Visits Crimea
Russian President Vladimir Putin visited Crimea, where he attended a meeting with Ukrainian leaders and reiterated Russia's support for the region.

## Putin visits Crimea amid tensions

# Event 8: NBA Player Jason Collins Comes Out as Gay
NBA player Jason Collins became the first openly gay athlete in major professional sports, announcing his sexuality in a column for Sports Illustrated.

## Jason Collins comes out as gay

# Event 9: Germanwings Flight 4U9525 Crashes
Germanwings Flight 4U9525 crashed near Barcelona, Spain, killing all 150 people on board. The cause was later attributed to deliberate pilot action.

## Germanwings Flight 4U9525 crashes

# Event 10: NASA's Kepler Space Telescope Mission Ends
NASA announced that its Kepler space telescope had completed its primary mission of searching for exoplanets and would be repurposed for a new mission.

## Kepler space telescope completes primary mission
