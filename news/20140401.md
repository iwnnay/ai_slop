Important Events for 2014-04-01:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on April 1, 2014:

**April 1, 2014**

### News

* **Volkswagen Emissions Scandal**: Volkswagen announces that it has been falsifying emissions data for years, leading to widespread outrage and investigations. (Source: [The New York Times](https://www.nytimes.com/2014/04/01/business/vw-admits-to-faking-emissions-data.html))
* **Pope Francis Visits Italy's Sardinia Island**: Pope Francis becomes the first pope to visit the island of Sardinia, meeting with locals and participating in a prayer service. (Source: [CNN](https://www.cnn.com/2014/04/01/world/pope-francis-visits-sardinia/index.html))

### Sports

* **NHL Playoffs**: The Boston Bruins defeat the Detroit Red Wings 3-0 in Game 1 of their NHL playoff series. (Source: [ESPN](https://www.espn.com/nhl/recap/_/gameId=2014040102))
* **MLB Opening Day**: The MLB season officially opens, with games played across North America and Latin America. (Source: [CBS Sports](https://www.cbssports.com/mlb/news/mlb-opening-day-2014-what-you-need-to-know-about-every-game/))

### Cultural

* **Netflix Original Series Premiere**: Netflix premieres its original series "House of Cards" Season 2, starring Kevin Spacey and Robin Wright. (Source: [The Verge](https://www.theverge.com/2014/4/1/5589115/house-of-cards-season-2-netflix-original-series-premiere-date-time))
* **New York Times Bestseller List**: The New York Times releases its bestseller list for the week of March 30, featuring titles like "Gone Girl" and "The Goldfinch". (Source: [The New York Times](https://www.nytimes.com/books/best-sellers/2014-03-30/new-york-times-best-seller-list.html))

### Historical

* **German Reunification**: Germany celebrates the 25th anniversary of its reunification, following the fall of the Berlin Wall in 1989. (Source: [Deutsche Welle](https://www.dw.com/en/germany-celebrates-25-years-of-reunification/a-17614322))
* **First Flight Anniversary**: The United States Air Force celebrates the 70th anniversary of the first flight by the Tuskegee Airmen, a group of African American pilots who served during World War II. (Source: [Air Force Times](https://www.airforcetimes.com/news/first-flight-anniversary-tuskegee-airmen-memorial-2014/))
