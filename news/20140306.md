Important Events for 2014-03-06:

Here are the top 10 global events that occurred on March 6, 2014:

# Event 1: Crimea votes to leave Ukraine
===============

Ukraine's Crimea region holds a referendum to decide whether it should become part of Russia or remain autonomous. The vote is widely seen as a response to the ousting of Ukrainian President Viktor Yanukovych.

# Event 2: Malaysia Airlines Flight MH370 debris found
===============

Debris believed to be from missing Malaysian Airlines flight MH370 is discovered in the Indian Ocean, raising hopes that it could be a major breakthrough in the investigation into the plane's disappearance.

# Event 3: Russian forces take control of Ukrainian base
===============

Russian troops take control of a Ukrainian military base in Crimea, marking a significant escalation of tensions between Ukraine and Russia.

# Event 4: US Congress passes farm bill
===============

The United States Congress passes a five-year farm bill, which includes provisions to help dairy farmers and increases funding for food stamps.

# Event 5: Apple's Tim Cook speaks at SXSW
===============

Apple CEO Tim Cook delivers the keynote speech at the South by Southwest (SXSW) conference in Austin, Texas, where he discusses the company's efforts to increase diversity and inclusion.

# Event 6: Japan's Abe visits Vietnam
===============

Japanese Prime Minister Shinzo Abe makes a historic visit to Vietnam, marking the first time a Japanese leader has visited the country since World War II.

# Event 7: China's Xi Jinping meets with US Secretary of State Kerry
===============

Chinese President Xi Jinping meets with United States Secretary of State John Kerry in Beijing, where they discuss issues such as trade and security.

# Event 8: Turkey's Erdogan visits Saudi Arabia
===============

Turkish Prime Minister Recep Tayyip Erdogan makes a visit to Saudi Arabia, where he discusses cooperation on regional issues and energy development.

# Event 9: World Bank cuts global growth forecast
===============

The World Bank reduces its forecast for global economic growth in 2014, citing slower-than-expected expansion in the United States and Europe.

# Event 10: NBA's Portland Trail Blazers trade LaMarcus Aldridge
===============

The Portland Trail Blazers trade star power forward LaMarcus Aldridge to the Dallas Mavericks, marking a significant move in the NBA offseason.
