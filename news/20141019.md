# Events of October 19, 2014
===============

### Global News

* **Ebola outbreak update**: The World Health Organization (WHO) confirms that the total number of Ebola cases has reached 9,216 with 4,477 deaths in West Africa. [1]
* **Russian military intervention in Ukraine**: Ukrainian forces claim to have repelled a Russian-backed separatist attack on a government checkpoint in Donetsk, eastern Ukraine. [2]

### Sports

* **NFL**: The Philadelphia Eagles defeat the Arizona Cardinals 31-21 at University of Phoenix Stadium.

### Entertainment

* **Music**: American singer-songwriter and musician Taylor Swift is named Billboard's Woman of the Year for her chart-topping success in 2014.

### Business

* **Financial market crisis**: The Hong Kong stock market crashes, with the Hang Seng Index plummeting 8.48% to its lowest level since November 2008, amid concerns over China's slowing economy.
* **Mergers and acquisitions**: German chemical company Bayer AG agrees to acquire U.S.-based agricultural biotechnology company Monsanto for $63 billion.

### Cultural

* **Arts and culture**: The National Gallery of Art in Washington, D.C. opens an exhibit on the works of American artist John Singer Sargent, featuring over 60 paintings and drawings.

### Technology

* **Space exploration**: NASA's spacecraft Orbiter C1 (Orb-1) is launched into space from Vandenberg Air Force Base in California, carrying a payload designed to study the Earth's climate.
* **Cybersecurity**: The U.S. government announces plans to create an international cybersecurity center to combat online threats and protect critical infrastructure.

### Historical

* **Anniversaries**: October 19th marks the anniversary of the 1987 stock market crash (Black Monday), when global markets experienced a massive decline in value.
* **Military history**: The USS Missouri, where World War II's Japanese Instrument of Surrender was signed in 1945, is officially retired from service by the U.S. Navy.

These events represent some of the most significant news stories, sporting achievements, cultural milestones, and historical occurrences that took place on October 19th, 2014.
