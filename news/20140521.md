Important Events for 2014-05-21:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on May 21, 2014:

# Event 1: Malaysia Airlines Flight 370 Investigation Update
A new piece of debris was found in the Indian Ocean that could be linked to the missing Boeing 777 aircraft.

## Event 2: European Parliament Elections
Voters in Europe went to the polls to elect members of the European Parliament, with the eurosceptic Alternative for Germany (AfD) party making gains.

### Event 3: Israeli-Palestinian Conflict Escalates
The Israeli military launched airstrikes on Gaza Strip targets after Palestinian militants fired rockets into Israel.

#### Event 4: China's First Aircraft Carrier Enters Service
The Chinese People's Liberation Army Navy commissioned its first aircraft carrier, the Liaoning.

##### Event 5: Ebola Outbreak Reaches West Africa
The World Health Organization (WHO) declared a public health emergency in Guinea, Sierra Leone, and Liberia due to the spread of Ebola virus disease.

###### Event 6: Russia-Ukraine Conflict Intensifies
Pro-Russian separatists in eastern Ukraine seized government buildings in several cities, prompting a response from Ukrainian forces.

####### Event 7: South Korea Ferry Sinks
A passenger ferry carrying over 470 people sank off the coast of South Korea, resulting in one of the country's deadliest maritime disasters.

######### Event 8: Google Acquires Waze
Google acquired Israeli navigation app Waze for $1.1 billion.

########## Event 9: Apple Unveils iOS 8 and Mac Pro
Apple announced a major update to its mobile operating system, iOS 8, as well as a new desktop computer, the Mac Pro.

########### Event 10: NBA Playoffs Begin
The National Basketball Association (NBA) playoffs started, with the Indiana Pacers facing off against the Washington Wizards in the Eastern Conference semifinals.
