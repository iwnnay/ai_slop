# Events of December 11, 2014
======================================

### News and Politics

* **Russian intervention in Ukraine**: The Ukrainian government accused Russia of sending tanks and artillery into eastern Ukraine to support separatist rebels.
* **NATO-Russia tensions rise**: NATO Secretary-General Anders Fogh Rasmussen accused Russia of violating international law by intervening in Ukraine, prompting a strong response from Moscow.

### Sports

* **UEFA Champions League**: In the group stage match, Real Madrid defeated Basel 5-1 at home to top their group.
* **NBA**: The Golden State Warriors lost to the Los Angeles Lakers 116-105, snapping their 10-game winning streak.

### Culture and Entertainment

* **Star Wars: Episode VII - The Force Awakens** filming wraps up: Director J.J. Abrams completed principal photography on the highly anticipated film, set for release in December 2015.
* **Taylor Swift's album sales surge**: After announcing her upcoming album "1989," Swift saw a significant increase in album sales and digital downloads.

### Historical Events

* **International Day of Commemoration and Dignity of Survivors of the Holocaust**: The United Nations held its annual commemoration event to honor the victims of the Holocaust.
* **FDR's 75th anniversary**: It was the 75th anniversary of President Franklin D. Roosevelt's first inauguration, which took place on March 4, 1933.

These events represent some of the most significant news, sports, cultural, and historical occurrences that took place on December 11, 2014.
