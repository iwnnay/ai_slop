Important Events for 2014-01-07:

January 7th, 2014! Here are 3-5 major global events that took place on this date:

1. **Syrian Civil War**: The United Nations Security Council passed a resolution (2139) condemning the use of barrel bombs by the Syrian government against civilians and calling for humanitarian access to affected areas. This was a significant development in the ongoing conflict, which had already resulted in hundreds of thousands of deaths and widespread human suffering.
2. **Egyptian Elections**: Egyptians went to the polls to elect a new president, with incumbent President Mohamed Morsi's opponent, Abdel Fattah el-Sisi, emerging victorious. El-Sisi would go on to become the country's next president, bringing an end to Morsi's short-lived tenure and marking a significant shift in Egypt's political landscape.
3. **Nelson Mandela Dies**: Former South African President Nelson Mandela passed away at the age of 95, leaving behind a legacy as a champion of racial equality and anti-apartheid activism. His death was met with widespread grief and tributes around the world.

Additional notable events on January 7th, 2014:

* **NFL Playoffs**: The Green Bay Packers defeated the San Francisco 49ers 23-20 in an NFC Wild Card Game.
* **Boxing**: Ukrainian boxer Sergey Kovalev knocked out Roman Karmazin to retain his WBO light heavyweight title.

These events were significant not only for their impact on global politics, sports, and culture but also for their historical significance, as they marked important turning points or milestones in the lives of prominent individuals.
