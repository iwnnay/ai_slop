Important Events for 2014-11-19:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on November 19, 2014:

### Global Events

* **Ebola Outbreak**: The World Health Organization (WHO) announced that the Ebola outbreak in West Africa had reached a "critical point" with over 5,000 reported cases and more than 2,500 deaths.
* **Ukraine Crisis**: Ukrainian President Petro Poroshenko signed laws to decentralize power, de-occupy Crimea, and create a special status for Donetsk and Luhansk regions.
* **Nuclear Disarmament Talks**: The United States and Russia agreed to extend the New START treaty for five years, which aims to reduce their strategic nuclear arsenals.

### Sports

* **NFL**: The Seattle Seahawks defeated the Arizona Cardinals 39-6 in an NFL game at CenturyLink Field.

### Music

* **Taylor Swift Album Release**: Taylor Swift's album "1989" was released, featuring hit singles like "Shake It Off", "Blank Space", and "Style".

### Film

* **Interstellar Premiere**: The science fiction film "Interstellar" directed by Christopher Nolan premiered in London.
* **The Theory of Everything**: The biographical romantic drama film about physicist Stephen Hawking's life, starring Eddie Redmayne and Felicity Jones, was released.

### Technology

* **Google+ Shut Down**: Google announced that it would be shutting down its social network Google+ due to a data breach affecting over 500,000 users.
* **SpaceX Reusability Test**: SpaceX successfully tested the reusability of its Falcon 9 rocket, landing it vertically after an orbital launch.

### Culture

* **Punk Rock Legend Dies**: Punk rock legend and Sex Pistols' bassist Paul Gray died at the age of 55 due to complications from a heart attack.
* **The Simpsons Breaks Record**: The longest-running primetime scripted show in television history, "The Simpsons", aired its 26th season premiere, breaking the record previously held by "Gunsmoke".

### History

* **Russian Cosmonaut Dies**: Russian cosmonaut and space explorer, Gennady Strekalov, died at the age of 73 after a long illness.
* **WWI Centenary**: The centenary of World War I was commemorated with ceremonies and events around the world.

These are just some of the significant global events that occurred on November 19, 2014.
