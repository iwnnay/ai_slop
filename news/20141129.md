# Events of November 29, 2014
===============

### Politics and Government

1. **Ebola Outbreak Update**: The World Health Organization (WHO) reported that the number of Ebola cases in West Africa had passed the 18,000 mark, with Guinea, Liberia, and Sierra Leone being the most affected countries.
2. **Kurdish Regional Government Referendum**: The Kurdish Regional Government in Iraq held a referendum on independence from Iraq, with over 95% of voters supporting secession.

### Sports

3. **NBA**: The Los Angeles Lakers traded point guard Jordan Farmar to the Utah Jazz in exchange for cash considerations.
4. **Soccer (FIFA)**: The FIFA World Cup qualification process concluded with several teams securing their spots for the tournament, including Brazil, Argentina, and Spain.

### Entertainment and Culture

5. **Movie Release**: The sci-fi thriller "Interstellar" starring Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, and Jessica Chastain was released in theaters worldwide.
6. **Music Release**: American rapper Lil Wayne's 12th studio album, "Free Weezy Album", was released.

### Business and Economics

7. **Stock Market**: The Dow Jones Industrial Average (DJIA) closed at a record high of 17,663.49 due to positive economic indicators and the Federal Reserve's decision not to raise interest rates.
8. **Economic Data**: The United States Bureau of Labor Statistics released data showing that the country added 233,000 new jobs in November, beating expectations.

### Science and Technology

9. **Space Exploration**: NASA's New Horizons spacecraft flew by Pluto, providing stunning images and valuable scientific data about the dwarf planet.
10. **Artificial Intelligence**: Google announced a significant breakthrough in artificial intelligence research, developing an AI system that could learn from human feedback without being explicitly programmed.
