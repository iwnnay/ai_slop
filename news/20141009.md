Important Events for 2014-10-09:

Here are 10 major global events that took place on October 9th, 2014:

**October 9th, 2014**
======================

### News

1. **Ebola outbreak**: The World Health Organization (WHO) announces that the Ebola virus has spread to 4 new countries: Senegal, Sierra Leone, Guinea-Bissau, and Liberia.
2. **Moldova elections**: Moldovans go to the polls to elect a new president, with Igor Dodon winning the election.

### Sports

3. **NBA season opener**: The 2014-15 NBA season kicks off with a tripleheader on October 9th, featuring games between the Miami Heat vs. Chicago Bulls, Golden State Warriors vs. Phoenix Suns, and Portland Trail Blazers vs. Utah Jazz.
4. **MLB playoffs**: The San Francisco Giants defeat the Pittsburgh Pirates 8-0 in Game 2 of their National League Division Series.

### Culture

5. **New album releases**: Singer-songwriters Ed Sheeran (x) and Snoop Dogg (Bush) release new albums on this date.
6. **Comic book milestones**: The first issue of comic book series "Lumberjanes" is released by BOOM! Studios, while the final issue of "The Unwritten" is published.

### History

7. **Yemen conflict**: Houthi rebels capture Yemen's capital city, Sana'a, amidst a growing crisis in the country.
8. **Ukraine protests**: Pro-Russian protesters storm government buildings in Ukraine, sparking fears of a broader crisis.
9. **Philippines typhoon anniversary**: The Philippines marks one year since Super Typhoon Haiyan devastated the country, killing over 6,000 people.

### Other

10. **Space exploration**: NASA's OSIRIS-REx spacecraft is launched from Cape Canaveral Air Force Station on a United Launch Alliance Atlas V rocket to explore an asteroid and return samples to Earth.
