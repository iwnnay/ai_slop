4. **Australian Open tennis tournament**: In Melbourne, Australia, the Australian Open tennis tournament kicked off with top seeds Serena Williams, Novak Djokovic, and Victoria Azarenka leading the charge.

5. **SpaceX launches Dragon spacecraft**: SpaceX successfully launched its Dragon spacecraft on a Falcon 9 rocket from Cape Canaveral, Florida, carrying cargo and supplies to the International Space Station.
