# Events of May 16, 2014

### Political and Diplomatic

* **Vladimir Putin's Re-Election**: Russian President Vladimir Putin was re-elected to a third term as president, winning 63.6% of the vote.
* **US-Iran Nuclear Talks**: The United States and Iran began nuclear negotiations in Vienna, Austria, aimed at resolving concerns over Tehran's nuclear program.

### Business and Economy

* **Microsoft-Nokia Deal Approved**: A US court approved Microsoft's $7.2 billion deal to acquire Nokia's Devices & Services division, marking a major shift in the tech industry.
* **US Unemployment Rate**: The US unemployment rate fell to 6.3%, its lowest level since September 2008.

### Sports

* **NHL Playoffs**: In Game 4 of the NHL Western Conference Finals, the Los Angeles Kings defeated the Chicago Blackhawks 5-2, taking a 3-1 series lead.
* **Spanish GP**: Nico Rosberg won the Spanish Grand Prix in Formula One, his second victory of the season.

### Cultural and Entertainment

* **TV Show "Orphan Black" Premieres**: The Canadian sci-fi thriller TV series "Orphan Black," starring Tatiana Maslany, premiered on Space (Canada) and BBC America (US).
* **Documentary Film "The Unknown Known" Released**: A documentary film about former US Secretary of Defense Donald Rumsfeld, directed by Errol Morris, was released in the United States.

### Historical

* **World War II Anniversary**: It was the 69th anniversary of the Allied forces liberating Nazi concentration camps, marking a significant moment in World War II history.
* **Nobel Prize Winner's Birthday**: Physicist and Nobel laureate Maria Goeppert Mayer was born on May 16, 1906.
