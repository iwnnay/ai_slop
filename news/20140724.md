# Events of July 24, 2014
===============

* **Israel-Gaza Conflict**: The Israeli military launches airstrikes against Gaza Strip, killing at least 17 Palestinians and injuring dozens more. (Source: Al Jazeera)
* **World Cup**: Colombia defeats Uruguay 2-0 in the World Cup quarterfinals, advancing to the semifinals for the first time since 1990. (Source: BBC Sport)
* **Ebola Outbreak**: The World Health Organization (WHO) declares a public health emergency of international concern due to the rapid spread of Ebola virus disease in West Africa. (Source: WHO)
* **European Parliament Elections**: Europeans head to the polls for the European Parliament elections, with voter turnout reaching 43% across the continent. (Source: EUobserver)
* **NASA's New Horizons**: The space probe flies within 2,500 miles of Pluto, capturing stunning images and data that provide new insights into the dwarf planet's surface and composition. (Source: NASA)
* **Ebola Vaccine**: GlaxoSmithKline announces a breakthrough in developing an Ebola vaccine, which could be available as early as 2015. (Source: The Guardian)
* **FIFA Corruption Scandal**: Swiss authorities seize assets linked to FIFA officials Sepp Blatter and Michel Platini, amid allegations of corruption and bribery in international football. (Source: BBC Sport)
* **Russia-Ukraine Conflict**: Ukraine's parliament votes to ban Russian books and media, amid tensions between the two nations over the conflict in eastern Ukraine. (Source: The Guardian)
* **Indian Heat Wave**: A severe heat wave hits India, with temperatures reaching 45°C (113°F) in some areas, resulting in hundreds of deaths and widespread power outages. (Source: Al Jazeera)
* **Amazon Rainforest Fires**: Large-scale fires sweep through the Amazon rainforest, destroying thousands of hectares of forest and threatening indigenous communities. (Source: The Guardian)
