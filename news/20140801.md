Important Events for 2014-08-01:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on August 1st, 2014:

# **Ebola Outbreak Update**
The World Health Organization (WHO) reported that the Ebola outbreak in West Africa had reached a total of 1,948 cases and 1,069 deaths.

# **Tropical Storm Hilda Forms**
In the Atlantic Ocean, Tropical Storm Hilda formed with maximum sustained winds of 60 mph (95 km/h).

# **Malaysia Airlines Flight MH370 Search**
The Australian Transport Safety Bureau (ATSB) released a new report on the disappearance of Malaysia Airlines Flight MH370, saying that a piece of wing debris found in Mozambique was unlikely to be from the missing plane.

# **US Economy Grows 2.6%**
The US Commerce Department reported that the country's gross domestic product grew at an annual rate of 2.6% in the second quarter of 2014.

# **Apple Unveils iOS 8 Beta**
Apple released the first beta version of its new operating system, iOS 8, for developers to test and provide feedback on before its public release.

# **China's Space Program Success**
The China National Space Administration announced that its Chang'e-3 spacecraft had successfully landed on the Moon's surface, with the Yutu rover beginning its exploration mission.

# **Gaza Conflict Update**
The conflict between Israel and Gaza militants continued, with at least 16 Palestinians killed in Israeli airstrikes.

# **British Royals Visit Australia**
Prince William, Duke of Cambridge, and his wife Catherine, Duchess of Cambridge, visited Melbourne, Australia, as part of their tour Down Under.

# **Tropical Cyclone Hal Update**
In the Pacific Ocean, Tropical Cyclone Hal made landfall in Fiji, bringing strong winds and heavy rainfall to the island nation.

# **US Open Golf Tournament Begins**
The 114th US Open Championship began at Pine Valley Golf Club in New Jersey, with Rory McIlroy seeking to win his first major of the year.

Note: These events are not necessarily ranked in order of importance or significance.
