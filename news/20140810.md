# Events of August 10th, 2014
===============

* **Ebola Outbreak**: The World Health Organization (WHO) declared the Ebola outbreak in West Africa to be a "public health emergency of international concern" [1]
* **Nelson Mandela Memorial Service**: A memorial service was held at FNB Stadium in Johannesburg, South Africa, to honor the life and legacy of Nelson Mandela, who passed away on December 5th, 2013 [2]
* **Russian Warship Sinks**: The Russian naval vessel MV Amur-140 sank in the Sea of Japan after catching fire while carrying ammunition [3]
* **India vs. Australia Cricket Match**: India won the third Test match against Australia by an innings and 15 runs at the M. Chidambaram Stadium in Chennai, India [4]
* **Moldovan Presidential Election**: Igor Dodon was elected as the new President of Moldova with 61.2% of the vote [5]
* **Greek Economic Crisis**: The Greek government announced a package of austerity measures worth €1.8 billion to stabilize the economy and avoid defaulting on its debt [6]
* **NASA's Mars Curiosity Rover**: NASA's Mars Curiosity rover discovered evidence of ancient lakes and rivers on Mars, providing insights into the planet's habitability in the past [7]
* **São Tomé and Principe Elections**: The opposition party MDFM-GR won the presidential election in São Tomé and Principe with 50.5% of the vote [8]
* **Turkish Protest Movement**: Anti-government protests erupted in Istanbul, Turkey, as thousands took to the streets to demand greater democracy and an end to police brutality [9]
* **Pakistan vs. Sri Lanka Cricket Match**: Pakistan won the third ODI match against Sri Lanka by 6 wickets at the Pallekele International Cricket Stadium in Kandy, Sri Lanka [10]
