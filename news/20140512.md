Important Events for 2014-05-12:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on May 12, 2014:

# Event 1: Malaysia Airlines Flight 370 Investigation Update
===========================

The investigation into the disappearance of Malaysia Airlines Flight 370 took a new turn with the release of a preliminary report revealing that the flight likely ended in the Indian Ocean.

# Event 2: Syrian Civil War Escalates
==================================

Fighting between government forces and rebels intensified in Aleppo, Syria's largest city, as government airstrikes continued to target residential areas.

# Event 3: Ukraine Crisis Deepens
==============================

Pro-Russian protesters seized government buildings in eastern Ukraine, amid growing tensions over the country's political future.

# Event 4: European Union Expands
==================================

Croatia officially joined the European Union, becoming its 28th member state and the third former Yugoslav nation to join the bloc.

# Event 5: NBA Playoffs Continue
============================

The Los Angeles Clippers took a 3-1 lead over the Golden State Warriors in their Western Conference semifinal series.

# Event 6: NFL Draft Begins
==========================

The 2014 NFL Draft got underway, with the Houston Texans selecting defensive end Jadeveon Clowney first overall.

# Event 7: Apple Reports Earnings
================================

Apple Inc. reported quarterly earnings of $9.5 billion, down from $10.2 billion in the same period a year earlier.

# Event 8: Google Launches Android L
=============================

Google unveiled Android L, the latest version of its popular mobile operating system, featuring improved security and performance.

# Event 9: Hong Kong Pro-Democracy Protesters Clash with Police
=====================================

Thousands of pro-democracy protesters clashed with police in Hong Kong's central business district, marking the first major protest since China's Communist Party tightened control over the territory's electoral system.

# Event 10: The Fault in Our Stars Movie Premieres
=============================

The film adaptation of John Green's bestselling novel "The Fault in Our Stars" premiered in Los Angeles, starring Shailene Woodley and Ansel Elgort as two teenagers struggling with cancer.
