Important Events for 2014-05-15:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on May 15th, 2014:

**Global Events**

### Ukraine Protests Intensify
Ukrainian riot police clashed with protesters in Kiev's Independence Square, marking the latest escalation in a standoff that has left scores dead and thousands injured.

### US Senate Confirms Attorney General Nominee
The US Senate confirmed Loretta Lynch as the new Attorney General of the United States, making her the first African American woman to hold the post.

### European Union Extends Sanctions Against Russia
In response to Russia's annexation of Crimea, the European Union extended its sanctions against Russian officials and companies, targeting those involved in the Ukrainian crisis.

### Israel-Gaza Border Tensions Rise
Tensions flared along the Israel-Gaza border after a Palestinian militant was killed by Israeli fire during protests against the Gaza blockade, prompting retaliatory rocket attacks from Gaza.

### World Health Organization Reports MERS Outbreak
The WHO confirmed an outbreak of Middle East Respiratory Syndrome (MERS) in Saudi Arabia, with over 200 cases reported and more than 100 deaths globally since the virus was first detected in 2012.

### MLB: Yankees' Derek Jeter Announces Retirement
New York Yankees shortstop Derek Jeter announced his retirement from professional baseball at the end of the 2014 season, ending a remarkable 20-year career with the team.

### NBA: Portland Trail Blazers Fire Head Coach Terry Stotts
The Portland Trail Blazers fired head coach Terry Stotts after five seasons, despite leading the team to four playoff appearances and two division titles during his tenure.

### Indian Space Research Organisation Launches PSLV-C23
India's space agency launched a Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle (PSLV-C23) carrying 10 satellites, including the IRNSS-1A navigation satellite, into orbit from Sriharikota.

### Hong Kong Pro-Democracy Protesters Clash with Police
Pro-democracy protesters clashed with police in Hong Kong as they demanded greater autonomy and electoral reform, marking a new era of political activism in the territory.

### China's Li Keqiang Meets With Russian President Vladimir Putin
Chinese Premier Li Keqiang met with Russian President Vladimir Putin in Moscow, discussing cooperation on energy projects, trade, and international issues.
