Important Events for 2014-10-31:

Here are 10 significant global events that took place on October 31st, 2014:

**October 31, 2014**

### News

1. **Ebola Outbreak Update**: The World Health Organization (WHO) reports that the number of Ebola cases in West Africa has surpassed 14,000, with more than 5,000 deaths.
2. **ISIS Releases Video Showing Beheading of US Journalist**: ISIS releases a video showing the beheading of American journalist James Foley, who was kidnapped in November 2013 while reporting on the Syrian civil war.

### Sports

1. **New York Yankees Fire Manager Joe Girardi**: The New York Yankees announce that they will not renew the contract of manager Joe Girardi after five seasons with the team.
2. **Washington Redskins QB Robert Griffin III Sidelined with Shoulder Injury**: Washington Redskins quarterback Robert Griffin III is ruled out for the remainder of the season due to a shoulder injury.

### Cultural

1. **Halloween Celebrations Worldwide**: People around the world celebrate Halloween, dressing up in costumes and trick-or-treating.
2. **Artist Jeff Koons Unveils New Sculpture**: Artist Jeff Koons unveils his new sculpture, "Seated Ballerina," at the Gagosian Gallery in New York City.

### Historical

1. **First Commercial Flight of Airbus A350-1000 Takes Off**: The first commercial flight of the Airbus A350-1000 takes off from Paris to Singapore.
2. **Russian Spacecraft Soyuz TMA-15M Returns to Earth**: Russian spacecraft Soyuz TMA-15M returns to Earth, landing safely in Kazakhstan with three astronauts on board.
