Important Events for 2014-01-03:

January 3rd, 2014! Here are 3-5 major events that took place on this date:

1. **North Korea executes high-ranking officials**: North Korean leader Kim Jong-un ordered the execution of 15 high-ranking officials, including the vice premier and several generals, for their alleged roles in a plot to overthrow him. This event marked one of the most significant purges within the ruling Workers' Party of Korea since Kim Jong-un took power in 2011.
2. **NASA's Curiosity rover discovers evidence of ancient lake on Mars**: NASA's Curiosity rover discovered sedimentary rocks and mineral deposits that suggested an ancient lake existed on Mars about 3.5 billion years ago. This finding provided valuable insights into the planet's watery past and the possibility of life existing there.
3. **India launches record-breaking 104 satellites in single mission**: India successfully launched a PSLV-C23 rocket, carrying 104 small satellites (mostly from various countries) into orbit. This achievement set a new Guinness World Record for the most satellites placed into space at once, showcasing India's growing capabilities in the field of space exploration.
