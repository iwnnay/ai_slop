Important Events for 2014-06-15:

Here are the top 10 global events that took place on June 15th, 2014:

# 1: World Cup 2014 - Colombia vs. Ivory Coast
The FIFA World Cup group stage match between Colombia and Ivory Coast ended in a 2-1 win for Colombia, with goals from James Rodríguez and Juan Camilo Zúñiga.

# 2: The Guardian publishes leaked US intelligence documents
The British newspaper published classified documents revealing widespread surveillance by the National Security Agency (NSA) on ordinary people, including journalists and politicians.

# 3: European Parliament votes to restrict food waste
Members of the European Parliament voted in favor of a proposal to reduce food waste in the EU by 50% by 2030.
