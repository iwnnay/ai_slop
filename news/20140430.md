Important Events for 2014-04-30:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on April 30, 2014:

**2014-04-30: Major Global Events**
==================================================

### News

1. **Boko Haram Abduction**: Boko Haram militants kidnapped over 200 schoolgirls in Chibok, Nigeria.
2. **Ukraine Crisis**: Pro-Russian separatists seized government buildings in Donetsk and Luhansk, Ukraine, amid ongoing tensions between Ukraine and Russia.

### Sports

3. **NFL Draft**: The 2014 NFL Draft took place, with the Seattle Seahawks selecting defensive end Justin Maxwell as their first pick.
4. **Giro d'Italia**: Italian cyclist Nairo Quintana won Stage 10 of the Giro d'Italia, his second stage victory in the race.

### Culture

5. **Star Wars Day**: Fans celebrated Star Wars Day, also known as May the Fourth Be with You, a play on the famous Star Wars phrase "May the Force be with you."
6. **Coachella Music Festival**: The Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival kicked off its two-weekend run in Indio, California, featuring headliners OutKast, Muse, and Arcade Fire.

### History

7. **German Reunification**: It was the 25th anniversary of German reunification, marking the day when East Germany (GDR) dissolved and West Germany (FRG) united under a single government.
8. **Cuban Missile Crisis Anniversary**: The world marked the 53rd anniversary of the Cuban Missile Crisis, which brought the United States and the Soviet Union to the brink of nuclear war.

### Other

9. **SpaceX CRS-3 Mission**: SpaceX launched its third commercial resupply mission (CRS-3) to the International Space Station, carrying cargo and supplies for the astronauts on board.
10. **Royal Baby Name Announcement**: The Duke and Duchess of Cambridge announced that their newborn son's name would be Prince George Alexander Louis, becoming the first heir apparent in generations.

These are just a few of the significant events that occurred on April 30, 2014.
