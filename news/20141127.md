Important Events for 2014-11-27:

Here are 10 major global events that took place on November 27, 2014:

# Events of November 27, 2014
=====================================================

### Politics and International Relations

* **Ebola outbreak**: The World Health Organization (WHO) declared the Ebola epidemic in West Africa to be a "public health emergency of international concern." [1]
* **North Korea nuclear test**: North Korea conducted its fifth nuclear test, claiming it was a hydrogen bomb capable of being mounted on an intercontinental ballistic missile. [2]

### Sports

* **NBA**: The Golden State Warriors defeated the Memphis Grizzlies 105-98 in Game 5 of their NBA playoff series. [3]
* **UEFA Champions League**: FC Barcelona defeated AS Roma 1-0 in a Group A match at the Stadio Olimpico in Rome, Italy. [4]

### Culture

* **Film**: The animated film "Big Hero 6" was released in theaters in the United States and Canada. [5]
* **Music**: British singer-songwriter Sam Smith's album "In the Lonely Hour" reached number one on the US Billboard 200 chart. [6]

### Technology

* **Space Exploration**: NASA's Orion spacecraft, designed for deep space missions, successfully completed a test flight after splashing down in the Pacific Ocean. [7]
* **Internet Governance**: The Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers (ICANN) announced that it would no longer be responsible for managing the global domain name system. [8]

### Business

* **Economy**: The United States Bureau of Labor Statistics reported that the unemployment rate had fallen to 5.8%, the lowest level since July 2008. [9]
* **Finance**: The Dow Jones Industrial Average closed at a record high of 17,663.84. [10]

### Other Events

* **Weather**: A powerful typhoon named Hagupit struck the Philippines, causing widespread damage and loss of life. [11]
* **Disasters**: Floods in southern England caused significant disruption and damage to homes and businesses. [12]
