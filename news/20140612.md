Important Events for 2014-06-12:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on June 12th, 2014:

# **Ebola Outbreak**: The World Health Organization (WHO) declared the Ebola outbreak in West Africa a "public health emergency of international concern" [1]

# **Malaysia Airlines Flight 17 Shootdown**: Malaysian Airlines Flight MH17 was shot down over Ukraine by pro-Russian separatists, killing all 298 people on board [2]

# **Indian Elections**: The results of India's general elections were announced, with the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) winning a majority [3]

# **FIFA World Cup**: Germany won the FIFA World Cup for the fourth time by defeating Argentina 1-0 in the final match [4]

# **Thai Military Coup**: The Thai military seized power from Prime Minister Yingluck Shinawatra, dissolving Parliament and imposing martial law [5]

# **Pope Francis Visits Korea**: Pope Francis visited South Korea, becoming the first Pope to visit the country since John Paul II in 1988 [6]

# **North Korea's Third Nuclear Test**: North Korea conducted its third nuclear test, sparking international condemnation [7]

# **India's Heat Wave**: A severe heat wave hit India, causing widespread deaths and health issues [8]

# **G20 Summit**: The G20 summit was held in Brisbane, Australia, with leaders discussing economic growth and climate change [9]

# **US Supreme Court Ruling**: The US Supreme Court ruled that the government could not require businesses to provide birth control coverage under the Affordable Care Act [10]
