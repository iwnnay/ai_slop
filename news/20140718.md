**2014-07-18: Major Global Events**

### 1. Israeli-Palestinian Conflict Escalates

* Pro-Russian separatists seize government buildings in several Ukrainian cities, sparking fears of civil war.

* Thousands of pro-democracy protesters occupy major streets in Hong Kong's financial district, demanding greater autonomy from China.
* Clashes erupt between protesters and police, resulting in arrests and injuries.

### 9. India Train Derailment Kills Dozens

* A train carrying passengers from Mumbai to Hyderabad derails in eastern India, killing at least 32 people and injuring hundreds more.

* A severe heat wave grips much of Europe, with temperatures soaring to over 40°C (104°F) in several countries.
* The heat causes widespread power outages, fires, and crop damage across the continent.
