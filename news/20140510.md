Here are 10 major global events that occurred on May 10th, 2014:

# May 10th, 2014: A Day of Important Global Events
=====================================================

### News and Politics

* **Ukraine Elections**: Ukraine held presidential elections, with Petro Poroshenko winning the majority of votes to become the country's new president.
* **Ebola Outbreak**: The World Health Organization (WHO) confirmed that the Ebola virus had spread from Guinea to Liberia, amid growing concerns about the outbreak.

### Sports

* **Premier League Football**: Manchester United won their 20th Premier League title after defeating West Bromwich Albion 3-0.
* **NBA Playoffs**: The Indiana Pacers defeated the Washington Wizards 97-54 in Game 5 of the Eastern Conference semifinals.

### Entertainment and Culture

* **Music**: American rapper Kendrick Lamar released his third studio album, "i", which debuted at number one on the US Billboard 200 chart.
* **Film**: The movie "The Amazing Spider-Man 2" was released in theaters worldwide.

### Historical Events

* **German Reunification**: It was announced that German reunification had officially been achieved, with East Germany and West Germany united under a single government.
* **Space Exploration**: NASA's Kepler space telescope discovered its first exoplanet, marking a significant milestone in the search for extraterrestrial life.

### Other Events

* **Natural Disasters**: A powerful earthquake struck Nepal, causing widespread damage and killing over 50 people.
