Here are 10 major events that occurred on October 1st, 2014:

# October 1, 2014: A Day of Significant Global Events
=====================================================

### 1. Hong Kong Protests Continue to Grow

Pro-democracy protests in Hong Kong continued to gain momentum as hundreds of thousands of people took to the streets, demanding greater democratic reforms from the government.

### 2. ISIS Seizes Key Town in Iraq

Islamic State (ISIS) militants seized the key town of Amirli in northern Iraq, marking a significant victory for the extremist group.

### 3. Russia and Ukraine Reach Gas Deal

Russia and Ukraine reached a deal to resolve a gas dispute that had threatened to disrupt energy supplies to Europe.

### 4. Ebola Outbreak Spreads in West Africa

The Ebola outbreak continued to spread rapidly across West Africa, with cases reported in Guinea, Liberia, and Sierra Leone.

### 5. Google Acquires Nest for $3.2 Billion

Google announced it would acquire smart home company Nest for $3.2 billion, marking a significant expansion into the Internet of Things (IoT) market.

### 6. Apple Reports Record Quarterly Sales

Apple reported record quarterly sales of $39.8 billion and profit of $7.5 billion, driven by strong demand for its iPhones and iPads.

### 7. Sony Unveils Next-Gen PlayStation Console

Sony unveiled its next-generation PlayStation console, the PS4, which featured improved graphics, controller, and online capabilities.

### 8. Russia and China Conduct Joint Military Drills

Russia and China conducted joint military drills in Eastern Siberia, marking a significant show of cooperation between the two nations.

### 9. New York Yankees Clinch Playoff Berth

The New York Yankees clinched a playoff berth for the first time since 2012, despite finishing with their worst record in 18 years.

### 10. "Gone Girl" Tops Box Office Charts

The psychological thriller "Gone Girl", based on the bestselling novel by Gillian Flynn, topped box office charts, earning over $37 million in its opening weekend.

These events represent a mix of significant news, sports, cultural, and historical happenings that took place on October 1st, 2014.
