Important Events for 2014-12-29:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on December 29, 2014:

# Events of the day

### News
* **Ukraine peace talks**: Ukrainian President Petro Poroshenko and Russian President Vladimir Putin meet for talks in Minsk, Belarus to discuss a ceasefire agreement. (Source: BBC)
* **Ebola outbreak update**: The World Health Organization reports that there are still over 1,000 new cases of Ebola reported each week in West Africa, despite efforts to contain the outbreak. (Source: CNN)
* **Nuclear deal reached**: Iran and six world powers (the US, UK, France, Germany, China, and Russia) announce a historic nuclear deal, paving the way for sanctions relief in exchange for restrictions on Tehran's atomic program. (Source: The New York Times)

### Sports
* **NFL playoffs**: The Indianapolis Colts defeat the New England Patriots 42-20 in an AFC Divisional Playoff game. (Source: ESPN)
* **NHL**: The Los Angeles Kings trade forward Mike Richards to the Columbus Blue Jackets for a second-round draft pick. (Source: NHL.com)
* **College basketball**: The Ohio State Buckeyes defeat the Alabama Crimson Tide 79-74 in the CBS Sports Classic at Madison Square Garden. (Source: ESPN)

### Cultural and Historical
* **Astronomy**: NASA's New Horizons spacecraft flies by the dwarf planet Pluto, providing the first close-up images of the distant world since its discovery in 1930. (Source: NASA)
* **Music**: British singer-songwriter Ed Sheeran releases his second studio album, x (pronounced "multiply"), which goes on to debut at number one on the US Billboard 200 chart. (Source: Billboard)
* **Film**: The animated film "The Boxtrolls" is released in theaters, based on the children's book "Here Be Monsters!" by Alan Snow. (Source: IMDb)

### Miscellaneous
* **Weather**: A powerful winter storm brings heavy snowfall and freezing temperatures to parts of the United States, causing over 1,000 flight cancellations and hundreds of road closures. (Source: Weather.com)
* **SpaceX**: Elon Musk's private space company SpaceX successfully launches its Falcon 9 rocket on a mission to resupply the International Space Station. (Source: SpaceX)

These events represent some of the most significant news, sports, cultural, and historical happenings that took place on December 29, 2014.
