Important Events for 2014-09-24:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on September 24, 2014:

**2014-09-24: A Day of Global Importance**
==================================================

### International News

1. **Ukraine Crisis**: Ukraine's President Petro Poroshenko announced the start of a ceasefire agreement with pro-Russian separatists in eastern Ukraine, following several days of violence and diplomatic efforts to resolve the crisis.
2. **Ebola Outbreak**: The World Health Organization (WHO) reported that the Ebola outbreak had spread to all six districts of Guinea, while Liberia's death toll rose to 1,373.

### Sports

3. **NBA**: The Los Angeles Lakers traded point guard Steve Nash to the Phoenix Suns in a three-team deal.
4. **NFL**: The NFL announced that the 2015 Pro Bowl would be played at University of Phoenix Stadium in Glendale, Arizona, instead of Honolulu, Hawaii.

### Entertainment

5. **Music**: Singer-songwriter Taylor Swift released her new album "1989", marking her first official pop record.
6. **Film**: The movie "Gone Girl" (2014), directed by David Fincher and starring Ben Affleck and Rosamund Pike, premiered in the United States.

### History

7. **Space Exploration**: NASA's Orbital ATK Cygnus spacecraft arrived at the International Space Station, carrying cargo and supplies.
8. **Science**: The European Organization for Nuclear Research (CERN) announced that data from its Large Hadron Collider confirmed the existence of a new subatomic particle, known as the "Higgs boson".
9. **Arts**: The National Gallery in Washington, D.C. opened an exhibition featuring 60 works by artist and activist Frida Kahlo.
10. **Business**: Alibaba Group Holding Ltd. raised $25 billion in one of the largest initial public offerings (IPOs) ever, listing on the New York Stock Exchange.

These events represent a mix of significant global news, sports, entertainment, scientific discoveries, historical milestones, and cultural exhibits that took place on September 24, 2014.
