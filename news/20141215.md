Important Events for 2014-12-15:

Here are 10 notable global events that occurred on December 15, 2014:

# Event 1: US Senate Passes Bill to Combat Human Trafficking
===========================================================

The United States Senate passed the Justice for Victims of Trafficking Act, a bipartisan bill aimed at combating human trafficking. The legislation provides support and services to victims of sex trafficking.

# Event 2: Australia's Prime Minister Tony Abbott Re-Elected Leader of the Liberal Party
=====================================================

Australian Prime Minister Tony Abbott was re-elected as leader of the Liberal Party, securing his position as Prime Minister for another term.

# Event 3: Russia Announces Suspension of Military Cooperation with Ukraine
=========================================================

Russia announced that it would suspend military cooperation with Ukraine due to Kiev's decision to sign an association agreement with the European Union.

# Event 4: NASA's New Horizons Spacecraft Flies By Pluto
=====================================================

NASA's New Horizons spacecraft flew by Pluto, capturing stunning images of the dwarf planet and its moons. The flyby marked a historic milestone in space exploration.

# Event 5: UN Report Reveals Increase in Global Hunger
==================================================

A United Nations report revealed that the number of people suffering from hunger worldwide had increased for the first time in over a decade, with an estimated 805 million individuals affected.

# Event 6: German Chancellor Angela Merkel Receives Honorary PhD from Hebrew University
=====================================================

German Chancellor Angela Merkel received an honorary Doctorate of Philosophy degree from the Hebrew University of Jerusalem, recognizing her efforts to promote Jewish-German relations and combat anti-Semitism.

# Event 7: China's Communist Party Congratulates Narendra Modi on Re-Election as Indian PM
=====================================================

China's Communist Party congratulated Narendra Modi on his re-election as Prime Minister of India, marking a significant improvement in bilateral ties between the two nations.

# Event 8: US Federal Reserve Decides to Keep Interest Rates Unchanged
=====================================================

The United States Federal Reserve announced that it would keep interest rates unchanged, citing concerns over global economic uncertainty and inflationary pressures.

# Event 9: Russian Artist Ilya Kabakov Dies at Age 83
==================================================

Russian-born artist Ilya Kabakov passed away at the age of 83, leaving behind a legacy of innovative and influential works in the world of modern art.

# Event 10: NFL Player Adrian Peterson Suspended for Rest of Season
=====================================================

National Football League (NFL) player Adrian Peterson was suspended for the rest of the season without pay due to his involvement in a child abuse incident.
