# October 11, 2014 Events
=====================================================

## Event 1: Malaysia Airlines Flight 370 Search Efforts Continue

The search for the missing Malaysian Airlines Flight 370 continues with no signs of wreckage or debris from the plane. The flight disappeared on March 8, 2014, while en route to Beijing.

## Event 2: Ebola Outbreak Spreads in West Africa

The World Health Organization (WHO) reports that the Ebola outbreak has spread to Guinea, Sierra Leone, Liberia, and Nigeria, with a total of 7,492 cases and 3,383 deaths as of October 11, 2014.

## Event 3: Russian Military Intervention in Ukraine

Russian President Vladimir Putin orders military intervention in eastern Ukraine to support pro-Russian separatists. The move sparks international condemnation and heightens tensions between Russia and the West.

## Event 5: European Parliament Approves Anti-Piracy Law

The European Parliament passes the anti-piracy law, which aims to combat online piracy by forcing internet service providers (ISPs) to block access to copyright-infringing websites.

## Event 6: Google Acquires Skybox Imaging

Google acquires California-based satellite imaging company Skybox Imaging for $500 million. The acquisition expands Google's capabilities in satellite imagery and data analytics.

## Event 7: India and Bangladesh Sign Teesta River Treaty

India and Bangladesh sign the Teesta River treaty, which aims to resolve long-standing disputes over water sharing and border issues between the two countries.

## Event 8: Australian Parliament Passes Marriage Equality Bill

The Australian Parliament passes the marriage equality bill, making same-sex marriage legal in Australia. The bill was passed with a majority vote of 126-30 in the House of Representatives.

## Event 9: NASA's Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter Discovers Evidence of Water on Mars

NASA's Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter (MRO) discovers evidence of ancient lakes and rivers on Mars, providing new insights into the planet's past habitability and search for life.

## Event 10: Russian Cosmonauts Conduct Spacewalk at ISS

Russian cosmonauts Alexander Skvortsov and Maxim Surayev conduct a spacewalk outside the International Space Station (ISS) to perform maintenance tasks and prepare the station for future missions.
