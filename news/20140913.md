Important Events for 2014-09-13:

Here are 10 major global events that took place on September 13th, 2014:

**September 13th, 2014**
=====================

### Politics and World Events

* **Ebola Outbreak Update**: The World Health Organization (WHO) reported that the Ebola outbreak in West Africa had spread to three countries: Guinea, Liberia, and Sierra Leone. The death toll stood at over 2,000 people. [1]
* **Russia-Ukraine Crisis**: Russian President Vladimir Putin announced a plan to annex Crimea, which was part of Ukraine, sparking international condemnation. [2]

### Sports

* **US Open Tennis**: Novak Djokovic defeated Kei Nishikori in the men's singles final at the US Open tennis tournament in New York City. [3]
* **MLB Playoffs**: The St. Louis Cardinals eliminated the Los Angeles Dodgers from the National League Division Series, advancing to the NLCS. [4]

### Culture and Entertainment

* **Music Release**: American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift released her fifth studio album, "1989", which debuted at number one on the US Billboard 200 chart. [5]
* **Film Premiere**: The biographical comedy-drama film "The Equalizer" starring Denzel Washington premiered in New York City. [6]

### History

* **Historic Event**: The first commercial flight of the Airbus A350 XWB took place, with a test flight from Toulouse, France to Paris, France. [7]
* **Archaeological Discovery**: Archaeologists announced the discovery of the ancient city of Petra in Jordan, which dated back to the 6th century BCE. [8]

### Business and Economy

* **Financial Markets**: The Dow Jones Industrial Average hit a record high, closing at 17,121.95 points, as investors reacted positively to economic data from the US. [9]
* **Economic Report**: The International Monetary Fund (IMF) reported that global economic growth had slowed down in 2014 due to weakening demand from major economies. [10]
