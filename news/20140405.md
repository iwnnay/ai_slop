Important Events for 2014-04-05:

Here are 10 major global events that took place on April 5, 2014:

# 1: Russian Annexation of Crimea
Russian President Vladimir Putin signed a treaty to annex Crimea from Ukraine, completing the process started in March 2014.

# 2: Google Buys Nest Labs for $3.2 Billion
Google announced its acquisition of smart home company Nest Labs, founded by Tony Fadell and Matt Rogers, for $3.2 billion.

# 3: Boston Marathon Bombing Investigation Update
FBI released new evidence related to the April 15, 2013 Boston Marathon bombing, including a surveillance video showing Dzhokhar Tsarnaev, one of the suspects.

# 4: Ukraine Crisis Escalates
Ukrainian protesters seized government buildings in several cities as protests against President Viktor Yanukovych continued to escalate.

# 5: European Union Sanctions Russia
The EU imposed sanctions on Russian officials and individuals involved in the annexation of Crimea, mirroring similar actions taken by the United States.

# 6: NBA Playoffs Begin
The NBA playoffs started with the first round games between teams like the Chicago Bulls vs. the Washington Wizards, San Antonio Spurs vs. the Dallas Mavericks, and more.

# 7: Apple Buys Beats Electronics for $3 Billion
Apple announced its acquisition of headphones and music streaming company Beats Electronics, co-founded by Dr. Dre and Jimmy Iovine.

# 8: Japan's Prime Minister Resigns
Japanese Prime Minister Shinzo Abe resigned due to health reasons, citing his need to focus on his treatment for a recurring illness.

# 9: New Species of Ancient Human Discovered
Scientists announced the discovery of a new species of ancient human, Homo naledi, found in South Africa's Cradle of Humankind.

# 10: Russian Cosmonauts Conduct Spacewalk
Russian cosmonauts Oleg Artemyev and Alexander Skvortsov conducted a spacewalk on the International Space Station, performing maintenance tasks and conducting scientific experiments.

These events were significant in their respective fields - politics, technology, sports, science, and more.
