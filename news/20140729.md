## Global Events

### Israel-Gaza Conflict Intensifies

* Pro-Russian separatists in eastern Ukraine seized government buildings in Donetsk and Luhansk
* Ukrainian authorities vowed to take action against the rebels

* The World Health Organization (WHO) reported a total of 1,795 cases and 944 deaths from Ebola in West Africa
* Medical teams were deployed to Guinea, Liberia, and Sierra Leone to combat the outbreak
