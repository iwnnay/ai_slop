Important Events for 2014-05-06:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on May 6th, 2014:

# 1: European Parliament Elections
---------------

May 6th marked the day for European parliamentary elections across the continent. The polls saw record low turnout in several countries, with many citizens choosing to abstain from voting.

# 2: Ukraine Protests Intensify
---------------------------

Tensions continued to rise in Ukraine as protesters clashed with government forces, leaving at least three people dead and over a hundred injured. The crisis deepened as pro-Russian separatists seized key buildings in eastern regions.

# 3: SpaceX Falcon 9 Launch
---------------------------

SpaceX successfully launched its Falcon 9 rocket from Cape Canaveral, Florida, carrying the Dragon spacecraft into orbit. This marked the company's third resupply mission to the International Space Station for NASA.

# 4: Boston Marathon Bomber Sentenced
-----------------------------------

Dzhokhar Tsarnaev, one of the Boston Marathon bombers, was sentenced to death without possibility of parole. The 2013 bombing killed three people and left many more injured.

# 5: US Senate Approves Cybersecurity Bill
-----------------------------------------

The United States Senate passed a cybersecurity bill aimed at improving the nation's defenses against cyber threats. The legislation aimed to boost information sharing between government agencies and private companies.

# 6: EU Reaches Deal on Fishing Quotas
-------------------------------

After months of negotiations, European Union member states reached an agreement on fishing quotas for the 2014-15 period. This deal aimed at ensuring sustainable fishing practices while balancing the interests of different countries.

# 7: Russia-US Relations Continue to Deteriorate
--------------------------------------------

The diplomatic spat between Russia and the United States over Ukraine continued, with both sides imposing sanctions against each other's officials.

# 8: Chinese Premier Visits Latin America
-----------------------------------------

Chinese Premier Li Keqiang began a high-profile visit to Latin America, aiming to strengthen trade ties and increase economic cooperation between China and the region.

# 9: Indian General Election Underway
-----------------------------

India embarked on its massive general election, with over 814 million eligible voters participating in this year's polls. The country was poised for a new government following the expiration of the current administration's term.

# 10: World War II Veteran Dies at Age 108
-----------------------------------------

American World War II veteran and prisoner-of-war Herbert K. Pilcher passed away on May 6th, just days short of his 109th birthday. He was one of the oldest surviving POWs from the war.
