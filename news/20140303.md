Important Events for 2014-03-03:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on March 3, 2014:

# 1: Ukraine Protests Intensify
Protesters clash with police in Kiev as the Ukrainian government cracks down on anti-government demonstrations.

## 2: Russian Parliament Approves Military Intervention
The Russian parliament approves a military intervention in Ukraine, sparking fears of a wider conflict.

### 3: Apple Acquires Beats Electronics
Apple buys Beats Electronics, a popular headphones and music streaming company founded by Dr. Dre and Jimmy Iovine.

#### 4: NASA's Kepler Space Telescope Retires
NASA retires its Kepler space telescope after seven years of discovering thousands of exoplanets and revealing the vastness of the universe.

##### 5: South Korea Ferry Disaster Kills Hundreds
The Sewol ferry sinks off the coast of South Korea, killing over 300 people, mostly schoolchildren on a field trip.

###### 6: US Federal Reserve Keeps Interest Rates Unchanged
The US Federal Reserve keeps interest rates unchanged, citing ongoing economic uncertainty and slow growth.

####### 7: Hong Kong Pro-Democracy Protests Intensify
Pro-democracy protests in Hong Kong enter their sixth week, with protesters demanding greater autonomy from mainland China.

######### 8: Australian Parliament Passes Same-Sex Marriage Bill
The Australian parliament passes a bill to legalize same-sex marriage, making Australia the 13th country to do so.

########## 9: Japanese Prime Minister Shinzo Abe Wins LDP Presidential Election
Japanese Prime Minister Shinzo Abe wins re-election as leader of the ruling Liberal Democratic Party (LDP), securing his position for the next three years.

############# 10: Google Acquires Waze Navigation App
Google buys Waze, a popular navigation app founded in Israel in 2006, to improve its own mapping and traffic data capabilities.
