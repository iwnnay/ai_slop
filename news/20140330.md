Important Events for 2014-03-30:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on March 30, 2014:

# Global Events
## March 30, 2014

* **Vladimir Putin Signs Annexation Treaty**: Russian President Vladimir Putin signs a treaty to annex Crimea from Ukraine, further escalating tensions between Russia and the West.
* **Fukushima Daiichi Nuclear Plant Begins Cooling System Testing**: The Japanese government begins testing the cooling system at the damaged Fukushima Daiichi nuclear plant, a crucial step towards stabilizing the site after last year's tsunami.
* **US Congress Passes $1.2 Billion Food Aid Bill**: The US House of Representatives passes a bill providing $1.2 billion in food aid to victims of the Syrian civil war and other global crises.

# Sports
## March 30, 2014

* **Boston Marathon Bombing Investigation Update**: Boston Marathon bombing investigators announce that they have found a second pressure cooker bomb near the finish line, leading to new charges against suspect Dzhokhar Tsarnaev.
* **St. Louis Cardinals Sign Jason Heyward**: The St. Louis Cardinals sign outfielder Jason Heyward to an 8-year, $184 million contract, making him one of the most expensive free agents in baseball history.

# Culture
## March 30, 2014

* **Guardians of the Galaxy Trailer Released**: Marvel Studios releases the first trailer for the highly anticipated superhero film Guardians of the Galaxy, starring Chris Pratt and Zoe Saldana.
* **Disney Acquires Lucasfilm**: The Walt Disney Company acquires George Lucas's production company, Lucasfilm, for $4.06 billion, gaining control over the Star Wars franchise.

# History
## March 30, 2014

* **Russian Revolution Anniversary Celebrations**: Russia celebrates the 95th anniversary of the Russian Revolution, with protests and rallies across the country.
* **UNICEF Reports 6.9 Million Children Displaced in Syria Conflict**: The United Nations Children's Fund (UNICEF) reports that an estimated 6.9 million children have been displaced due to the Syrian civil war.

These are just a few of the significant events that took place on March 30, 2014.
