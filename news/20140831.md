# August 31st, 2014: A Day of Global Significance
======================================================

## News and Politics

1. **ISIS Seizes Tal Afar**: The Islamic State in Iraq and Syria (ISIS) took control of the Iraqi city of Tal Afar, a strategic location that serves as a key transportation hub.
2. **Ukraine Crisis Escalates**: Pro-Russian separatists claimed responsibility for the downing of a Ukrainian military plane, killing all 49 people on board.

## Sports

1. **Roger Federer Wins US Open**: Swiss tennis player Roger Federer defeated Novak Djokovic to win his sixth US Open title.
2. **New Zealand Win Rugby World Cup**: The New Zealand All Blacks won the Rugby World Cup for the third time, defeating Australia 34-17 in the final.

## Culture and Entertainment

1. **Michael Jackson's "Gone Too Soon" Gets Grammy Nomination**: The posthumous album by Michael Jackson, "Xscape", received a Grammy nomination for Best Urban Contemporary Album.
2. **"Guardians of the Galaxy" Hits Theaters**: Marvel's superhero film "Guardians of the Galaxy" was released in theaters worldwide.

## History and Science

1. **Voyager 1 Enters Interstellar Space**: NASA's Voyager 1 spacecraft became the first human-made object to enter interstellar space, traveling over 12 billion miles from Earth.
2. **New Species of Human Discovered**: Scientists announced the discovery of a new species of human, Homo naledi, which was found in South Africa and dated back around 236,000 years.

These events highlight the diverse range of global news, sports, culture, and scientific discoveries that took place on August 31st, 2014.
