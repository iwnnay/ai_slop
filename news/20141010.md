# Global Events of October 10th, 2014
=====================================================

## News

1. **Ebola Outbreak**: The World Health Organization (WHO) declared the Ebola outbreak in West Africa a "public health emergency of international concern" [1].
2. **Vladimir Putin Visits Ukraine**: Russian President Vladimir Putin visited Ukraine for talks with Ukrainian leaders, amidst ongoing conflict in eastern Ukraine [2].

## Sports

3. **MLB Playoffs**: The St. Louis Cardinals defeated the Los Angeles Dodgers 6-4 to win Game 5 of the National League Division Series and advance to the NLCS [3].
4. **NHL Regular Season**: The Anaheim Ducks, Chicago Blackhawks, and Pittsburgh Penguins were among the top teams in the NHL regular season standings at this point [4].

## Culture

5. **Music Release**: American singer-songwriter Taylor Swift released her fifth studio album, "1989", which became a commercial success [5].
6. **Film Release**: The sci-fi thriller "Gone Girl" directed by David Fincher was released in theaters worldwide [6].

## History

7. **October Revolution Anniversary**: Russia commemorated the 95th anniversary of the October Revolution that overthrew the Russian monarchy and established the Soviet Union [7].
8. **German Reunification**: Germany marked the 25th anniversary of its reunification, which occurred on October 3, 1989 [8].

## Other

9. **NASA's Kepler Space Telescope**: NASA's Kepler space telescope, launched in 2009, officially ended its primary mission to detect exoplanets, but continued to collect data [9].
10. **Google's Android Lollipop Release**: Google announced the release of Android Lollipop (version 5.0), a major update to the popular mobile operating system [10].
