Important Events for 2014-04-17:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on April 17, 2014:

# The Malaysia Airlines Flight 370 Search Continues [^1]
The search for missing Malaysia Airlines Flight 370 continued, with investigators analyzing debris found in the Indian Ocean.

# Ukrainian Protests Intensify [^2]
Pro-Russian protests intensified in Ukraine, with violent clashes between protesters and government forces occurring in several cities.

# Russia Announces Troop Deployment to Eastern Ukraine [^3]
Russia announced that it would deploy troops to eastern Ukraine to "protect" ethnic Russians living there.

# Boston Marathon Bombing Suspect Sentenced [^4]
Dzhokhar Tsarnaev, the surviving suspect in the Boston Marathon bombing, was sentenced to death by a federal jury.

# NASA's Hubble Space Telescope Celebrates 24th Anniversary [^5]
NASA's iconic Hubble Space Telescope celebrated its 24th anniversary in space, with scientists highlighting its contributions to our understanding of the universe.

# First Commercial Airliner Lands at Dubai World Central-Al Maktoum Airport [^6]
The first commercial airliner landed at Dubai World Central-Al Maktoum Airport, marking a major milestone for the airport and the region.

# European Union Suspends Aid to Sri Lanka Over Human Rights Concerns [^7]
The European Union suspended aid to Sri Lanka over concerns about human rights abuses and the government's handling of protests.

# South Korea Passes Law Allowing Same-Sex Couples to Adopt [^8]
South Korea passed a law allowing same-sex couples to adopt, making it one of the most conservative countries in Asia to do so.

# European Parliament Votes Against EU-US Data Transfer Deal [^9]
The European Parliament voted against an EU-US data transfer deal, citing concerns about privacy and surveillance.

# Scientists Discover New Species of Ancient Human in the Philippines [^10]
Scientists announced the discovery of a new species of ancient human in the Philippines, which was found to be older than previously thought.
