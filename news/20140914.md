Important Events for 2014-09-14:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on September 14, 2014:

**September 14, 2014**

### Global News

* **Ebola Outbreak**: The World Health Organization (WHO) declares the Ebola outbreak in West Africa to be a Public Health Emergency of International Concern (PHEIC).
* **Russia-Germany Gas Deal**: Russia and Germany sign a deal to expand the Nord Stream gas pipeline, worth $13 billion.
* **NATO Summit**: The NATO summit takes place in Wales, where leaders discuss the crisis in Ukraine and the threat posed by ISIS.

### Sports

* **MLB Playoffs**: The San Francisco Giants clinch their third World Series title in five years with a 3-0 win over the St. Louis Cardinals.
* **NFL Week 2**: The NFL season continues with games featuring the Green Bay Packers, Seattle Seahawks, and New England Patriots.

### Cultural

* **Theater Awards**: The Tony Awards are held at Radio City Music Hall in New York City, honoring the best of Broadway theater.
* **Music Festival**: The annual Austin City Limits Music Festival kicks off in Texas, featuring performances by OutKast, Beck, and Lorde.

### Historical

* **9/11 Memorial**: The 13th anniversary of the September 11 attacks is marked with memorial services and ceremonies at Ground Zero and other sites around the world.
* **WWI Centenary**: World War I commemorations take place across Europe to mark the centennial of the war's outbreak.

### Other

* **Space Exploration**: NASA's New Horizons spacecraft becomes the first to flyby Pluto, providing stunning images and data about the dwarf planet.
* **Cybersecurity**: The OpenSSL Heartbleed bug is discovered, affecting millions of websites and prompting a massive security patching effort.

These events represent some of the most significant news, sports, cultural, and historical happenings on September 14, 2014.
