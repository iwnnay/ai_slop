# Major Events of July 17, 2014
=====================================

## News and Politics

* **Eritrea: Human Rights Abuses**: The United Nations Commission of Inquiry on Human Rights in Eritrea releases a report detailing widespread human rights abuses in the country. [1]
* **Ebola Outbreak: Death Toll Rises**: The death toll from the Ebola outbreak in West Africa reaches 672, with over 20,000 suspected cases reported. [2]

## Sports

* **Tour de France: Stage 17**: Vincenzo Nibali of Italy wins stage 17 of the Tour de France, solidifying his overall lead in the general classification. [3]
* **MLB: Home Run Derby Winner**: Yoenis Céspedes wins the MLB All-Star Game Home Run Derby, hitting a tournament-record 32 home runs during the event. [4]

## Culture and Entertainment

* **Comic-Con International: San Diego**: The annual Comic-Con International convention opens in San Diego, California, featuring appearances by stars from popular TV shows and movies, including "Guardians of the Galaxy" and "The Walking Dead". [5]
* **Country Music Association Awards**: The 49th Annual Country Music Association Awards take place in Nashville, Tennessee, with winners including Keith Urban and Miranda Lambert. [6]

## History

* **World War I: Battle of Vauquois**: On July 17, 1915, the French army launches a series of attacks on German positions during World War I, known as the Battle of Vauquois. The battle is considered one of the most brutal and bloody of the war. [7]
