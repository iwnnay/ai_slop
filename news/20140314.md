Important Events for 2014-03-14:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on March 14, 2014:

# 1: Russia Annexes Crimea
===========================

Russia signed an agreement to annex Crimea from Ukraine, effectively taking control of the peninsula and sparking international outrage.

# 2: Malaysian Airlines Flight MH370 Disappearance
=====================================

The investigation into the disappearance of Malaysian Airlines Flight MH370 was ongoing on this day. The plane had gone missing on March 8, 2014, and authorities were still searching for any signs of wreckage or survivors.

# 3: Crimea Crisis Escalates
=========================

Pro-Russian protests turned violent in Ukraine's capital city of Kiev, leaving several people injured and sparking concerns about the escalating crisis in Crimea.

# 4: NASA's Kepler Space Telescope Retires
=====================================

After nine years of service, NASA announced that its Kepler space telescope would be retired due to a malfunction. The telescope had discovered thousands of exoplanets during its time in operation.

# 5: Ukraine Parliament Impeaches President Viktor Yanukovych
===============================

Ukraine's parliament voted to impeach President Viktor Yanukovych, who was accused of ordering the violent crackdown on protesters that left scores dead and hundreds injured.

# 6: Russian Forces Capture Simferopol, Crimea
=====================================

Russian forces captured the city of Simferopol, the capital of Crimea, sparking concerns about a full-scale invasion of Ukraine.

# 7: Australian Open Men's Singles Final
===============================

Stanislas Wawrinka defeated Novak Djokovic in five sets to win the men's singles title at the Australian Open tennis tournament.

# 8: US Senate Confirms New Secretary of Defense
==============================

The US Senate confirmed Chuck Hagel as the new Secretary of Defense, replacing Leon Panetta in the role.

# 9: Israel and Palestine Agree on Gaza Ceasefire
=====================================

Israel and Palestinian militant groups agreed to a ceasefire following an escalation of violence in the Gaza Strip that had left several people dead.

# 10: SpaceX Launches Falcon 9 Rocket with ORBCOMM OG2 Satellites
=============================

SpaceX successfully launched its Falcon 9 rocket into space, carrying a payload of ORBCOMM OG2 satellites designed to provide global wireless connectivity.
