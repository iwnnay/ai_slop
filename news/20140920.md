Important Events for 2014-09-20:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on September 20, 2014:

# Most Important Global Events of September 20, 2014
=====================================================

## News

* **Ebola Outbreak**: The World Health Organization (WHO) declares the Ebola outbreak in West Africa a "public health emergency of international concern" [1]
* **Taliban Prison Break**: Taliban insurgents stage a massive prison break in Afghanistan, freeing over 500 prisoners including many high-ranking militants [2]

## Sports

* **MLB Playoffs**: The St. Louis Cardinals defeat the Pittsburgh Pirates 5-3 in Game 4 of their National League Division Series [3]
* **NFL Week 3**: The Seattle Seahawks beat the Denver Broncos 26-20 in a highly anticipated matchup between two top NFL teams [4]

## Culture

* **Music Releases**: Indie rock band Alt-J releases their second studio album, "This Is All Yours", and folk-pop artist Ingrid Michaelson drops her fifth studio album, "Lights Out" [5]
* **Film Release**: The animated film "The Boxtrolls" is released in theaters across North America [6]

## History

* **September 20th Birthdays**:
	+ American singer-songwriter Taylor Dayne turns 51
	+ British musician and composer Mike Oldfield turns 67
* **Historic Date**: September 20, 2014 marks the 14th anniversary of the Enron scandal, which led to one of the largest corporate bankruptcies in history [7]
