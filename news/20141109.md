Important Events for 2014-11-09:

Here are the top 10 global events that took place on November 9th, 2014:

# 1: ISIS Releases Video of Beheaded Journalist Steven Sotloff
============================================================

On this date, ISIS released a video showing the beheading of American journalist Steven Sotloff. The murder was part of a series of brutal killings carried out by the terrorist group.

# 2: European Parliament Passes Resolution on Ukraine Crisis
==========================================================

In response to the ongoing crisis in Ukraine, the European Parliament passed a resolution calling for a peaceful settlement and increased pressure on Russia to respect Ukraine's sovereignty.

# 3: NASA's Kepler Space Telescope Retires After 9 Years of Service
================================================================

After nine years of exploring the cosmos, NASA announced that its Kepler space telescope had completed its mission. The spacecraft was instrumental in discovering thousands of exoplanets.

# 4: Hong Kong Pro-Democracy Protesters Clash with Police
=====================================================

As the pro-democracy movement gained momentum in Hong Kong, protesters clashed with police on November 9th. The unrest highlighted concerns over government transparency and the city's future as a semi-autonomous territory.

# 5: FIFA Announces 2018 World Cup Venue Cities
==================================================

The Fédération Internationale de Football Association (FIFA) announced the cities that would host the 2018 FIFA World Cup in Russia. The event featured 11 host cities, with Moscow and St. Petersburg serving as co-hosts.

# 6: German Chancellor Angela Merkel Wins Fourth Term in Office
=====================================================

Angela Merkel secured a fourth term as Germany's Chancellor, leading her Christian Democratic Union (CDU) to victory in federal elections. Her tenure marked significant developments in European politics and international relations.

# 7: The Weeknd Releases His Third Studio Album, 'Beauty Behind the Madness'
=================================================================

Canadian singer-songwriter Abel Tesfaye, better known as The Weeknd, released his critically acclaimed third studio album, Beauty Behind the Madness. The album featured hits like "Earned It" and "The Hills."

# 8: NASA's Orion Spacecraft Completes Uncrewed Test Flight
=====================================================

NASA successfully conducted an uncrewed test flight of its Orion spacecraft, marking a significant milestone in the development of the vehicle designed for deep space missions.

# 9: European Court of Human Rights Rules Against Russia on Crimea Annexation
=================================================================

In a landmark decision, the European Court of Human Rights ruled that Russia's annexation of Crimea was illegal and violated human rights. The ruling emphasized the importance of international law in resolving territorial disputes.

# 10: Sony Pictures Cancels Release of 'The Interview'
=====================================================

Following threats from hackers linked to North Korea, Sony Pictures cancelled the release of The Interview, a comedy film that jokingly depicted the assassination of Kim Jong-un.
