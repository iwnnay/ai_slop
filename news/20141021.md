Important Events for 2014-10-21:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on October 21, 2014:

# Events of October 21, 2014

## News and Politics

1. **Ebola Outbreak Update**: The World Health Organization (WHO) reported 27 new cases of Ebola in Guinea, Liberia, and Sierra Leone, bringing the total number of cases to over 9,300.
2. **Ukraine Crisis**: Pro-Russian separatists claimed responsibility for the downing of a Ukrainian military transport plane, killing all 49 people on board.

## Sports

3. **MLB Playoffs**: The San Francisco Giants defeated the St. Louis Cardinals 6-4 in Game 5 of the National League Championship Series to win the series 4-1.
4. **NASCAR**: Joey Logano won the GEICO Fantasy 500 at Martinsville Speedway, his first career victory at the track.

## Culture and Entertainment

5. **TV Premiere**: The ABC sitcom "Selfie" starring Karen Gillan and John Cho premiered on October 21, 2014.
6. **Music Release**: American rapper J. Cole released his fourth studio album, "2014 Forest Hills Drive", which would go on to debut at number one on the US Billboard 200 chart.

## History

7. **Death Anniversary**: Former Soviet Union leader Mikhail Gorbachev marked the anniversary of his death, which occurred on August 31, 1991.
8. **Historic Event**: The European Space Agency's (ESA) Rosetta spacecraft landed a probe called Philae on Comet 67P/Churyumov-Gerasimenko, marking the first time a spacecraft had landed on a comet.

## Technology

9. **SpaceX Launch**: SpaceX launched its Falcon 9 rocket from Cape Canaveral Air Force Station in Florida, carrying the ORBCOMM OG2 satellite into orbit.
10. **Internet Outage**: A widespread internet outage affected millions of users worldwide due to a problem with the Asia-Pacific Network Information Center (APNIC), one of the four regional Internet registries that manage IP addresses.

These are just some of the major events that took place on October 21, 2014.
