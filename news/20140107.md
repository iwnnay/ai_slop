Here are 10 major global events that took place on January 7, 2014:

**January 7, 2014**

### News and Politics

* [1] **North Korea executes Kim Jong-un's uncle**: Jang Song-thaek, the former vice chairman of the National Defense Commission, is executed on charges of treason. (Source: BBC)
* [2] **US and EU impose sanctions on North Korea**: The United States and European Union impose new sanctions on North Korea in response to its nuclear test and human rights abuses.
* [3] **Indian Prime Minister Manmohan Singh resigns**: Manmohan Singh submits his resignation as the Prime Minister of India, bringing an end to his 10-year tenure.

### Sports

* [5] **NBA: Cleveland Cavaliers acquire Andrew Bynum**: The Cleveland Cavaliers trade for center Andrew Bynum, hoping to bolster their frontcourt.

### History and Science

* [9] **Asteroid 2014 AA passes close to Earth**: The asteroid, estimated to be about 100 feet (30 meters) in diameter, safely passes within 1,300 miles (2,100 kilometers) of Earth.

### Other
