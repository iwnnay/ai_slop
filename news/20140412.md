Here are 10 major global events that occurred on April 12, 2014:

# Events of April 12, 2014

**Politics and International Relations**

* **Vladimir Putin signs bill to grant himself power to appoint Russia's prime minister**: Russian President Vladimir Putin signed a bill giving him the power to appoint the country's prime minister without consulting parliament. [1]
* **FARC rebels release Colombian politician held captive since 2002**: The Revolutionary Armed Forces of Colombia (FARC) released Ingrid Betancourt, a former Colombian senator and presidential candidate, who had been held captive for over 11 years. [2]

**Sports**

* **Boston Marathon bombing suspect Dzhokhar Tsarnaev sentenced to death**: Dzhokhar Tsarnaev was formally sentenced to death by a federal jury in Boston for his role in the April 2013 Boston Marathon bombing, which killed three people and injured hundreds. [3]
* **Arsenal wins FA Cup title with 3-2 victory over Hull City**: Arsenal FC won their 11th FA Cup title by defeating Hull City 3-2 at Wembley Stadium in London. [4]

**Culture**

* **Google announces Google Play Music All Access subscription service**: Google launched its music streaming service, Google Play Music All Access, which allowed users to access millions of songs and create personalized playlists. [5]
* **The Lego Movie becomes top-grossing animated film of 2014**: The animated film "The Lego Movie" surpassed $200 million at the box office, making it the highest-grossing animated film of 2014. [6]

**History**

* **Russian cosmonauts return from ISS after six-month stay**: Russian cosmonauts Alexander Skvortsov and Mikhail Kornienko returned to Earth after a six-month stay on the International Space Station (ISS). [7]
* **Historic tornado outbreak in the United States**: A severe thunderstorm outbreak produced a record 219 tornadoes across the United States, with the majority occurring in Texas, Oklahoma, Kansas, and Missouri. [8]

**Other**

* **Tornado warning system upgraded in Japan**: The Japanese government upgraded its early warning system for tornadoes, allowing for faster and more accurate warnings to be issued to residents. [9]
* **Apple announces Apple Watch smartwatch**: Apple announced its new smartwatch, the Apple Watch, which was scheduled to be released later that year. [10]
