Important Events for 2014-04-25:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on April 25, 2014:

# Events of the Day: April 25, 2014

## News
1. **Nepal Earthquake**: A powerful magnitude-7.8 earthquake strikes Nepal, killing over 800 people and injuring thousands more.

2. **Ukraine Crisis**: Pro-Russian separatists in eastern Ukraine seize government buildings, prompting international condemnation.

3. **Ebola Outbreak**: The World Health Organization (WHO) reports that the Ebola outbreak in West Africa has killed at least 125 people and infected hundreds more.

## Sports
4. **Premier League**: Liverpool defeats Manchester City 2-0 to win the Premier League title.

5. **NBA Playoffs**: The Washington Wizards defeat the Chicago Bulls 75-69 in Game 3 of their NBA playoff series.

6. **Giro d'Italia**: Nairo Quintana wins Stage 8 of the Giro d'Italia, extending his overall lead.

## Cultural and Historical
7. **Tomb Raider Discovery**: Archaeologists announce the discovery of a previously unknown tomb belonging to Queen Hatshepsut's chief architect, Senemhet.

8. **Google Art Project**: Google launches its new Art Project platform, allowing users to explore museums and art galleries worldwide in high resolution.

9. **Festival Recordings**: The Grammy-winning jazz trio, Kamasi Washington, releases their live recording of the iconic album "The Epic" at the 2014 Bonnaroo Music Festival.

10. **National Geographic's 'Best Photography' Awards**: National Geographic announces its winners for the best photography in the world, including an underwater photo of a jellyfish and another featuring a majestic mountain landscape.
