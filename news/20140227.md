Important Events for 2014-02-27:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on February 27, 2014:

**Global Events**
===============

February 27, 2014 was marked by several significant global events. Here are some of the most notable ones:

### 1. Ukraine Protests Intensify

* Thousands of protesters clashed with police in Kiev as the Ukrainian government announced a state of emergency in response to growing protests demanding President Viktor Yanukovych's resignation.

### 2. South Korea Ferry Sinking

* The Sewol ferry, carrying over 470 people, mostly high school students, sank off the coast of South Korea, resulting in one of the country's deadliest maritime disasters.

### 3. Venezuela Protests Erupt

* Anti-government protests broke out in Caracas and other cities across Venezuela as opposition leaders demanded the resignation of President Nicolás Maduro amid economic crisis and political turmoil.

### 4. Russia and Ukraine Reach Gas Deal

* After months of tensions, Russian gas giant Gazprom and Ukrainian energy company Naftogaz signed a deal to end a dispute over natural gas supplies, ensuring steady fuel deliveries for Ukraine's winter heating season.

### 5. North Korea Tests Rocket Engine

* North Korea announced the successful test-firing of a rocket engine designed for its long-range ballistic missiles, amid ongoing tensions with South Korea and the United States.
