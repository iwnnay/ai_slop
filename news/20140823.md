# Events of August 23rd, 2014

## News

### Gaza Conflict
Israel and Hamas agreed to a temporary ceasefire in the Gaza conflict, which had been ongoing since July. The agreement was reached after intense diplomatic efforts.

### Ukraine Crisis
Ukraine's parliament voted to grant immunity to President Petro Poroshenko, amid concerns over his handling of the crisis in eastern Ukraine.

### Ebola Outbreak
The World Health Organization (WHO) reported that a nurse who had treated Ebola patients in Sierra Leone had contracted the virus while wearing protective gear. This was the first recorded case of transmission through contact with someone wearing personal protective equipment (PPE).

The Boston Red Sox and Los Angeles Dodgers played a home run derby at Fenway Park, with both teams hitting 12 home runs each.

### Tennis
Marin Cilic won the men's singles title at the US Open tennis tournament by defeating Kei Nishikori in straight sets (6-3, 6-3, 6-3).
