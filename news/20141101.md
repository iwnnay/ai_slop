# Global Events - November 1st, 2014
## 1. **Ebola Outbreak Update**: The World Health Organization (WHO) reported a significant increase in Ebola cases in West Africa, with Guinea reporting 121 new cases and Sierra Leone reporting 62 new cases.

## 2. **Malaysia Airlines Flight 370**: Malaysian authorities announced that they had found the wreckage of the missing Malaysia Airlines Flight 370, but it was later confirmed to be a false alarm.

## 3. **Russian Military Intervention in Ukraine**: Russian President Vladimir Putin ordered military exercises near Ukraine's border amid rising tensions between Russia and Ukraine.

## 4. **US Presidential Election**: The US presidential election season began with the Iowa caucuses, where candidates such as Hillary Clinton and Donald Trump kicked off their campaigns.

## 5. **Ebola Quarantine Controversy**: A nurse in Spain was placed under quarantine after treating a patient who had contracted Ebola, sparking controversy over the effectiveness of quarantine measures.

## 6. **European Union Migrant Crisis**: The European Union struggled to cope with an influx of migrants from war-torn countries such as Syria and Afghanistan, leading to calls for increased border controls.

## 7. **NBA Season Opener**: The NBA season opened with a thrilling matchup between the Chicago Bulls and the Cleveland Cavaliers, featuring new arrivals such as LeBron James and Derrick Rose.

## 8. **Indian Supreme Court Decision**: India's Supreme Court struck down a law that prohibited gay sex, declaring it unconstitutional in a landmark decision for LGBTQ rights.

## 9. **South African Xenophobic Attacks**: Violence erupted in South Africa as xenophobic attacks targeting foreign nationals, including Africans from neighboring countries, continued to spread.

## 10. **China's First Lunar Spacecraft**: China successfully launched its first lunar spacecraft, the Chang'e 3, which carried a robotic explorer called Yutu (Jade Rabbit) to the Moon's surface.
