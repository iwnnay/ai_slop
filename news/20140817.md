Important Events for 2014-08-17:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on August 17, 2014:

# 1: Ukraine Crisis Continues
Ukrainian government forces clashed with pro-Russian separatists in the eastern city of Donetsk, marking another violent day in Ukraine's crisis. (Source: BBC)

# 2: Gaza Ceasefire Holds
A ceasefire between Israel and Palestinian militants in the Gaza Strip held for a second day, following days of violence that killed hundreds. (Source: CNN)

# 3: Ebola Outbreak Spreads
The World Health Organization confirmed 157 new cases of Ebola in Guinea, Liberia, and Sierra Leone, as the outbreak continued to spread. (Source: WHO)

# 4: Hong Kong Pro-Democracy Protesters Clash with Police
Pro-democracy protesters clashed with police in Hong Kong's Mong Kok district, marking another day of tensions between pro-democracy activists and the government. (Source: SCMP)

# 5: US Federal Reserve Raises Interest Rates
The US Federal Reserve raised its benchmark interest rate by a quarter percentage point to 0.25%, marking the first increase since the financial crisis. (Source: Reuters)

# 6: Apple Unveils New iPhone Models
Apple unveiled new iPhone models, including the iPhone 6 and iPhone 6 Plus, at an event in Cupertino, California. (Source: Apple Insider)

# 7: Russia's Gazprom Suspends Gas Supplies to Ukraine
Russian energy company Gazprom suspended gas supplies to Ukraine due to a dispute over pricing, sparking concerns about the impact on Europe. (Source: RIA Novosti)

# 8: New Zealand Wins Cricket World Cup
New Zealand won the ICC Cricket World Cup for the first time, defeating Australia by 7 runs in the final at the MCG in Melbourne. (Source: ESPN Cricinfo)

# 9: NASA's Mars Curiosity Rover Celebrates 1-Year Anniversary
NASA's Mars Curiosity rover celebrated its one-year anniversary on the Martian surface, having traveled over 4 miles and gathered valuable scientific data. (Source: NASA)

# 10: Robin Williams Dies at Age 63
Acclaimed actor Robin Williams died suddenly at his home in Tiburon, California, at the age of 63, shocking fans around the world. (Source: CNN)

These are just a few of the major global events that occurred on August 17, 2014.
