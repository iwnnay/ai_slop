# Events of Note for July 9, 2014
=====================================


1. **Israel-Gaza Conflict**: The Israeli military launches airstrikes against Gaza Strip in response to rockets fired by Palestinian militants.

5. **Syrian Civil War**: Syrian government forces capture the city of Raqqa from Islamic State (ISIS) militants.
6. **Australian Politics**: Australian Prime Minister Kevin Rudd announces his resignation as leader of the Labor Party and as Prime Minister, effective September 18th.
7. **NASA Spaceflight**: NASA's SpaceX Dragon spacecraft successfully docks with the International Space Station (ISS), carrying supplies and scientific experiments.
8. **European Economy**: The European Commission reduces its growth forecast for the EU economy, citing sluggish recovery and high unemployment rates.
9. **Indian Film Industry**: Legendary Indian filmmaker Yash Chopra passes away at the age of 80, leaving behind a legacy of iconic films like "Deewar" and "Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge".
10. **Japanese Economy**: Japan's economy contracts by 1.7% in the second quarter due to weak consumption and investment, marking its first recession since 2012.
