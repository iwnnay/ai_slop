# Events of May 20, 2014
=====================================



### Politics and World News

* **Ukraine Crisis**: Pro-Russian separatists seize government buildings in the eastern Ukrainian city of Donetsk, escalating tensions in the crisis.
* **Syrian Civil War**: The Syrian army captures the strategic city of Qusair from rebel forces, a major blow to the opposition.

### Sports

* **NBA Playoffs**: The Indiana Pacers defeat the Washington Wizards 102-82 in Game 4 of their Eastern Conference semifinal series.
* **MLS**: The New York Red Bulls score three unanswered goals to beat Sporting Kansas City 3-1 at Red Bull Arena.

### Entertainment and Culture

* **Music**: American singer-songwriter Ed Sheeran releases his second studio album, "x" (pronounced "multiply"), featuring the hit single "Sing".
* **Film**: The sci-fi action film "X-Men: Days of Future Past" is released in theaters, starring Hugh Jackman and James McAvoy.

### History

* **Viking Era**: Archaeologists discover a Viking-age temple at Trelleborg, Denmark, dating back to the 10th century.
* **Civil Rights Movement**: On this day in 1963, Civil Rights activist Medgar Evers is assassinated outside his home in Jackson, Mississippi.

### Technology

* **Space Exploration**: NASA's Kepler space telescope, launched in 2009, runs out of fuel and ceases operation after discovering over 2,600 exoplanets.
* **Cybersecurity**: The US Federal Trade Commission (FTC) files a complaint against Snapchat, alleging the messaging app misled users about the disappearing nature of its messages.

These are just some of the significant events that occurred on May 20, 2014.
