Important Events for 2014-04-09:

Here are the top 10 global events that occurred on April 9, 2014:

# **Malaysia Airlines Flight 370: Search Efforts Continue**
The search for Malaysia Airlines Flight 370 continues after it vanished on March 8, 2014. On this day, Australian authorities announced that they had found a piece of debris that could be from the missing plane.

# **Ukraine Crisis: Pro-Russian Forces Seize Government Buildings**
Pro-Russian forces in eastern Ukraine seize government buildings in several cities, escalating tensions between Russia and Ukraine. This marked a significant escalation of the crisis, which would eventually lead to Russia's annexation of Crimea.

# **NBA Playoffs: Pacers Eliminate Hawks with 92-80 Win**
In the NBA playoffs, the Indiana Pacers defeat the Atlanta Hawks 92-80 to take a 3-2 lead in their first-round series. The win marked the Pacers' fifth consecutive playoff victory over the Hawks.

# **Google Acquires Wimba for $100 Million**
Google acquires online learning platform Wimba for $100 million, expanding its presence in the education technology market.

# **Australian Parliament Passes Carbon Pricing Legislation**
The Australian Parliament passes legislation to reintroduce a carbon pricing system, aimed at reducing greenhouse gas emissions and meeting international climate targets.

# **Pakistan Supreme Court Upholds Death Sentence of Militant Leader**
The Pakistan Supreme Court upholds the death sentence of militant leader Malik Ishaq, who was linked to numerous terrorist attacks in the country.

# **Turkish Government Approves Constitution Reform Bill**
Turkey's government approves a constitution reform bill aimed at expanding presidential powers and strengthening the role of parliament.

# **NASA's LADEE Lunar Mission Ends with Crash on Moon**
NASA's LADEE (Lunar Atmosphere and Dust Environment Explorer) mission comes to an end as the spacecraft crashes on the Moon's surface. The mission was designed to study the Moon's thin atmosphere and dust environment.

# **Russia Lifts Sanctions Against Georgia**
Russia lifts sanctions against Georgia, easing tensions between the two countries after a brief conflict in 2008.

# **FIFA World Rankings: Spain Retains Top Spot**
In the FIFA world rankings, Spain retains its top spot, followed by Germany, Argentina, and Colombia. This marked the 15th consecutive month that Spain held onto the number one ranking.
