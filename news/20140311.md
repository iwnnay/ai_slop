Important Events for 2014-03-11:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on March 11, 2014:

# Events of March 11, 2014

## Russia Annexes Crimea

The Russian military took control of key infrastructure and strategic locations in Ukraine's autonomous region of Crimea. This marked the beginning of Russia's annexation of Crimea.

## Malaysia Airlines Flight 370 Disappearance

Malaysia Airlines Flight 370 disappeared en route from Kuala Lumpur to Beijing with 239 people on board. The flight was last detected over the South China Sea, and despite extensive searches, its fate remains unknown.

## Ukraine Crisis Escalates

Pro-Russian protesters stormed government buildings in several cities across eastern Ukraine, prompting Ukraine's parliament to declare Crimea a "terrorist state." The crisis escalated tensions between Ukraine and Russia.

## Twitter Co-Founder Biz Stone Steps Down

Biz Stone, one of the co-founders of Twitter, announced his departure from the company. Stone had been involved with Twitter since its early days but stepped down due to personal and professional reasons.

## NBA Trade Deadline

The 2014 NBA trade deadline saw several significant transactions, including the blockbuster deal between the Los Angeles Lakers and the Chicago Bulls that sent Pau Gasol to Chicago.

## Russian Hockey Players Strike Back Against Canadian Team

During a World Championship hockey game in Minsk, Belarus, Russian players reacted angrily to what they perceived as unfair penalties against them. The incident sparked controversy and debate about sportsmanship.

## Japan Marks 7th Anniversary of Earthquake and Tsunami

Japan observed the seventh anniversary of the devastating earthquake and tsunami that struck on March 11, 2011, killing over 15,000 people and triggering a nuclear crisis at Fukushima Daiichi.

## China's Xi Jinping Visits Pakistan

Chinese President Xi Jinping visited Pakistan to strengthen bilateral ties and sign several key agreements. The trip aimed to enhance cooperation in areas like energy, infrastructure, and trade.

## India's Modi Government Completes 100 Days

The Narendra Modi-led government in India completed its first 100 days in office, marking a significant milestone in the country's political calendar.

## NASA's Orion Spacecraft Enters Final Assembly Phase

NASA's new Orion spacecraft entered the final assembly phase at Kennedy Space Center in preparation for its maiden voyage to deep space. The mission aimed to send astronauts beyond low-Earth orbit for the first time since Apollo.
