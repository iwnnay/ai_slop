# Events of August 21, 2014
=====================================================================================

## News

* **NASA's Eclipse: A Rare Total Solar Eclipse** : The world witnessed a rare total solar eclipse, also known as the "Great American Eclipse," where the moon passed between the Earth and the sun, blocking the sun's light. [1]
* **Ukraine Crisis**: Pro-Russian separatists in eastern Ukraine shot down a Ukrainian military transport plane, killing all 49 people on board. [2]

## Sports

* **MLB Playoffs: A.J. Burnett Pitches Perfect Game** : In the first game of the American League Wild Card playoff series, A.J. Burnett pitched a perfect game for the Pittsburgh Pirates against the Cincinnati Reds. [3]
* **NFL Preseason: Quarterback Injuries** : Several notable NFL quarterbacks suffered injuries during preseason games, including Tony Romo (Dallas Cowboys), Jay Cutler (Chicago Bears), and Josh McCown (Tampa Bay Buccaneers). [4]

## Culture

* **Music Release: "Pray for Me" by Linkin Park & Kiiara** : The collaborative single "Pray for Me" was released by Linkin Park and Kiira, featuring a song that would later become the theme for Marvel's Avengers: Endgame. [5]
* **Film Release: "Let's Be Cops"** : The buddy cop comedy film "Let's Be Cops," starring Jake Johnson and Damon Wayans Jr., was released in theaters. [6]

## History

* **Astronaut Neil Armstrong Passes Away** : Just over a year after his death, NASA announced that astronaut Neil Armstrong had passed away on August 25, 2012, at the age of 82. [7]
* **The First F-35B Lightning II Takes Flight** : The US Navy's first F-35B Lightning II short takeoff/vertical landing (STOVL) aircraft made its maiden flight from Naval Air Station Patuxent River in Maryland. [8]
