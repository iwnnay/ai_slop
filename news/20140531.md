# May 31st, 2014 - A Day of Note
=====================================================

### News

1. **European Parliament election**: The European Parliament elections took place in several countries, with the European Union holding its eighth parliamentary election.
2. **Syrian Civil War**: The Syrian Air Force conducted airstrikes on the city of Idlib, killing at least 40 people and injuring many more.

### Sports

3. **UEFA Champions League Final**: Real Madrid defeated Atlético Madrid 4-1 in the UEFA Champions League Final, held at the Estádio da Luz in Lisbon, Portugal.
4. **MLB**: The Kansas City Royals played their first-ever game at the newly renovated Kauffman Stadium.

### Cultural

5. **Music Festival**: The Electric Daisy Carnival (EDC) music festival took place in Las Vegas, Nevada, featuring performers like Calvin Harris and Zedd.
6. **Art Exhibition**: The "China's First Emperor: The Man Who Unified China" exhibition opened at the National Museum of Scotland in Edinburgh.

### Historical

7. **World War II**: On May 31st, 1942, German troops launched a surprise attack on the French port city of Saint-Nazaire.
8. **Cold War**: In 1964, Soviet Premier Nikita Khrushchev visited the United States, marking a rare high-level diplomatic encounter between the two superpowers.

### Other

9. **Space Exploration**: The European Space Agency's Gaia spacecraft, designed to create the most precise 3D map of the Milky Way galaxy, was launched into orbit.
10. **Weather**: A severe weather outbreak affected parts of the United States, with multiple tornadoes and damaging winds reported in several states.
