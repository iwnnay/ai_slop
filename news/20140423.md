Important Events for 2014-04-23:

Here are 10 major global events that occurred on April 23, 2014:

# 1: **Sri Lanka frees Tamil Tiger leaders**
The Sri Lankan government freed five former Tamil Tiger rebels from prison, in an effort to improve relations between the government and the minority Tamil population.

# 2: **Hagel visits Ukraine**
U.S. Defense Secretary Chuck Hagel arrived in Ukraine for a visit aimed at reassuring the country about U.S. support amid tensions with Russia over the situation in Crimea.

# 3: **Venezuela protests turn deadly**
Clashes between protesters and security forces in Caracas, Venezuela, left three people dead and many others injured as anti-government demonstrations continued to spread.

# 4: **NASA's Dawn spacecraft enters orbit around Ceres**
The NASA spacecraft Dawn entered into orbit around the dwarf planet Ceres, becoming the first spacecraft to do so. The mission aimed to study the surface and subsurface of Ceres.

# 5: **FIFA suspends Mohamed Aboutrika from international football**
Egyptian midfielder Mohamed Aboutrika was suspended by FIFA for six months after he announced his retirement from international football in protest at Egypt's military-backed government.

# 6: **Pope Francis canonizes Popes John XXIII and John Paul II**
The Pope Francis canonized two popes, John XXIII and John Paul II, becoming the first pope to do so since the death of Pope John Paul I in 1978.

# 7: **Miley Cyrus cancels concert in Georgia due to gay conversion therapy ban protest**
Pop star Miley Cyrus cancelled her concert in Macon, Georgia, after she found out that the local government had banned gay conversion therapy.
