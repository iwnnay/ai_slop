**December 14, 2014**
=====================

### News

1. **Pope Francis Appoints New Cardinals**: Pope Francis appointed 16 new cardinals from around the world, including several from the United States and Europe, in a bid to make the Catholic Church more diverse and representative of the global Church.
2. **NASA's OSIRIS-REx Spacecraft Launched**: NASA launched its OSIRIS-REx spacecraft on a five-year journey to explore the asteroid Bennu and return samples to Earth.
3. **US Government Reports 288,000 New Jobs Created**: The US Bureau of Labor Statistics reported that the economy added 288,000 new jobs in November 2014, as part of a sustained recovery from the Great Recession.

### Sports
