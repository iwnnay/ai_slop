Important Events for 2014-07-02:

Here are 10 major events that occurred on July 2nd, 2014:

# **Ukraine Crisis**: Pro-Russian activists seized government buildings in several cities in eastern Ukraine, escalating tensions between Ukraine and Russia.

## **Nelson Mandela's Birthday Celebrations**: The world celebrated the birthday of Nelson Mandela, former President of South Africa and anti-apartheid activist, who would have turned 96 years old.

### **Tiger Woods' Comeback**: Tiger Woods announced his return to professional golf after a three-month hiatus due to back surgery, sparking speculation about his chances at the upcoming Open Championship.

#### **Ebola Outbreak Update**: The World Health Organization (WHO) reported that the Ebola outbreak in West Africa had spread to four countries: Guinea, Liberia, Sierra Leone, and Nigeria, with over 1,000 cases reported.

##### **US Drone Strike**: A US drone strike killed several Taliban militants in a compound in eastern Afghanistan, sparking concerns about civilian casualties.

###### **Russian Human Rights Activist Arrested**: Russian authorities arrested well-known human rights activist and journalist Sergei Nikitin for allegedly spreading false information about Russia's military presence in Ukraine.

####### **South Africa's Nkandla Scandal**: South African President Jacob Zuma faced mounting pressure to resign over allegations of corruption and misuse of public funds, with the opposition calling for his impeachment.

##### **Gaza-Israel Conflict Escalates**: The Israeli military launched airstrikes on Gaza, killing several Palestinians and sparking international condemnation, as tensions between Israel and Hamas escalated.

#### **Indian Floods**: Heavy rainfall caused widespread flooding in the Indian state of Jammu and Kashmir, leaving thousands stranded and dozens dead.

### **World Cup Qualifiers**: In soccer news, Argentina defeated Colombia 1-0 in a World Cup qualifying match, while Brazil drew 0-0 with Chile.
