Important Events for 2014-04-14:

Here are 10 major global events that took place on April 14, 2014:

# Events of Note: April 14, 2014
======================================================

1. **Volkswagen Emissions Scandal Unfolds**
-------------------------------

German automaker Volkswagen announces it will recall hundreds of thousands of vehicles worldwide after discovering that some models emitted excessive pollutants.

2. **NATO and Russia in Tensions Over Ukraine**
---------------------------------------------

The North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO) holds an emergency meeting to discuss the crisis in Ukraine, with concerns growing over Russian military involvement.

3. **Pope Francis Meets With Ecumenical Patriarch Bartholomew I**
---------------------------------------------------------

Pope Francis meets with the Ecumenical Patriarch of Constantinople, Bartholomew I, marking a significant step towards unity between the Roman Catholic and Eastern Orthodox churches.

4. **Miley Cyrus Performs at the Time 100 Gala**
--------------------------------------------

Pop star Miley Cyrus performs at the annual Time 100 gala in New York City, celebrating the world's most influential people.

5. **Boston Red Sox and Toronto Blue Jays Play a Rain-Delayed Game**
-------------------------------------------------------------

A rain delay forces the Boston Red Sox and Toronto Blue Jays to play their MLB game over two days, with the Red Sox ultimately emerging victorious.

6. **Sarajevo Marks 20th Anniversary of NATO Intervention in Bosnia**
----------------------------------------------------------------

The city of Sarajevo commemorates the 20th anniversary of NATO's military intervention in Bosnia and Herzegovina, aimed at ending the devastating Bosnian War.

7. **Google Acquires WIMM Labs**
-----------------------------

Google purchases wearable technology company WIMM Labs, marking another significant move by the tech giant into the world of wearables.

8. **The New York Times Reports on NSA Surveillance Program**
--------------------------------------------------------

The New York Times reveals new details about the National Security Agency's (NSA) surveillance program, including its broad scope and global reach.

9. **Nobel Peace Prize Winner Malala Yousafzai Speaks at Harvard University**
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

Taliban survivor and Nobel Peace Prize winner Malala Yousafzai delivers a powerful speech at Harvard University, urging young people to stand up against oppression and fight for their rights.

10. **NASA's Kepler Space Telescope Celebrates 4 Years in Orbit**
---------------------------------------------------------

NASA's Kepler space telescope marks four years of exploring the cosmos, having discovered thousands of exoplanets during its time in orbit around the Sun.
