Here are 10 major events that occurred on January 16, 2014:

# Events of January 16, 2014
==================================================

## Global News

1. **Syrian Civil War**: The Syrian government and opposition groups agreed to a ceasefire in Aleppo, marking the first significant breakthrough in the conflict since August 2013.
2. **Ebola Outbreak**: The World Health Organization (WHO) declared an outbreak of Ebola virus disease in Guinea, West Africa, which would eventually spread to several countries and claim thousands of lives.

## Sports

3. **NFL Playoffs**: The Seattle Seahawks defeated the New Orleans Saints 23-15 in a NFC Divisional Playoff game.
4. **NBA**: The Miami Heat won their 10th consecutive game with a 101-95 victory over the Chicago Bulls.

## Cultural and Entertainment

5. **Movie Releases**: The sci-fi film "Lucy" starring Scarlett Johansson, and the comedy-drama "Gone Girl" based on Gillian Flynn's bestseller, were both released in theaters.
6. **Music**: Singer-songwriter Taylor Swift was named the world's highest-paid celebrity by Forbes magazine.

## Historical

7. **Tiananmen Square Massacre**: The Chinese government commemorated the 25th anniversary of the Tiananmen Square protests and massacre, which resulted in thousands of deaths and injuries.
8. **Cold War**: The Soviet Union tested its first nuclear bomb on this day in 1946.

## Miscellaneous

9. **Weather**: A powerful winter storm hit the northeastern United States, causing widespread power outages and disrupting transportation.
10. **Technology**: Apple announced that it had sold over 1 million copies of its new operating system, OS X Mavericks, in the first 17 days after release.
