The Shadow in the Lighthouse

Detective Jameson "Jim" Thompson stepped out of the misty fog, his eyes scanning the rugged coastline as he approached the Ravenswood Lighthouse. The small coastal town was nestled between towering cliffs and the open sea, a place where time seemed to stand still.

He had been called to investigate the murder of Edward Blackstone, a wealthy businessman found dead at the lighthouse. As he entered the tower, Jim noticed the reclusive lighthouse keeper, Emily Wilson, standing watchful guard beside the stairs. Her eyes seemed to bore into his soul, as if daring him to suspect her.

"What can you tell me about the victim?" Jim asked, his tone firm but polite.
