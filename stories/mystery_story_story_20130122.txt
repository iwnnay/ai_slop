**The Shadow in the Lighthouse**

Detective Jameson "Jim" Thompson stepped out of his car and onto the mist-shrouded coastal town of Ravenswood, Maine. The autumn air was crisp, and the smell of saltwater filled his nostrils. He had been investigating a series of strange occurrences on this rugged coastline for weeks, and nothing seemed to make sense.

The local lighthouse, once a beacon of safety, now stood as an eerie presence, its light flickering erratically like a morse code warning. Fishermen had reported seeing strange lights and hearing whispers in the darkness, but Jim couldn't find any concrete evidence. That was until Emily Grant arrived in town, claiming to have inherited the old lighthouse from her recently deceased uncle.

Jim's instincts told him that something wasn't right about Emily. She seemed evasive and nervous during their initial meetings. He decided to pay her a visit at the lighthouse, hoping to get a better sense of what was going on.

As he approached the tower, Jim noticed Emily standing near the edge, her eyes fixed on the sea. She turned to him, her beauty momentarily distracting him from his mission. But as they spoke, Jim sensed that she was hiding something.

"What do you know about your uncle's past?" Jim asked, trying to sound nonchalant.

Emily hesitated before answering. "Not much, detective. He just left me the lighthouse and some old papers."
