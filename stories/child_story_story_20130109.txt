**The Little Cloud Who Wanted to be Big**

In a sunny meadow filled with vibrant wildflowers, surrounded by rolling hills and tall trees, there lived a little cloud named Luna. She was small and fluffy, with a soft white body and a wispy tail that trailed behind her like a streamer.

Luna loved watching the big clouds drift lazily across the sky, casting shadows over the meadow. She longed to be as big and powerful as they were. "Why can't I be like them?" she would sigh to herself. "I want to cover the entire sky!"

One day, Luna decided to take matters into her own hands. She floated down to the meadow and started drinking dew from the flowers. She guzzled and gulped, hoping to grow bigger and stronger.

At first, it seemed to work! Luna felt a slight expansion in her chest, and her tail grew longer and fluffier. But as she continued to drink, she began to feel... heavy. Very heavy.

"Oh no!" Luna cried out as she started to fall to the ground. "I'm too big!"

Just then, a friendly bee named Buzz flew into view, collecting nectar from the same flowers that had been quenching Luna's thirst. "What's wrong, little cloud?" he asked, noticing Luna's distress.

Luna explained how she had tried to grow bigger and ended up falling down. Buzz chuckled. "I think I can help you see things differently," he said with a smile.

"Why not just be happy being small?" Buzz suggested. "You can still bring shade to the meadow and make the sun feel warm on the earth."

Luna thought about this for a moment. She had never really considered that her small size could have its own advantages. In fact, she realized that she loved making the flowers in the meadow feel cozy and protected under her gentle cloud cover.

As Buzz finished collecting his nectar, he said, "You know what? I think your small size is quite special. You can get close to the flowers and see all the tiny details that we bigger creatures might miss."

Luna's heart swelled with pride. She felt happy being just the right size for her meadow friends.

From that day on, Luna spread joy and shade over the meadow, knowing that being small was exactly what made her special. And Buzz became her best friend, always there to offer words of encouragement whenever she needed them.

**The End**
