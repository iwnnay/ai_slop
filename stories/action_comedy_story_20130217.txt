As the chaos subsided, Jack turned to Sophia and grinned. "You know what? I think we make a pretty good team."

Their unlikely partnership cemented, Jack and Sophia set out to protect Neo-Tokyo from further threats – as the city's most unorthodox, yet effective, heroes.
