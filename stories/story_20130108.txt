## Pip and the Tiny Acorn Bridge

Pip the squirrel’s tail wobbled like a happy jellybean. *Wobble! Wobble!* It wobbled when Pip ran. *Wobble! Wobble!* It wobbled when Pip ate. *Wobble! Wobble!* But when it was time to jump the stream? *Wobble! Wobble!* "Too big!" said Pip. "My acorn tree is on the other side… and I can’t jump!"

Pip loved the Acorn Tree. It stood in Oak Tree Park, tall and green, with the *most* delicious, shiny acorns. But the stream was wide and wiggly, like a silver ribbon.
